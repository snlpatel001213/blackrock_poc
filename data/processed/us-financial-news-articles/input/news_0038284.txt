Flu virus causes rising number of hospitalizations Friday, January 19, 2018 - 01:22 
The Centers for Disease Control says the influenza A strain known as H3N2 has been linked with increased hospitalizations and death, but this year’s virus is still not as severe as the 2014/2015 season.
