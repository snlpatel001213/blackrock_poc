Lithuania booze ad ban creates business headache 4:32pm GMT - 01:51 
Print distributors in Lithuania are being forced to tear pages out from magazines or cover liquor adverts with stickers after the country introduced an alcohol advertising ban on January 1. As David Pollard reports, it's proving quite a headache for local distributors.
