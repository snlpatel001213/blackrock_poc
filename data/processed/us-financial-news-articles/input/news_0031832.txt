January 19, 2018 / 3:06 PM / Updated 4 minutes ago North Korea cancels visit to prepare for Olympic performance in South Korea - Yonhap Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
(Reuters) - North Korea has cancelled a visit by a delegation to South Korea to prepare for a trip by an art troupe during next month’s Winter Olympics, Seoul’s Unification Ministry was quoted as saying by South Korea’s Yonhap news agency on Friday. 
The North did not give a reason for the cancellation, Yonhap quoted the ministry as saying. ( bit.ly/2rkPE4N ) Reporting by Subrat Patnaik in Bengaluru; Editing by Alison Williams
