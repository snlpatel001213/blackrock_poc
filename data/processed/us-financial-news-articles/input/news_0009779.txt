These pages often address the problem of excessive occupational licensing. Working together, we hope to take one major step toward reform.
Each state decides how best to protect the health and safety of its citizens, and professional licensure plays an important role. No one wants to be operated on by an unlicensed doctor or share the road with an unlicensed truck driver.
