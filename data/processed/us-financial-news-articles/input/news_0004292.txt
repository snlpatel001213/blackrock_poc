NEW YORK, Jan. 2, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Wolf Popper LLP is investigating claims on behalf of investors in Sucampo Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (NASDAQ: SCMP), concerning the proposed acquisition of Sucampo by Mallinckrodt plc (NYSE: MNK). Under the terms of the proposed acquisition, Sucampo's shareholders will receive $18.00 per share in cash through a tender offer, for a total transaction value of $1.2 billion. According to one analyst, Sucampo's Phase 2 and 3 assets are worth $29 per share on their own.
Wolf Popper is investigating claims on behalf of investors in Sucampo concerning the transaction. Sucampo's shareholders seeking more information about the transaction or Wolf Popper's investigation can contact Mr. Stine at (212) 759-4600 or cstine@wolfpopper.com .
