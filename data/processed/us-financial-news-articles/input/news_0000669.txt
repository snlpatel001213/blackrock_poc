President Donald Trump and South Korean President Moon Jae-in agreed not to hold springtime military exercises during the Olympics, the South Korean president’s office said, a move that could cool tensions with North Korea.
The agreement came during a 30-minute phone call between the two presidents, Seoul’s presidential Blue House said on Thursday.
