Thomson Reuters confirms deal talks with Blackstone 1:34pm EST - 01:44 
Data provider Thomson Reuters confirmed it is in advanced talks for U.S. private equity firm Blackstone Group LP to buy a major stake in the data and analytics part of the company, which would put Blackstone in competition with other Wall Street data providers like Bloomberg and Dow Jones.
