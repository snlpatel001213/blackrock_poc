NEW BRUNSWICK, N.J., Jan. 2, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Johnson & Johnson (NYSE: JNJ) today announced that its Board of Directors has declared a cash dividend for the first quarter of 2018 of $0.84 per share on the company's common stock. The dividend is payable on March 13, 2018 to shareholders of record at the close of business on February 27, 2018. The ex-dividend date is February 26, 2018.
