January 15, 2018 / 8:35 PM / Updated 29 minutes ago Earthquake rattles Greece, felt in Athens Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
ATHENS (Reuters) - An earthquake that rattled Greece on Monday night was felt in the capital Athens, a Reuters reporter said. 
The U.S. Geological Survey said the 4.4 magnitude quake struck 51 km (30 miles) south-southeast of Patras. Reporting by Renee Maltezou; Editing by Alison Williams
