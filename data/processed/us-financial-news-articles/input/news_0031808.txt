Diversity isn’t just a corporate feel-good measure. It has bottom-line benefits, a new study suggests.

In one of the largest research endeavors of its kind, McKinsey & Co. examined the financial data of more than 1,000 major companies across 12 countries, as well as the gender and ethnic makeup of their workforces.
