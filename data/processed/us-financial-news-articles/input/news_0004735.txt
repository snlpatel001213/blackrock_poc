January 4, 2018 / 2:59 PM / Updated 21 minutes ago CANADA STOCKS-TSX adds to record high as financials, industrials climb Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
TORONTO, Jan 4 (Reuters) - Canada’s main stock index rose on Thursday, adding to its record high, as the financial and industrial groups climbed, while shares of energy and marijuana producers pared some recent gains. 
The Toronto Stock Exchange’s S&P/TSX composite index rose 7.86 points, or 0.05 percent, to 16,379.41 shortly after the open. Six of the index’s 10 main groups were higher. Reporting by Fergal Smith Editing by Chizu Nomiyama
