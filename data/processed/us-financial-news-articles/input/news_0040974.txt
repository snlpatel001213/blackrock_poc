WASHINGTON—Senate Democrats sought to narrow the parameters of an immigration deal with the White House Thursday, saying an agreement should focus on border security and the fate of Dreamers, undocumented immigrants brought to the U.S. as children.
Speaking on the Senate floor, Democratic leader Chuck Schumer of New York urged both parties to limit their scope to those two issues as a way to reach a deal before the government’s funding expires at midnight on Feb. 8.
