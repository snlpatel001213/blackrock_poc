Trump cancels trip to Britain, 'not a big fan' of embassy Friday, January 12, 2018 - 02:05 
U.S. President Donald Trump has cancelled what would have been his first official visit to the United Kingdom, stating he's disappointed in a 2008 decision to move the American embassy. Matthew Larotonda reports.
