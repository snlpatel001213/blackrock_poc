AT&T to launch 5G in U.S. by late 2018 Published 12:02 AM ET Thu, 4 Jan 2018 Reuters SHARES 
Jan 4 (Reuters) - AT&T Inc, the No. 2 U.S. wireless carrier, said it would launch fifth-generation (5G) mobile network service in a dozen cities in the United States by late 2018, after international wireless standards for the network were finalized last month. 
The 5G technology is expected to provide higher speed and response times than 4G networks used today. (Reporting by Rama Venkat Raman in Bengaluru; Editing by Amrutha Gayathri)
