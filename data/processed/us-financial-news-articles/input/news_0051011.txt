CLEVELAND, Jan. 30, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- NeverBounce , the industry leading email verification and list cleaning service, announced today the expansion of its sales operations with the addition of Russell Fletcher. Fletcher will join NeverBounce as Vice President of Sales.
"As we continue to expand our enterprise offerings and global reach, adding seasoned sales professionals is essential," said Brad Owen, CEO and Founder of NeverBounce. "Russell is uniquely qualified to help lead this initiative with intimate knowledge and past experience in the sector of email deliverability, email marketing, and e-commerce. We are thrilled to add a sales leader with such an impressive track record of success."
Prior to joining NeverBounce, Fletcher served as Vice President of Sales at Port25 Solutions, developer and licenser of leading commercial message transfer agent software PowerMTA. Fletcher also lead sales efforts and teams for Cheetah Digital, WhatCounts, and most recently, SendForensics.
"Clean email data is a critical aspect of optimizing deliverability," said Fletcher, "and NeverBounce is the clear leader in providing real-time verification and list hygiene services. I'm extremely honored and excited to join such a dynamic team."
Beyond spearheading NeverBounce's sales operations, Fletcher will actively be looking to expand the sales team to meet the growing demand for NeverBounce's email address verification services. Based in Tampa, FL., Fletcher has begun scouting locations for a potential new regional sales office. NeverBounce headquarters will remain in Cleveland, Ohio.
