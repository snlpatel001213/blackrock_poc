BELLEVUE, Wash., Apptio, Inc . (NASDAQ: APTI), the business management system of record for hybrid IT, today announced that it will release financial results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year ended December 31, 2017 following the close of the U.S. markets on Monday, February 5, 2018. Apptio will host a conference call and live webcast to discuss the results.
Conference Call Details
The conference call will begin at 2 p.m. Pacific Time (5 p.m. Eastern Time) on February 5, 2018. Interested parties may listen to the call by dialing 844-233-0116 (passcode: 4795737), or if outside North America, by dialing 574-990-1011 (passcode: 4795737). Individuals may access the live teleconference from the investor relations section of the Apptio website at investors.apptio.com .
A replay of the call via webcast will be available at investors.apptio.com or by dialing 855-859-2056 (passcode: 4795737), or if outside North America, by dialing 404-537-3406 (passcode: 4795737). The dial-in replay will be available until the end of day February 12, 2018. The webcast replay will be available for 12 months.
About Apptio
Apptio (NASDAQ: APTI) is the business management system of record for hybrid IT. We transform the way IT runs its business and makes decisions. With our cloud-based applications, IT leaders manage, plan and optimize their technology investments across on-premises and cloud. With Apptio, IT leaders become strategic partners to the business by demonstrating value of IT investments, accelerate innovation and shift their technology investments from running the business to digital innovation. Hundreds of customers choose Apptio as their business system of record for hybrid IT. For more information, please visit www.Apptio.com .
