Many classic movies can be summed up by a single scene, or even a single shot: Norman Bates behind a shower curtain with a knife; Charles Foster Kane dropping a snow globe down the stairs. In the delicious “Have a Nice Day,” the most significant image might be the bag of 100-yuan banknotes, each bearing the face of Chinese communism’s sainted Mao Zedong, and which animator/director Liu Jian returns to time and again. That they are stolen banknotes is part of the joke.
