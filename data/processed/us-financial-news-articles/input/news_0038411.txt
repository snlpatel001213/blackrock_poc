Chinese scientists break key barrier by cloning monkeys 5:10pm GMT - 00:44 
Chinese scientists have for the first time cloned monkeys using the same technique that produced Dolly the sheep two decades ago, breaking a technical barrier that could open the door to copying humans. Jim Drury reports.
