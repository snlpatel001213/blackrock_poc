Russia sends warships close to Britain, again Monday, January 08, 2018 - 01:10 
A British frigate escorted Russian ships through the English Channel on Monday, Britain's defense ministry said, adding that it was the latest sign of an upsurge in Russian naval activity near UK waters during the holidays.
