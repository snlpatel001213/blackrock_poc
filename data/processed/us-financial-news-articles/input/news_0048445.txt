Polish team rescues French climber from Pakistan's 'Killer Mountain'   00:46 
Officials say Polish climbers rescued a French mountaineer from the treacherous slopes of Pakistan's ''Killer Mountain'' on Sunday but were unable to save her climbing companion. Nathan Frandino reports.
