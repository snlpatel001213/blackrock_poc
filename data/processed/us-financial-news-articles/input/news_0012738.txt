Mobile and Web application development company DevBBQ joins Spiria.
MONTREAL, Jan. 9, 2018 /PRNewswire/ - Spiria , one of North America's fastest-growing custom software development firms, is proud to announce the addition of DevBBQ to its Canadian team. The acquisition of this Toronto-based mobile and Web application developer will accelerate Spiria's ability to deliver innovative digital solutions to businesses across North America.

DevBBQ is the developer behind innovative digital solutions for companies like Paymi, Salesforce, Party City, Collinson Group and Deloitte.
