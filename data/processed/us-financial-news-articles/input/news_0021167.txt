SEOUL—Government attempts to tighten control over cryptocurrency trading are sparking a fierce public backlash in South Korea.
A petition on the official website of South Korea’s presidential office was set to reach 200,000 signatures Monday evening, hitting a threshold that would compel a government response, which could be the opening of an investigation. The petition, which was launched on Dec. 28, has until Jan. 27 to reach the target signatories.
