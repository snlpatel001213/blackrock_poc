Venezuela's "petro" cryptocurrency outlawed by congress 5:04pm GMT - 01:55 
Venezuela's opposition-run parliament has outlawed a ''petro'' cryptocurrency promoted by socialist President Nicolas Maduro, calling it an effort to illegally mortgage the cash-strapped country's oil reserves. As Ivor Bennett reports, the news comes as unrest grows in a once-booming industrial city plagued with food shortages and a malaria outbreak.
