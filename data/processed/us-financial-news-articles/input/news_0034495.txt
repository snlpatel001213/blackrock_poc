MOSCOW (Reuters) - Oil supplies via the Russian Samara-Tikhorets pipeline, damaged by fire on Thursday, were restored on Sunday after completion of repair work, oil pipeline monopoly Transneft ( TRNF_p.MM ) said.
The pipeline, which ships Siberia Light oil to refineries and Russian Black Sea export terminals, caught fire in the Saratov region on Thursday.
