January 9, 2018 / 9:10 AM / Updated 10 minutes ago Turkey to continue Euphrates Shield operation in northern Syria, Erdogan says Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
ISTANBUL (Reuters) - Turkey’s military will continue its operation in Syria’s Afrin and Manbij regions, President Recep Tayyip Erdogan said on Tuesday during a parliamentary address to his ruling AK Party. FILE PHOTO: A general view shows the Kurdish city of Afrin, in Aleppo's countryside, Syria March 18, 2015. REUTERS/Mahmoud Hebbo/File Photo 
In 2016, Turkey launched the Euphrates Shield operation on its Syrian border to eradicate what it called a “corridor of terror”, made up by the dual threat of Islamic State and Syrian Kurdish fighters. Writing by Ali Kucukgocmen; Editing by David Dolan
