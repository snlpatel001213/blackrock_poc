January 11, 2018 / 1:15 PM / in 27 minutes Dutch ABP pension fund to pull investments in tobacco, nuclear arms makers Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
AMSTERDAM, Jan 11 (Reuters) - ABP, the largest Dutch pension fund, said on Thursday it will sell all its remaining holdings in tobacco companies and nuclear arms manufacturers, worth roughly 3.3 billion euros ($4 bln). ($1 = 0.8319 euros) (Reporting by Anthony Deutsch; Editing by Susan Fenton)
