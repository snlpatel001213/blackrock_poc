North Korea to send athletes, cheer squad to Winter Olympics Tuesday, January 09, 2018 - 01:51 
North Korea says it will send a delegation of high-ranking officials, athletes and a cheering squad to the Pyeongchang Winter Olympics in South Korea next month, during rare talks with the South.
