January 16, 2018 / 9:04 AM / in 8 minutes UAE to lodge aviation complaint against Qatar at UN agency Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
DUBAI, Jan 16 (Reuters) - The United Arab Emirates will lodge a complaint against Qatar at the United Nations aviation agency on Tuesday after Qatari fighter jets twice intercepted UAE civilian aircraft during routine flights to Bahrain, a UAE official said. 
The UAE will accuse Qatar of violating the Chicago Convention in its complaint to the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO), UAE General Civil Aviation Authority Director-General Saif Mohammed al-Suwaidi told Reuters. (Reporting by Alexander Cornwell; Editing by Andrew Torchia)
