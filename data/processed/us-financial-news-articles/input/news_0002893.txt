About Rudolph Technologies
Rudolph Technologies, Inc. is a leader in the design, development, manufacture and support of process control systems for defect inspection and thin film metrology, lithography tools, and process control software used by semiconductor and advanced packaging device manufacturers worldwide. Rudolph delivers comprehensive solutions throughout the fabrication and packaging processes with its families of proprietary products that provide critical yield-enhancing information, enabling microelectronic device manufacturers to drive down costs and time to market for their devices. Headquartered in Wilmington, Massachusetts, Rudolph supports its customers with a worldwide sales and service organization.
