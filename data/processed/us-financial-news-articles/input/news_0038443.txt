Sports fans know that you need to move fast to get the best seats. Even then, they are rarely ever cheap.
That’s the gamble investors are making with Activision Blizzard. The videogame publisher spent the last year hustling to build up its own esports league based on its popular “Overwatch” game. The Overwatch League kicked off earlier this month. At the same time, investors bid up Activision’s stock by 75% in 2017 and another 13% so far this year.
