 39 AM / Updated 27 minutes ago Russia starts shipping S-400 air defence missile system to China - TASS cites source Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russia has started delivering its S-400 surface-to-air missile system to China under the terms of a contract signed in 2014, the TASS news agency cited an unnamed source in the Russian military-industrial complex as saying on Thursday.
