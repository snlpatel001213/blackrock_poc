NEW YORK, Jan 30 (Reuters) - U.S. stocks fell for the second straight day on Tuesday as healthcare stocks and rising bond yields weighed on all three major U.S. indexes.
The Dow Jones Industrial Average fell 361.83 points, or 1.37 percent, to 26,077.65, the S&P 500 lost 31.06 points, or 1.09 percent, to 2,822.47 and the Nasdaq Composite dropped 64.02 points, or 0.86 percent, to 7,402.48. (Reporting by Stephen Culp; Editing by Nick Zieminski)
