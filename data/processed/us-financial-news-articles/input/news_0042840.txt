HANOI (Reuters) - Vietnam’s tech firm FPT Corp says its unit FPT Software signed a $100 million contract with Innogy SE to provide technological solutions for Innogy, FPT said on Thursday.
The deal is FPT Software’s biggest contract ever and will allow the Vietnamese firm to provide the solutions to Germany-based utility firm Innogy during 2018-2024, FPT said in a statement.
