UK: jobs data points way up for surging pound 12:29pm EST - 01:46 
The number of people in work in Britain surged unexpectedly in the three months to November and regular wages rose at their fastest rate in almost a year. As David Pollard reports, it's helped sterling rise even further amid renewed confidence over Brexit.
