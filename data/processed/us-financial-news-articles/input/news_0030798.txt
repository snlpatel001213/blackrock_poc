PITTSBURGH--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- The board of directors of PPG (NYSE:PPG) today declared a regular quarterly dividend of 45 cents per share, payable March 12 to shareholders of record Feb. 16.
This marks the company’s 478 th consecutive dividend payment. PPG has paid uninterrupted annual dividends since 1899.
