Jan 15 (Reuters) - Tianjin Pengling Rubber Hose Co Ltd
* Says it sees net profit for FY 2017 to decrease by 18.6 percent to 23.7 percent, or to be 120 million yuan to 128 million yuan
* Says the net profit of FY 2016 was 157.3 million yuan
* Comments that decreased sales price as well as increased raw material price and costs as main reasons for the forecast
