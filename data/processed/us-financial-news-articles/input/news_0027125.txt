Schools closed and road conditions turned hazardous in the Southern U.S. on Tuesday as an arctic cold front moving from Texas to Maine promised severe winter weather conditions.
The storm brought bitter cold and a mix of snow, sleet and freezing rain to parts of Texas and Louisiana on Tuesday, according to the National Weather Service. Forecasters said the winter blast would spread east on Wednesday, blanketing central Appalachia, the mid-Atlantic and the Northeast with snow.
