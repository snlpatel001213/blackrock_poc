Domino’s Pizza Inc. Chief Executive J. Patrick Doyle will step down at the end of June, after boosting the chain’s sales in recent years while rivals and other restaurant chains struggled.

Mr. Doyle, 54 years old, led Domino’s for eight years, during a tumultuous period for many U.S. food companies. Domino’s has fared better than most, posting rising quarterly same-store sales for more than six years.
