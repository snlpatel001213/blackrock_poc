To listen to the call, please dial 800-593-9959 (international participants dial 517-308-9340) and reference the passcode “Sleep.” To access the webcast, please visit the investor relations area of the Sleep Number website.
The webcast replay will remain available in the investor relations area of the company’s website for approximately 60 days.
