Jan 17 (Reuters) - Melrose Industries Plc:
* MELROSE INDUSTRIES - FIRM OFFER FOR GKN PLC * ANNOUNCES TERMS OF ITS FIRM OFFER TO ACQUIRE ENTIRE ISSUED AND TO BE ISSUED SHARE CAPITAL OF GKN PLC
* GKN SHAREHOLDERS WILL BE ENTITLED TO RECEIVE: 1.49 NEW MELROSE SHARES FOR EACH GKN SHARE AND 81 PENCE IN CASH
* ‍ACQUISITION WILL INCLUDE A MIX AND MATCH FACILITY​ * ‍CASH CONSIDERATION PAYABLE UNDER TERMS OF ACQUISITION WILL BE FUNDED THROUGH A NEW DEBT FACILITY​
* BASED ON MELROSE‘S CLOSING PRICE OF 234.3 PENCE PER MELROSE SHARE ON JAN 16, DEAL VALUES EACH GKN SHARE AT 430.1 PENCE
* MELROSE INDUSTRIES - ‍GROUP NET LEVERAGE IS EXPECTED TO BE IN LINE WITH ITS DECLARED STRATEGY OF APPROXIMATELY 2.5X ENLARGED GROUP EBITDA AS A RESULT OF DEAL​
* DEAL VALUES ENTIRE ISSUED AND TO BE ISSUED ORDINARY SHARE CAPITAL OF GKN AT APPROXIMATELY £7.4 BILLION
