There could be serious problems in financial markets if long-term interest rates rise in 2018. There could be serious problems if they don’t.
Since it became clear that the Republican tax plan was going to become law, Treasury investors have been a bit more nervous— but just a little. The yield on the 10-year Treasury rose to its highest level since March on concerns that tax cuts could heat the economy up, but fell back to end the year at 2.41%, about where it started.
