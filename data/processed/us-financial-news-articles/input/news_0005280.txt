Sadanand Dhume is incorrect when he suggests the government of Bangladesh is to blame for recent terrorist attacks committed by individuals linked to the country (“East Is East: Bangladesh Exports a New Generation of Jihadists,” op-ed, Dec. 29). The title is also misleading. Bangladesh is a democratic and secular nation. Its citizens enjoy the right to free speech and practice different religions guaranteed by its constitution.
