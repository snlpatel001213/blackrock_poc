Candidate pledges to stay in IL attorney general race after being robbed 3:06pm EST - 01:13 
Aaron Goldstein, who is a candidate running for Illinois attorney general, was robbed Thursday afternoon in Chicago's northwest while he was on a campaign photo shoot. After the incident, he pledged to stay in the race and put the robbery behind him. Colette Luke has more.
