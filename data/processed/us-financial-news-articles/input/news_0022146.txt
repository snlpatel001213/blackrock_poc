VENICE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Snap Inc. (NYSE: SNAP) will hold its quarterly conference call to discuss fourth quarter and full year 2017 financial results on Tuesday, February 6, 2018 at 2:00 p.m. Pacific Time (5:00 p.m. Eastern Time).
A live webcast will be accessible at investor.snap.com . A recording of the webcast will also be available following the conference call.
About Snap Inc.
Snap Inc. is a camera company. We believe that reinventing the camera represents our greatest opportunity to improve the way people live and communicate. Our products empower people to express themselves, live in the moment, learn about the world, and have fun together.
