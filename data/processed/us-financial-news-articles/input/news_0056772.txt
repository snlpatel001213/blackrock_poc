TARPON SPRINGS, Fla., Jan. 31, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Brian Buckley, Operations Manager of Thunder Energies Corporation, a publicly traded company with stock symbol (OTC:TNRG), today announced the appointment of Robert Devries as the Vice-President of Production. In his new role, Robert will oversee manufacturing and production of Directional Neutron Sources for Thunder Energies currently available in the U.S.A. and Europe for distribution the world over.
Dr. R. M. Santilli, CEO of Thunder Energies, states: "It is an immense pleasure to welcome Eng. Devries to the Thunder Energies team. Prior to his appointment as Vice President, Robert has performed a vital role in the finalization of the design and manufacturing of various components of our Directional Neutron Source. Prior to joining our team, Eng. Devries worked many years in design and manufacturing in the U.S.A. as well as in Italy and China.‚Äù
