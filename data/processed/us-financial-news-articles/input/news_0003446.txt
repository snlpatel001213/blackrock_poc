 02 AM / Updated 20 minutes ago Four dead as South African train crashes, bursts into flames Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
JOHANNESBURG (Reuters) - Four people died and dozens were injured after a passenger train derailed and crashed into a truck before bursting into flames in South Africa’s Free State province, emergency services said on Thursday. 
“Four people have been declared dead... with approximately 40 injured,” said Netcare’s spokesman Tebogo Magoro, a spokesman for emergency services group Netcare911. 
Paramedic service ER24 said as many as 100 people had been injured. Reporting by Mfuneko Toyana; editing by John Stonestreet
