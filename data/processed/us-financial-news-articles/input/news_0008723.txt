Jan 9 (Reuters) - Aisin Seiki Co Ltd
* Says it repurchased 1.7 million shares of its common stock for 10.42 billion yen in total from Dec. 1, 2017 to Dec. 31, 2017
* Says this was part of the share repurchase plan announced on May 24, 2017
* Says it accumulatively repurchased 8.4 million shares for 49.05 billion yen in total as of Dec. 31, 2017
