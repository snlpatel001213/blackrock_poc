Irish PM at Davos: 'don't throw snowballs' 12:01pm GMT - 03:24 
Speaking ahead of appearances by Donald Trump and Theresa May at the World Economic Forum in Davos, Irish Prime Minister Leo Varadkar dismissed concerns of a currency war with the U.S. and reiterated the EU's stance that the UK can not 'cherry pick' a trade deal.
