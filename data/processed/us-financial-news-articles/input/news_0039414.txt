The climb to Hawi can make or break cyclists on the Ironman World Championship course in Kona, Hawaii. Caroline Gaynor has the added challenge of riding uphill into the climb’s famous headwinds on a tandem bike. Ms. Gaynor, 34, is an eight-time Ironman guide for blind and visually impaired athletes.
