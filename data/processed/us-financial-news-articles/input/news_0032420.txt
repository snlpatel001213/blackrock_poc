CHICAGO, Jan. 18, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- LotLinx, the industry's #1 Automotive AI, today announced that Denise Chudy has stepped down from her positions at LotLinx to pursue other opportunities.
Ms. Chudy said, "I appreciate the opportunity to have helped LotLinx grow its disruptive technology to become a market-leading digital advertising platform. While I will miss the daily excitement of being inside the company, I will continue to watch its growth as a significant shareholder and I am proud to be a part of the LotLinx family."
Len Short, Chairman and CEO of LotLinx, said "We are thankful to Denise for her time at LotLinx. She helped us grow into the industry's leading digital platform for increasing inventory turn. We wish her the best in her future endeavors."
About LotLinx
LotLinx is an award-winning Automotive AI company dedicated to empowering automotive dealers and the agencies that work with them, up to the OEM level. The LotLinx TURN platform can transform dealer results, dramatically increasing online engagement and net-new shoppers while eliminating digital advertising waste. Founded in 2012 and based in Chicago, LotLinx works with thousands of dealers including rooftops under the nation's Top 100 auto groups, and recently expanded into the Canadian market. For more visit www.lotlinx.com.
