Citigroup Inc. on Tuesday posted one of its largest quarterly losses ever, dragged down by a $22 billion write-down related to the recently passed tax overhaul. This helped its shares to rise by around 1% in morning trading.
No, this isn’t “opposite day.” As for most banks, the new tax law will be strongly beneficial to Citigroup in the long run. Meanwhile the one-time write-down would do only negligible damage to its balance-sheet strength and may even have some salutary effects on the bank’s relations with its investors.
