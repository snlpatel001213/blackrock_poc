January 26, 2018 / 11:44 AM / Updated 12 minutes ago Judge to decide on February 6 on Assange's bid to drop UK arrest warrant Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
LONDON (Reuters) - A British judge will rule on Feb. 6 whether she will drop an arrest warrant against Julian Assange that stems from his breach of UK bail conditions, as the WikiLeaks founder steps up efforts to leave the Ecuadorean embassy in London.
