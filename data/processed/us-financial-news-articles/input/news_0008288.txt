January 8, 2018 / 8:37 PM / Updated an hour ago Trump close to decision on Fed vice chair pick: administration official   
NASHVILLE (Reuters) - U.S. President Donald Trump is close to making a decision on who to nominate as vice chair of the Federal Reserve, an administration official said on Monday. 
The position became vacant in October after veteran central banker Stanley Fischer stepped down for personal reasons. Reporting by Jeff Mason, writing by Roberta Rampton; editing by Diane Craft
