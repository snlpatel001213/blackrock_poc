One baby dead, 15 injured after car plows through Copacabana sidewalk Friday, January 19, 2018 - 00:39 
Police say a speeding car plowed through evening strollers on a crowded sidewalk of Rio’s famed Copacabana beach on Thursday in an apparent accident, killing a baby and injuring 15 people.
