Americold Realty Trust expects to raise at least $330.8 million in an initial public offering, it said in a securities filing Tuesday.
The Atlanta-based firm is selling 24 million shares for between $14 and $16. As part of the offering, underwriters can buy 3.6 million more shares. If they use the extent of those options, Americold expects to raise around $381 million, it said.
