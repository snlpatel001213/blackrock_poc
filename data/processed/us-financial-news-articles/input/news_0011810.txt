BOISE, Idaho, Jan. 8, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Bare Metal Standard, Inc. (OTC BRRM) ("Bare" or the "Company") announced that its Board of Directors had approved a change in the Company's ticker symbol from "BRRM" to "BRMT". The change in ticker symbol will commence at the market open on January 8, 2018.
Financials Industry Regulatory Authority ( FINRA ) approved the symbol change. FINRA has announced on January 5, 2018 the symbol change will become effective at the beginning of trading on January 8, 2018.
All fillings and market related information will be reported under the new symbol effective January 8, 2018, as well. The CUSIP number for the common stock will remain unchanged.
