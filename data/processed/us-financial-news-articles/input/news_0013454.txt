RESTON, Virginia, January
MAXIMUS (NYSE: MMS), a leading provider of government services worldwide, announced today that its Board of Directors has approved a quarterly cash dividend of $0.045 per share, payable on February 28, 2018 to shareholders of record on February 15, 2018.
