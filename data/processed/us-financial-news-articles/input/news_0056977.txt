WATERBURY, Conn., Jan. 30, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Webster Financial Corporation (NYSE: WBS), the holding company for Webster Bank, N.A. and its HSA Bank division, announced today that its Board of Directors declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.26 per share on its common stock.
The dividend on the common shares will be payable February 27, 2018 to shareholders of record on February 13, 2018.
On its Series F Preferred Stock, Webster declared a quarterly cash dividend of $339.0625 per share ($0.3390625 per each depositary share, 1,000 of which represent one share of Series F preferred stock) payable on March 15, 2018 to shareholders of record on March 1, 2018.
About Webster
Webster Financial Corporation is the holding company for Webster Bank, National Association. With $26.5 billion in assets, Webster provides business and consumer banking, mortgage, financial planning, trust, and investment services through 167 banking centers and 334 ATMs. Webster also provides mobile and Internet banking. Webster Bank owns the asset-based lending firm Webster Business Credit Corporation; the equipment finance firm Webster Capital Finance Corporation; and HSA Bank, a division of Webster Bank, which provides health savings account trustee and administrative services. Webster Bank is a member of the FDIC and an equal housing lender. For more information about Webster, including past press releases and the latest annual report, visit the Webster website at www.websterbank.com .
