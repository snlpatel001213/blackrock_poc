Britain's lawmakers approve bill to sever EU ties 3:09pm GMT - 01:16 
British lawmakers have voted in favour of the government's legislative blueprint for Brexit, marking a victory for Prime Minister Theresa May over political opponents who want a softer approach to leaving the European Union.
