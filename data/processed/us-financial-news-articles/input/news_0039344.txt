Nassar cries after victim asks for an apology Tuesday, January 23, 2018 - 01:46 
Eighteen-year-old Emily Morales said she wanted to forgive the former MSU doctor Larry Nassar and asked that he apologize to her. Nassar, who is being sentenced this week after pleading guilty to additional counts of criminal sexual conduct related to allegations he assaulted girls under the guise of medical treatment, cried in court.
