Wolters Kluwer Legal & Regulatory Completes Swedish Divestment
January 4, 2018 - Wolters Kluwer Legal & Regulatory announces that it has today completed the divestment of certain Swedish Assets to Karnov Group as originally announced on October 25, 2017.
