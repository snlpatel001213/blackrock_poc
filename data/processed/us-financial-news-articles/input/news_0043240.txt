ECB policy unchanged, questions over euro surge 5:03pm GMT - 01:49 
European Central Bank chief Mario Draghi warned on Thursday that the surge in the euro was a source of uncertainty and said the bank might have to review strategy if U.S. comments on a weak dollar lead to a change in monetary conditions. David Pollard reports.
