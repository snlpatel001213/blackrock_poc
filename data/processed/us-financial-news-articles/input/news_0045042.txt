PHILADELPHIA—A man accused of ambushing a Philadelphia police officer with a hail of gunfire in the name of Islamic State refused to cooperate at the start of his attempted murder trial Thursday, telling a judge that he won’t “plead to anyone but Allah.”
With his back to a crowd of officers that included the city’s police commissioner, Edward Archer, 32 years old, offered the same response again and again as each charge stemming from the January 2016 shooting was read aloud.
