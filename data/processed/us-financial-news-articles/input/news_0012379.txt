DALLAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Capstead Mortgage Corporation (NYSE: CMO ) (“Capstead” or the “Company”) announced today that it will release its fourth quarter 2017 results following the close of trading on the New York Stock Exchange on Wednesday, January 31, 2018. The Company will host a conference call and live audio webcast to discuss its financial results on Thursday, February 1, 2018, at 9:00 a.m. ET.
To access the conference call, dial toll free (877) 505-6547 in the U.S., (855) 669-9657 for Canada, or (412) 902-6660 for international callers and ask to join the Capstead Mortgage Corporation call. The audio replay will be available one hour after the end of the conference call through May 1, 2018. The replay can be accessed by dialing toll free (877) 344-7529 in the U.S., (855) 669-9658 for Canada, or (412) 317-0088 for international callers and entering conference number 10115879.
A live audio webcast of the conference call can be accessed through a link in the investor relations section of the Company’s website at www.capstead.com . Please allow extra time prior to the call to download and install audio software, if needed. An audio archive of the webcast will be available until May 1, 2018 on the Company’s website.
About Capstead
Capstead Mortgage Corporation, formed in 1985 and based in Dallas, Texas, is a self-managed real estate investment trust for federal income tax purposes. Capstead invests in a leveraged portfolio of residential adjustable-rate mortgage (“ARM”) securities issued and guaranteed by Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, or Ginnie Mae.
