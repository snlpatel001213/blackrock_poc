SEOUL—The two Koreas agreed to meet for a second time on Monday to work out plans on how to accommodate North Korea’s participation in next month’s Winter Olympics, Seoul’s government said Saturday evening, in talks that are likely to determine how closely the two rivals will interact on the world stage.
