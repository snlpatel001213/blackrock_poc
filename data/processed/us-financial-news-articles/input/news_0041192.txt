MINNEAPOLIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- The Board of Directors of Ameriprise Financial, Inc. (NYSE: AMP) has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.83 per common share payable on February 28, 2018 to shareholders of record at the close of business on February 16, 2018.

With a nationwide network of 10,000 financial advisors and extensive asset management, advisory and insurance capabilities, we have the strength and expertise to serve the full range of individual and institutional investors’ financial needs.
