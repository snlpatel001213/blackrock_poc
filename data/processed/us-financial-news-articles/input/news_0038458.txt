If the government shut down Saturday morning, it forgot to inform Irina Cruz.
At a post office in New York City, as customers lined up to send and receive packages, Ms. Cruz, 27 years old, hadn’t heard of the shutdown.
“I didn’t know that happens,” said Ms. Cruz, who works in health care and was pushing her infant son in a stroller. “It’s unfortunate.”
