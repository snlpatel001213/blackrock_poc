WASHINGTON (Reuters) - Steve Bannon has stepped down as executive chairman of Breitbart News, the right-wing news organisation said on its website on Tuesday, after the former White House chief strategist was quoted in a book criticizing President Donald Trump. “Steve is a valued part of our legacy, and we will always be grateful for his contributions, and what he has helped us to accomplish,” Breitbart Chief Executive Larry Solov said in a statement on the website. Reporting by Eric Beech; Editing by Tim Ahmann
