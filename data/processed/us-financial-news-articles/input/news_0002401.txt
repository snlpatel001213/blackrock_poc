January 2, 2018 / 11:38 AM / Updated 15 minutes ago Man found dead in London's Canary Wharf financial district Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
(Reuters) - A man was found dead in a trench beneath a skyscraper in London’s Canary Wharf financial district on Tuesday morning, London Ambulance Service said. 
The ambulance service said it was called to Bank Street after reports of a person falling from one of the buildings. 
“Sadly, the patient was dead at the scene,” a spokesman said. Reporting by Guy Faulconbridge and Alistair Smout; editing by Stephen Addison
