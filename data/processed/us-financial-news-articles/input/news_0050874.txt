One way or another, Dell Inc. will likely go public again. But after four years as a private company, it risks looking hopelessly out of style.

The company now formally known as Dell Technologies appears to be looking at all options. The Wall Street Journal reported last week that those options include an initial public offering or the purchase of the remaining public shares of VMware, the software company in which Dell has a majority interest.
