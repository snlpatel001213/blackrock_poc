Jay Leno learns how this steam powered train is similar to a bicycle 4 Hours Ago The Mt. Washington Cog Railway transports visitors up the highest peak in the Northeastern United States. The historic locomotive uses steam power and coal to travel along a cog railway. Owner Wayne Presby explains how the track is just like the chain and sprocket on a bicycle to Jay Leno.
