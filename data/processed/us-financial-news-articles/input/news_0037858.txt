Procter & Gamble Co. is winning over shoppers with help from aggressive discounting that is rippling through the household-goods industry.
The maker of Tide detergent and Pampers diapers said Tuesday that average prices on its products fell in the most recent quarter for the first time since 2011. The discounting helped P&G boost sales in most of its business segments but has weighed on rivals.
