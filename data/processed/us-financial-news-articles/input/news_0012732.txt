MADISON, Wis., Jan. 08, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Sonic Foundry, Inc. (NASDAQ: SOFO), the trusted global leader for video creation and management solutions, today announced it will release results of its fourth quarter and fiscal 2017 on Friday, Jan. 12 after the close of the market.
The company will then host a corporate webcast for analysts and investors at 4:30 p.m. ET. Attendees will view the presentation via the webcasting platform, Mediasite , streaming the presentation for live and on-demand viewing.
To access the webcast, please register at www.sonicfoundry.com/earnings on or before Jan. 12, 2018. Viewers can participate in a Q&A session immediately following management’s prepared remarks through the Mediasite player.
A video archive of the full earnings call, including Q&A, will be available for 90 days.
About Sonic Foundry®, Inc.
Sonic Foundry (NASDAQ:SOFO) is the global leader for video capture, management and streaming solutions. Trusted by more than 4,300 educational institutions, corporations, health organizations and government entities in 65 countries, its Mediasite Video Platform quickly and cost-effectively automates the capture, management, delivery and search of live and on-demand streaming videos. Leading research firms Aragon, Forrester, Wainhouse and Frost & Sullivan recognize Sonic Foundry as a leader in enterprise video, webcasting and lecture capture. Learn more at www.sonicfoundry.com and @mediasite.
