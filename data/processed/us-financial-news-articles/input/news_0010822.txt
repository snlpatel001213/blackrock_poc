HENDERSON, Ky., Jan. 10, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Kyle Hittner has been promoted from Director of Customer Experience to Director of Brand Experience, Vice President. Hittner brings years of experience as an Executive Director at both the Henderson Area Arts Alliance and the Henderson County Tourist Commission.
In this new hybrid role, Kyle will oversee creative branding strategies and community engagement. Additionally, she will continue to oversee the Retail Department and the Field & Main Customer Experience, in partnership with Michelle Hope, Retail Operations; VP.
Hittner earned her Bachelor of Arts from Belmont University in 2004. She resides in Henderson, Kentucky with her husband, Jonathan and their dog Diggy.
