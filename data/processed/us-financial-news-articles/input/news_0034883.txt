Chinese police seize bookseller again, says daughter 10:29am GMT - 01:06 
Chinese agents have seized Swedish citizen and Hong Kong-based bookseller Gui Minhai from a Beijing-bound train, according to his daughter. He was abducted while on holiday in Thailand in 2015, one of five booksellers who went missing that year only to turn up later in Chinese custody.
