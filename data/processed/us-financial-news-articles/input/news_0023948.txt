Irish PM warns of hypocrisy in corporate tax debate 12:08pm EST - 01:49 
Irish Prime Minister Leo Varadkar accused fellow EU states of hypocrisy over corporate tax on Wednesday and appeared to point a finger at France in particular as he rejected claims in the European Parliament that Ireland was a tax haven. David Pollard reports.
