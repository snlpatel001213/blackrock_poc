SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Erinn Stringer joined G2 Insurance Services as a Client Manager. Erinn will serve G2’s commercial clients, including hospitality, commercial real estate and nonprofits.
“Erinn represents the future of G2,” said Mark Ruggles, CEO. “Her demonstration of leadership in developing relationships will add great value to our clients and our associates. I look forward to supporting her contribution to our team.”
Prior to joining G2 Insurance Services, Erinn was an Account Manager at Heffernan Insurance Brokers, handling all aspects of client stewardship. She started her career as an Administrative Assistant at Brown & Brown Insurance in Portland, Oregon. Erinn studied at the University of Oregon at Eugene and earned her bachelor degree at University of San Francisco.
“As I heard more about G2’s desire to foster long-term relationships with clients, I resonated with their vision,” said Erinn. “I also appreciate G2’s deep commitment to the community. There is something special about this company and I am thrilled to be able to contribute to its growth.”
Erinn is located in G2 Insurance Services’ San Francisco office. She can be reached at 415-426-6654 and estringer@G2insurance.com .
About G2 Insurance Services
Founded in 2012 and located in San Francisco, G2 Insurance Services is an independent, full-service property, casualty and employee benefits insurance brokerage firm focused on the risk management needs of for-profit companies, non-profit organizations and high-net-worth individuals. More information can be found at G2insurance.com .
