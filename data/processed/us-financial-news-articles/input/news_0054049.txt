NEWPORT, R.I., Jan. 31, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- SecZetta , the leading provider of third party identity risk and lifecycle management solutions, today announced it has joined SailPoint's Identity+ Alliance. The collaboration provides SecZetta customers using SailPoint's identity management solutions, a way to enable business users to directly manage non-employee identities. SecZetta products extend IGA programs to include an authoritative source for managing third party identity risk and identity lifecycle.
"SailPoint helps enterprises manage who has access to what, across all users, all applications and all data," said Joe Gottlieb, Senior Vice President of Corporate Development, SailPoint. "We are excited to welcome SecZetta into the Identity+ Alliance so that together, we can make sure that 'all users' includes the third parties managed by SecZetta."
"The collaboration between SecZetta and SailPoint creates a powerful business centric, identity risk and third party management toolset," said David Pignolet, President and CEO of SecZetta. "We are pleased to become a SailPoint Identity+ Alliance member and to enhance the range of identity management solutions available to our respective customers."
The SailPoint Identity+ Alliance provides standards and tools that ease the integration of identity management with security solutions and operational infrastructure, making the latter more "identity-aware" and significantly increasing the effectiveness of a customer's security and risk mitigation efforts.
