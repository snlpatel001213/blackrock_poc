As markets around the world surge to all-time highs, everyone is wondering anxiously whether stocks are overvalued. That’s a pretty good sign they’re not. Stocks are overvalued when hardly anybody is worrying.
By traditional measures of value, stocks do seem expensive right now. But those metrics have flaws, the worst of which is a tendency to look at the past rather than the future. Markets, by their nature, do the opposite.
