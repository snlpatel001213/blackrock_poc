CHAPEL HILL, N.C., Jan. 8, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- The University of North Carolina Kenan-Flagler Business School will host a conference call to unveil the results of a major study examining food prices trends among U.S. supermarket chains on Wednesday, January 10, 2018 at 2:00 pm ET. Amid news reports about the escalating price wars in the industry, the study focuses on the competitive price effect of Lidl's entry in the U.S. grocery market and the price-cutting strategies of Aldi, Food Lion, Kroger, Publix and Walmart.
