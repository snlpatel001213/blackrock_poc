NEW YORK, Jan. 24, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Rowley Law PLLC is investigating potential claims against U.S. Geothermal, Inc. (NYSE: HTM) and its board of directors for breach of fiduciary duty concerning the proposed acquisition of the company by Ormat Technologies Inc. Stockholders will receive $5.45 for each share of U.S. Geothermal, Inc. that they hold. The transaction is valued at approximately $109.9 million and is expected to close in the second quarter of 2018.
