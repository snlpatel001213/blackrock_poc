J-pop band still loyal to cryptocurrency after hack Monday, January 29, 2018 - 01:23 
Members of a Japanese girl pop group, the Virtual Currency Girls, have refused an offer to be paid in yen and say they'll stay loyal to cryptocurrencies despite a $530 million cyber heist jeopardising their chances of getting paid. Kate King reports
