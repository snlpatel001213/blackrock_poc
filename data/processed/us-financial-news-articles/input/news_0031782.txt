THE CITY OF NAPLES is an unusual choice for an art dealer looking to expand internationally. But for Thomas Dane, founder of the London-based gallery that bears his name, the idea of opening a new outpost on the Mediterranean coast was irresistible: “I can’t think of another city in Europe where I would like to see a gallery. This is an intuitive move. It’s a risk, but also an adventure.”
