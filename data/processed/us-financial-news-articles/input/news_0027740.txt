Simone Biles is one of the most electrifying athletes on the planet. Now 20, Biles has made a case as the greatest gymnast ever—a transformative talent who has pushed the boundaries of her sport, collecting virtually every honor it can bestow, including four gold medals at the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio.
To watch Biles compete at her gravity-defying best is to witness more than athletic craft and achievement. Her talent is incandescent, almost euphoric—the kind that makes even a casual observer slack-jawed and giddy.
