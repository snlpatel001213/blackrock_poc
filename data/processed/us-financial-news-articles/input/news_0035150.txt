January 22, 2018 / 8:15 PM / in 8 minutes Patterson-UTI says employees missing after Oklahoma rig fire Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
HOUSTON, Jan 22 (Reuters) - Drilling contractor Patterson-UTI said some of its employees were missing on Monday after a fire at one of its drilling rigs in Pittsburg County, Oklahoma. 
The company said it had activated its emergency response systems and was cooperating with first responders and authorities on the scene. (Reporting by Liz Hampton; Editing by Tom Brown)
