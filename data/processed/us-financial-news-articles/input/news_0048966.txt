CHARLOTTE, N.C., Jan. 29, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- SPX Corporation (NYSE:SPXC) announced today that it will release its fourth quarter and full-year 2017 financial results after market close on Thursday, February 15, 2018.
SPX Corporation President and Chief Executive Officer Gene Lowe and SPX Corporation Vice President, Chief Financial Officer and Treasurer Scott Sproule will discuss the company's 2017 results and 2018 financial guidance during a conference call at 4:45 p.m. Eastern time. Those interested in participating in the conference call should dial in five minutes prior to the start of the call. The call will be simultaneously webcast via the company's website at www.spx.com and the slide presentation will be available in the Investor Relations section of the site.
