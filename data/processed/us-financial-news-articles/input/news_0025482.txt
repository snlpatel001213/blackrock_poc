It isn’t a happy new year for dollar bulls. The U.S. currency has started 2018 by falling to a three-year low as measured by the ICE dollar index and is putting a decent dent into its big rally of 2014-15. The weak dollar has consequences elsewhere for central banks and markets—but isn’t necessarily something to be feared.
