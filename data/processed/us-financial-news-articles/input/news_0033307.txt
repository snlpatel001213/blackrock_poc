Jan 19 (Reuters) - KMH Co Ltd :
* Says it will issue 3rd series unregistered and unsecured private convertible bonds, to raise 3.8 billion won in proceeds for operations
* Maturity date is Jan. 18, 2021, coupon rate is 0 percent and yield to maturity is 1 percent for the bonds
* Conversion price is 10,834 won/share and conversion ratio is 100 percent
