In “Psychiatrists With Press Passes” (op-ed, Jan. 11), Karl Rove suggests (again) that President Trump should stop tweeting. Mr. Rove’s piece had an audience of the 2.5 million Journal subscribers. President Trump’s twitter account has 46.6 million followers and is increasing by 65,000 followers per day. Followers enjoy his tweets because they are clear, unfiltered by critics and often funny.
