Brexit won't hit ties, says China, as May seeks trade 5:06pm GMT - 01:45 
China's relations with Britain will remain unchanged through Brexit, Premier Li Keqiang said on Wednesday, as British Prime Minister Theresa May kicked off a three-day visit to China, where she aims to sign nine billion pounds in deals. David Pollard reports.
