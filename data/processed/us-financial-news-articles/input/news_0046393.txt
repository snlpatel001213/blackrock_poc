Jan 25 (Reuters) - SCHLATTER INDUSTRIES AG:
* INCREASED ITS NET SALES IN THE 2017 FINANCIAL YEAR BY 11.7% TO CHF 101.1 MILLION
* IN FY REALISED A LOWER ORDER INTAKE OF CHF 94.6 MILLION TO THE PREVIOUS YEAR
* ORDER BACKLOG AS AT 31.12.2017 STOOD AT CHF 42.5 MILLION (31.12.2016: CHF 49.0 MILLION)
* EXPECTS FY 2017 EBIT TO BE HIGHER THAN IN PREVIOUS YEAR OF AROUND CHF 2.5 MILLION (2016: CHF 1.1 MILLION) AND A NET PROFIT OF APPROXIMATELY CHF 3.0 MILLION (2016: CHF 0.5 MILLION)
