BALTIMORE, Jan. 23, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Under Armour, Inc. (NYSE: UA, UAA) plans to release the results of its fourth quarter (ended Dec. 31, 2017) on Tuesday, February 13, 2018 at approximately 7:00 a.m. ET. Following the news release, Under Armour management will host a conference call at 8:30 a.m. ET to review results.
This call will be webcast live at http://investor.underarmour.com/results.cfm and will be archived and available for replay approximately three hours after the live event.
