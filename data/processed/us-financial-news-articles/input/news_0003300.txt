In regard to Walter Russell Mead’s Dec. 26 op-ed “Trump’s ‘Blue Water’ Foreign Policy”: To its credit, the Trump administration’s national security statement is couched in more civil tones than those customarily employed by the president. Nevertheless, it provides little check on his more irresponsible impulses. In particular, it has nothing instructive to say on the two most pressing foreign policy problems confronting our country: potential war with North Korea and Russia’s continuing cyber attacks here and abroad. Mr. Mead’s salute to the NSS is a lofty and academic exercise that does not deign to mention either subject.
