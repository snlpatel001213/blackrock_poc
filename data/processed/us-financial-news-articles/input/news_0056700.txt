DALLAS, Jan. 30, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- InfraREIT, Inc. (NYSE: HIFR) ("InfraREIT" or the "Company") today announced its plans to release financial results for the fourth quarter and full year 2017 on Thursday, Mar. 1, 2018, before the opening of trading on the New York Stock Exchange. Copies of the Company's press release will be available on the InfraREIT Web site at www.InfraREITInc.com .
Management will host a conference call and webcast on the same day, Thursday, Mar. 1, 2018, at 10:00 a.m. U.S. Central Time to discuss the Company's fourth quarter and full year 2017 results and update its financial outlook. Prepared remarks by David A. Campbell, Chief Executive Officer, and Brant Meleski, Chief Financial Officer, will be followed by a question and answer session.
Interested parties are invited to pre-register for the telephonic portion of the call through a link posted in the "Investor Relations-Events-Presentations-Upcoming Events" section of the Company's Web site. Pre-registered telephonic participants will immediately receive an email with a calendar reminder, dial-in number and PIN that will allow them expedited access to the call on Mar. 1, 2018. Participants who do not wish to register in advance are invited to dial-in using 1-855-560-2576, or internationally at 1-412-542-4162, using access code: 10115363, or by asking for the InfraREIT conference call. The conference call will also be available through a webcast in the same section of the Investor Relations portion of the Company's Web site.
A replay of the conference call will be available on Thursday, Mar. 1, 2018, beginning at 5:00 p.m. U.S. Central Time, through Wednesday, Mar. 7, 2018. The phone number for the conference call replay is 1-877-344-7529 or, for calls from outside the U.S., 1-412-317-0088, using access code: 10115363. The replay will also be available on the Company's Web site following the end of the live call.
About InfraREIT, Inc.
InfraREIT is a real estate investment trust that is engaged in owning and leasing rate-regulated electric transmission assets in the state of Texas. The Company is externally managed by Hunt Utility Services, LLC, an affiliate of Hunt Consolidated, Inc. (a diversified holding company based in Dallas, Texas and managed by the Ray L. Hunt family). The Company's shares are traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol "HIFR". Additional information on InfraREIT is available at www.InfraREITInc.com .
