Why did sentiment on China improve so much in 2017? Progress in taming long-running structural problems, such as the country’s excess manufacturing capacity and mushrooming off-balance sheet debt, deservedly caught investors’ attention. The driving force behind that progress was the same factor that should now give investors pause: The primacy of Xi Jinping.
