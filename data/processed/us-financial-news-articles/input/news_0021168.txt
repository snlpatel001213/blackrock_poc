French dairy firm under fire over Salmonella outbreak 5:18pm GMT - 01:23 
One of the world's biggest dairies has come under fire for its slow response to a Salmonella outbreak. French company Lactalis has promised to compensate victims of a Salmonella contamination in its baby milk. Anna Bevan reports.
