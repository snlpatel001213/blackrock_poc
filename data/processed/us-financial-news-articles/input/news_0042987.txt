ENGLEWOOD CLIFFS, N.J., January 24, 2018 â€“ CNBC, First in Business Worldwide, today announced it will offer an audio news brief for HomePod, Apple's new wireless speaker for the home, which will be available to order on Friday, January 26.
Customers can simply say, "Hey Siri, play business news" or "Hey Siri, play me the news from CNBC," and the CNBC audio news brief will immediately provide users with news updates throughout the business day and insightful weekend look ahead features from CNBC anchors and reporters, as well as market commentary from CNBC's On-Air Stocks Editor Bob Pisani from the floor of the New York Stock Exchange.
Users can also hear the latest business news with the CNBC audio news brief on iPhone, iPad and Apple TV running iOS and tvOS 11.2.5.
