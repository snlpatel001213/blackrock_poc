Pope Francis drew anger from victims of clerical sexual abuse in Chile when he said they had slandered a bishop who is accused of covering up molestation of minors.
The accusations surround Bishop Juan Barros, who was appointed in 2015 to lead the diocese of Osorno in southern Chile. Bishop Barros has been accused by victims of Rev. Fernando Karadima of covering up abuses by Father Karadima. Father Karadima, an influential priest in Santiago, was removed from service by the Vatican and ordered in 2011 to a life of prayer...
