Budget battle in Congress risks U.S. readiness 11:24am EST - 02:01 
Pentagon planners warn a series of short-term budget deals and delays are having a real impact on national defense.. Everything from shipbuilding to live fire exercises are getting pushed back or put on hold. The end result, military leaders worry, is a fighting force that's less ready and less well-equipped.
