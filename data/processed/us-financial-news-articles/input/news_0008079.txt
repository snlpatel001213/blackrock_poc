Regarding “The Tax Bill Won’t Hurt Charitable Giving” (Letters, Jan. 2): On Dec. 31 the Cincinnati Bengals defeated the Baltimore Ravens on a last minute, fourth down, 49-yard pass play from Andy Dalton to Tyler Boyd, enabling the Buffalo Bills to make the playoffs for the first time in 17 years. Thousands of Bills fans have been flooding Mr. Dalton and Mr. Boyd’s charitable foundations in $17 increments to a total of more than $250,000. Apparently, Bills fans neglected to consult with their accountants before reaching for their checkbooks.
