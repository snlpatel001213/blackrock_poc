The European Union again flexed its legal muscle against a U.S. tech giant by hitting Qualcomm Inc. with a €997 million ($1.23 billion) antitrust fine on Wednesday for payments it made to Apple Inc. for the device maker’s exclusive use of Qualcomm chips.
