UK gifts Macron $62 million with eye to Brexit 3:23pm IST - 02:04 
British Prime Minister Theresa May is offering France $62 million to bolster their border controls. But as French President Emmanuel Macron makes his first state visit to the UK, this and other gifts may mean brushing awkward Brexit points under the carpet. Reuters' Jacob Greaves reports from London.
