SAN DIEGO, Jan. 11, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Nicholas J. De Blouw, a member of the Employment and Consumer Class Action group at Blumenthal Nordrehaug Bhowmik De Blouw LLP, has been named a partner of the California employment law firm .
De Blouw's practice area focuses on complex class actions brought on behalf of employees and consumers against large companies. He has experience with a variety of employment issues, including wage and hour class actions, wrongful termination, unpaid commission wages, missed meal and rest breaks, and violations of The Fair Credit Reporting Act.
De Blouw has worked on approximately 300 class actions involving many types of claims made under various California and federal labor laws. He received his J.D. from California Western School of Law and his undergraduate degree in Political Science, with honors, from Wayne State University, Detroit, Michigan.
Blumenthal Nordrehaug Bhowmik De Blouw LLP is a leading California employment law firm with offices in San Diego, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Sacramento, Riverside, and Chicago, Illinois. The firm represents consumers and employees in individual and class action cases involving violations of state and federal labor laws, including overtime pay, unemployment, employer retaliation, discrimination, wage and hour laws, wrongful termination, employee benefits, and Fair Credit Reporting Act violations.
