A rare campaign to recall a California judge who drew controversy for his handling of a sexual-assault case involving a Stanford University swimmer cleared a major hurdle this week and could be headed for a June ballot.
Backers submitted nearly 95,000 signatures Thursday to local officials in support of a recall of Santa Clara County Superior Court Judge Aaron Persky, a former prosecutor who has been on the bench since 2003.
