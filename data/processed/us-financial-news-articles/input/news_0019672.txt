A Mexican immigrant associated with a movement to shelter undocumented immigrants in places of worship was arrested by federal officials Thursday, his family members said, a move religious groups called a blow to their efforts.
Eliseo Jurado, the partner of a Peruvian woman taking sanctuary in a Colorado church, is the third person associated with the sanctuary movement to be detained over the past week, advocates said.
