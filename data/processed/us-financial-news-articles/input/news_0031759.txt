Jan 18 (Reuters) - Wooriro Co Ltd :
* Says it will issue 10th series unregistered and unsecured private convertible bonds, to raise 10 billion won in proceeds for facilities and investment as well as operations
* Maturity date is Jan. 24, 2021, coupon rate is 4 percent and yield to maturity is 4 percent for the bonds
* Conversion price is 3,098 won/share and conversion ratio is 100 percent
