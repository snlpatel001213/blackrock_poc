Jan 29(Reuters) - Fuan Pharmaceutical Group Co Ltd
* Sees FY 2017 net profit to increase by 21.6 percent to 39.7 percent, or to be 270 million yuan to 310 million yuan
* Says FY 2016 net profit was 222 million yuan
* Says increased operating revenue and gross margin and inclusion of new company are the main reasons for the forecast
