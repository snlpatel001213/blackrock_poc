Former Superintendent, Clara Pitoscia Brings 30+ Years of Education Experience to Role
AUSTIN, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- TalentEd, the leader in talent management software for K-12 education, has selected respected educator and school administrator Clara Pitoscia to serve as Strategic Education Advisor for Canada.
Pitoscia brings a wealth of knowledge to TalentEd’s team, having spent three decades working in education. Most recently, Pitoscia was Superintendent of Human Resources & Employee Relations for Ontario K-12 schools.
“Clara is a passionate, experienced and committed educator,” said Jack Blaha, CEO of PeopleAdmin, TalentEd’s parent company. “Her experience with people management, Canadian school boards and creating successful teaching and learning environments make her an invaluable asset to both our Canadian customers and our team.”
Pitoscia will play a key role in growing TalentEd’s presence in Canada and supporting Perform — TalentEd’s software solution designed to streamline evaluation processes, improve communication between educators and supervisors, and link educator evaluations with professional development to enhance educator effectiveness and student achievement.
To learn more about Pitoscia and TalentEd, join the webinar “ Leveraging Technology and Partnerships to Meet all of Ontario’s K-12 Talent Management Needs ,” Feb. 1, 2018, at 10 a.m.
About TalentEd
Powered by actionable analytics and insights gained from a team of education research experts, TalentEd by PeopleAdmin delivers solutions that streamline K-12 education recruitment, assessment, hiring, onboarding, records and contract management, absence management, performance management and professional development management.
TalentEd partners with thousands of schools in the U.S. and Canada to deliver data-driven talent management solutions that improve efficiency and effectiveness, so customers may focus on what they do best — empowering employees, advancing student achievement and inspiring a brighter future.
