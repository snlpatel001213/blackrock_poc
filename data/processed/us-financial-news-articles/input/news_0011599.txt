WASHINGTON—Rep. Ed Royce, a Republican representing a Southern California district, announced Monday he wouldn’t seek re-election, putting into play a seat that Democrats see as a potential pickup in the 2018 midterm elections.

Mr. Royce, 66 years old, chairs the House Foreign Affairs Committee and would have been forced to stand down from that position if re-elected, due to Republican term limits on panel leaders.
