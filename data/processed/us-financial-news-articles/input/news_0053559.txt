NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Levi & Korsinsky announces it has commenced an investigation of MetLife, Inc. (NYSE: MET) concerning possible violations of federal securities laws.
On January 29, 2018, MetLife announced it had postponed its earnings report and conference call related to its results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2017. The Company cited “material weakness” in its financial reporting. MetLife also advised investors it expects to increase reserves in total between $525 and $575 million on a pre-tax basis to cover certain annuity recipients “who have been unresponsive or missing over time.” To obtain additional information, go to :
