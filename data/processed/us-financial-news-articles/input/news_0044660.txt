In “Studying Western Civilization in the South Bronx” (Cross Country, Jan. 13), Jillian Kay Melchior reports the now well-known objection to learning in these courses only about “dead white dudes” in the Western canon. What is consistently lost in this discussion is the reason for studying this subject in the first place.
