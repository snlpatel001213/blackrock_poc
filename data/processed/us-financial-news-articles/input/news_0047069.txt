PARIS—A new wave of big-spending Chinese shoppers is driving torrid sales growth for the global luxury-goods industry after two years of cautious Chinese buying.

Buoyed by a surging stock market, Chinese consumers are splurging at home, during trips to Europe and increasingly online.
