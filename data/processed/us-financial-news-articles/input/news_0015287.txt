Rising shares of banks and health-care companies pushed major U.S. indexes up Tuesday, as investor confidence in the New Year rally showed few signs of abating.
Stocks have steadily climbed higher in the first trading days of 2018, extending last year’s rally. That is leaving investors hard pressed to find clues portending the rally’s end, especially as geopolitical tensions with North Korea appear to be easing and ahead of another quarter of upbeat earnings results.
