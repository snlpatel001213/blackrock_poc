LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Global nutrition company, Herbalife Ltd. (NYSE:HLF) will release its fourth quarter and full year 2017 financial results after the close of trading on the NYSE on Thursday, February 22, 2018. The same day, at 5:30 p.m. ET (2:30 p.m. PT), the Herbalife senior management team will host an investor conference call to discuss its recent financial results and provide an update on current business trends.
The dial-in number for this conference call for domestic callers is (877) 317-1296, and (262) 320-2006 for international callers (conference ID: 3862679). Live audio of the conference call will be simultaneously webcast in the investor relations section of the Company's website at http://ir.herbalife.com .
An audio replay will be available following the completion of the conference call in MP3 format or by dialing (855) 859-2056 for domestic callers or (404) 537-3406 for international callers (conference ID: 3862679). The webcast of the teleconference will be archived and available on Herbalife's website.
