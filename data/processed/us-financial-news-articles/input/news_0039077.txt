HONOLULU—Just a few months before a daunting primary, Gov. David Ige is on an apology tour.
In public appearances in the week since a false missile alert thrust Hawaii into panic, Mr. Ige has said he was sorry for the “fear and pain.” He was grilled by lawmakers about what went wrong. Questioned by reporters at a press conference Monday, he admitted his own response was delayed because he didn’t know his Twitter password.
