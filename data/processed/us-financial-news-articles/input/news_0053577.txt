Sluggish iPhone X sales revive a perennial risk for the army of companies that supply Apple Inc. For 3-D sensor maker ams AG, however, the troubles are dwarfed by the growth prospects.
Apple halved its first-quarter production target for its latest iPhone and cut component orders by 60%, The Wall Street Journal reported Tuesday. Yet on Monday iPhone X supplier ams guided up analysts’ growth expectations. The stock, Europe’s top large-cap performer in 2017, jumped another 17%.
