SEOUL—South Korea’s finance regulators are expanding measures to verify cryptocurrency investors’ identities, as part of the country’s moves to control one of Asia’s hottest bitcoin markets.
Korean regulators Tuesday said only individuals with verified bank accounts linked to cryptocurrency exchanges will be able to inject more fiat money into virtual currencies.
