Michigan special prosecutor takes on Nassar case Monday, January 29, 2018 - 01:40 
Michigan’s attorney general named a special prosecutor to lead an ''open and ongoing'' investigation of Michigan State University after campus sports doctor Larry Nassar sexually assaulted athletes over nearly 20 years. Lisa Bernhard reports.
