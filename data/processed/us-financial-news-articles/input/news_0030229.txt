January 18, 2018 / 10:30 AM / Updated 14 minutes ago Emirates places provisional order for 20 Airbus A380s Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
DUBAI, Jan 18 (Reuters) - Dubai-based airline Emirates has made a provisional order for 20 Airbus A380s, it said on Thursday. 
The deal includes 20 firm orders for the aircraft and options for a further 16, it said in the statement, adding the total value for the deal was $16 billion. (Reporting By Tom Arnold)
