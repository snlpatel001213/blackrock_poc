Saudi closes Ritz detention center as purge eases Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:19 
Saudi Arabian authorities have released all remaining detainees from Riyadh's Ritz-Carlton Hotel according to a Reuters source. It comes as Saudi authorities claim they aim to seize around $100 billion of illicit assets.
