MIT scientists develop robot to move with the crowds 1:01pm EST - 01:59 
Massachusetts Institute of Technology scientists are developing a robot to navigate its way through the unpredictable flow of human foot traffic, which could help with delivering goods, operating in malls and working in hospitals transporting medicine and moving patients. Roselle Chen reports.
