Alienation drives young Palestinians beyond politics Thursday, January 25, 2018 - 01:38 
Young Palestinians are increasingly beyond the reach of political factions as confrontations between masked protesters and Israeli soldiers take on a life of their own, particularly after U.S. President Donald Trump's decision to treat Jerusalem as Israel's capital. Matthew Larotonda reports.
