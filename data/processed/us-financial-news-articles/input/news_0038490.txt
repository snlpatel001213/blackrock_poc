MILWAUKEE, Jan. 22, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- The Wisconsin Electric board of directors on Jan. 22 declared a quarterly cash dividend of 90 cents a share on the company's Preferred Stock, 3.60% Series, payable March 1, 2018, to stockholders of record on Feb. 14, 2018. The board also declared a quarterly cash dividend of $1.50 a share on the company's Six Per Cent Preferred Stock, payable April 30, 2018, to stockholders of record on April 13, 2018.
Wisconsin Electric Power Co., doing business as We Energies, is a subsidiary of WEC Energy Group (NYSE: WEC). The company serves more than 1.1 million electric customers and more than 460,000 natural gas customers in Wisconsin. Visit the We Energies website at we-energies.com .
