Wells Fargo & Co. has appointed a former Federal Reserve Bank of New York official to oversee regulatory relations for its corporate risk group, a division of the company that has faced challenges and heightened regulatory scrutiny.
Sarah Dahlgren is joining Wells Fargo from McKinsey & Co., where she has served as a partner focusing on risk management since 2016. Ms. Dahlgren had spent 25 years at the New York Fed, most recently as the head of financial institution supervision.
