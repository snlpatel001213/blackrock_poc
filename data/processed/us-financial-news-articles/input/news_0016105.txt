Rubenstein Partners LP, a real-estate investment firm that specializes in office buildings, has doubled down on the Indianapolis suburbs, spending $133 million on an office park whose distinguishing feature is a 38-acre lake.
A venture including Philadelphia-based Rubenstein and Strategic Capital Partners of Indianapolis has purchased Precedent Office Park, which includes 1.1 million square feet in 19 buildings north of downtown Indianapolis. The seller was LaSalle Investment Management, of Chicago.
