Republican Senator Hatch to retire, opening door for bid by Romney 10:02am IST - 01:13 
U.S. Republican Senator Orrin Hatch said on Tuesday he will not seek re-election in November, opening the door to a potential Senate bid by Mitt Romney, the 2012 Republican presidential candidate and one of the party's harshest critics of President Donald Trump. Andy Sullivan reports.
