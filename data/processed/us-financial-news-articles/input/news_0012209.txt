Industry gives UK economy a shot in the arm 12:39pm EST - 01:45 
British industry enjoyed solid growth in November, benefiting from a global upturn that has allowed the economy to outperform gloomy forecasts made after 2016's Brexit vote, although it still lags behind its international rivals. Sonia Legg reports.
