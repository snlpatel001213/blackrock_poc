January 18, 2018 / 12:56 PM / Updated 11 minutes ago India insolvency court to hear Ericsson's petition against RCom next on Feb 19 Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
MUMBAI (Reuters) - India’s National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) will hear next on Feb. 19 Ericsson’s insolvency petition against telecoms carrier Reliance Communications, two lawyers on the case said. 
The NCLT initially on Thursday set the next date of hearing for Feb. 5, but later changed it to Feb. 19 on the lawyers’ plea. Reporting by Abhirup Roy; Editing by Subhranshu Sahu
