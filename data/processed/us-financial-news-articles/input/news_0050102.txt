HOUSTON, Jan. 29, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Powell Industries, Inc. (NASDAQ: POWL), a leading supplier of custom engineered solutions for the management, control and distribution of electrical energy, today announced that it will release fiscal 2018 first quarter results on Tuesday, February 6, 2018 after the market closes. In conjunction with the release, Powell Industries has scheduled a conference call, which will be broadcast live over the Internet, on Wednesday, February 7, 2018 at 11:00 a.m. eastern time.
