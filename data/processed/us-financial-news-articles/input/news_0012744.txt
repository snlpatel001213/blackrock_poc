Jan 8 (Reuters) - Fortis Inc :
* Says it will issue 14th series unregistered/unsecured convertible bonds worth of 10 billion won
* Says a 100 percent conversion rate of bonds to common shares at 1,510 won/share
* Says annual coupon rate is 0 percent and yield to maturity is 0 percent, and conversion period from March 20, 2019 to Feb. 27, 2021
