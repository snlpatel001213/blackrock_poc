January 11, 2018 / 1:18 PM / Updated 15 minutes ago Russia investigating drone attacks on Syria bases - RIA Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russia’s defence ministry said on Thursday it was investigating which country produced explosives used in recent drone attacks on Russian bases in Syria, RIA news agency cited the ministry as saying. 
“The explosive material in question is produced in a variety of countries, including in Ukraine,” RIA quoted major general Alexander Novikov as saying. Writing by Polina Ivanova; Editing by Vladimir Soldatkin
