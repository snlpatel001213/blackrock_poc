Jan 9 (Reuters) - Investment advisory firm Angel Island Capital said on Tuesday it appointed Lynette Vanderwarker as chief strategy officer. 
Vanderwarker joins from KKR &Co LLP, where she led the credit strategies group as managing director. 
Before that, she worked at BlackRock Inc for 10 years. (Reporting by Ahmed Farhatha in Bengaluru)
