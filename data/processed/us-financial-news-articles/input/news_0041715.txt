DUBLIN, Ohio, Jan. 24, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- The Wendy's Company (NASDAQ: WEN) will release its preliminary fourth quarter and full year 2017 results after the market closes on Wednesday, February 21. A conference call will follow on Thursday, February 22 at 9:00 a.m. ET, with a simultaneous webcast from the Investors section of the Company's website at www.aboutwendys.com . The live conference call, including the question and answer session, is expected to last up to 90 minutes and will be available by teleconference at (877) 572-6014 for domestic callers and (281) 913-8524 for international callers. An archived webcast with the accompanying slides will be available on the Company's website at www.aboutwendys.com .
