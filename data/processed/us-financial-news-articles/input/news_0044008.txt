From Aleppo to Davos for Oscar-tipped film 2:06pm EST - 01:12 
‘Last Men in Aleppo’, a documentary following the White Helmets rescuers under siege, was tipped for an Oscar this week. It was the first Syrian film ever nominated and it screened at Davos. Reuters’ Lucy Fielder spoke to the director, Feras Fayyad.
