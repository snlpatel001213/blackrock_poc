BISMARCK, N.D., Jan. 8, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- MDU Resources Group, Inc. (NYSE: MDU) will webcast its year-end 2017 earnings and 2018 guidance conference call Feb. 7 following the release of its results.
The webcast will begin at 2 p.m. EST and can be accessed at www.mdu.com . Audio and webcast replays will be available. Audio will be available through Feb. 21 at (855) 859-2056, or (404) 537‑3406 for international callers, conference ID 3158659.
