WASHINGTON—The Republican Governors Association has decided to give back $100,000 in donations from Steve Wynn’s company and cut other financial ties with the casino mogul, in the wake of a Wall Street Journal report on allegations describing a decadeslong pattern of sexual misconduct by Mr. Wynn.
“These are serious allegations,” said a written statement issued Monday by the governors’ group. The RGA also said it canceled its contract with Wynn Resorts Ltd. to hold its 2020 annual conference at the Wynn Las Vegas.
