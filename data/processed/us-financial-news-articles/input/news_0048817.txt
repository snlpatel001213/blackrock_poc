Updated 6 Mins Ago CNBC.com 
Revlon announced on Monday that President and CEO Fabian Garcia will step down to pursue other career opportunities. 
Board member Paul Meister will assume the role of Executive Vice Chairman of the Board and oversee day-to-day operations of the company on an interim basis. Garcia will stay on until the end of February, according to a company statement.
