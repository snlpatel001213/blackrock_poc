January 11, 2018 / 1:15 PM / Updated 27 minutes ago Bosnian region raises $12.3 mln via 6-mo T-bills issue, yield unchanged Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
SARAJEVO, Jan 11 (Reuters) - Bosnia's autonomous Serb Republic raised 20 million Bosnian marka ($12.3 million) through an auction of six-month treasury bills on Thursday aimed at helping it plug a budget gap and finance maturing debt, the Banja Luka Stock Exchange said in a filing.
