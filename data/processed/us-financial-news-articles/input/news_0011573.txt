No new Airbus orders for Macron in China 5:08pm GMT - 01:50 
President Emmanuel Macron has offered to open up the French economy to Chinese investment in exchange for greater access to China's booming markets, warning in talks in Beijing that existing trade imbalances would lead to protectionism. But, as Sonia Legg reports, despite bringing with him a delegation of French business, finance and industry, there were no immediate announcements on the second leg of his three-day trip of major deals.
