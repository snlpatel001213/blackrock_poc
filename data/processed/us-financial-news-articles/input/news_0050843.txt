NEW YORK, Jan. 30, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Nomura announced today that Charlie McElligott has joined as a Managing Director and cross asset strategist for the firm's Global Markets business.
Mr. McElligott is based in New York and joins Nomura from RBC Capital Markets where he was most recently a Managing Director and Head of US Cross-Asset Macro Strategy.
In this new role, Mr. McElligott will concentrate on the macro markets with a focus on positioning and sentiment alongside quantitative factor analysis. His goal is to provide actionable insights through daily content and high touch coverage, including risk-assessment, survey work and trade ideas.
"Charlie has earned a great reputation as a sharp macro thinker over the last 15 years and we look forward to his contribution to the Nomura client franchise," said Samir Patel, Head of Equity Products Sales and Structuring for Nomura in the Americas.
