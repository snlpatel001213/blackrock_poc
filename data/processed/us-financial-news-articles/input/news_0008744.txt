Altice boosted by plan to spin off U.S. operation Tuesday, January 09, 2018 - 01:06 
Telecoms and cable group Altice NV is separating its U.S. and European operations to try to reassure investors alarmed by its high debt and low revenue generation, especially in its core French telecoms business. Sonia Legg reports.
