MOSCOW, Jan 18 (Reuters) - Russian state bank VTB said on Thursday it has agreed to provide the phone operator Megafon with banking guarantees of up to 4 billion roubles ($70.5 million), which would last for 4 years.
Megafon plans to use the tool to meet its current financial needs.
