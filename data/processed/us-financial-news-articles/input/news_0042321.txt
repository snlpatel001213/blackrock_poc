ALEXANDRIA, Va., Jan. 25, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Robbins-Gioia, LLC (RG) announced that the Board of Managers has elected Phil Nolan as Chairman of the Board. Mr. Nolan, who originally joined RG's Board in March 2017, served in the CEO role at Camber Corporation, Galeos Group, and Stanley, Inc.
"Robbins-Gioia has a long, storied, and well-deserved reputation for excellence in our space and I am honored to be a part of their continued success," said Mr. Nolan.
In addition to his corporate leadership roles, Mr. Nolan serves on other Boards, including INOVA Health Systems. Prior to his private sector career, Phil served on active duty as a submarine officer in the U.S. Navy's Nuclear Power Program. He retired as a Captain in the U.S. Navy Reserve after 24 years of service.
RG CEO Brad King states that, "Phil has been a valuable partner and member of our Board over the past year and my respect for his character and insight has only grown. We are delighted that he has agreed to now serve as Chairman."
