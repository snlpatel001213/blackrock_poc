PHOENIX--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Pinnacle West Capital Corp. (NYSE: PNW) announced today that it has updated the start time for management’s live webcast and conference call to discuss 2017 financial results and recent developments. The new start time will be 11 a.m. ET (9 a.m. Arizona time) on Friday, Feb. 23, 2018.
Earlier that same morning – before U.S. financial markets open – the Company will release its 2017 fourth-quarter and full-year financial results.
To access the live conference call by telephone, participants can dial (877) 407-8035 or (201) 689-8035 for international callers. A replay of the call also will be available until 11:59 p.m. (ET), Friday, March 2, 2018, by calling (877) 481-4010 in the U.S. and Canada or (919) 882-2331 internationally and entering conference ID number 23277. A replay of the webcast can be accessed at pinnaclewest.com/presentations .
Pinnacle West Capital Corp ., an energy holding company based in Phoenix, has consolidated assets of nearly $17 billion, about 6,200 megawatts of generating capacity and 6,300 employees in Arizona and New Mexico. Through its principal subsidiary, Arizona Public Service , the Company provides retail electricity service to nearly 1.2 million Arizona homes and businesses. For more information about Pinnacle West, visit the Company’s website at pinnaclewest.com .
