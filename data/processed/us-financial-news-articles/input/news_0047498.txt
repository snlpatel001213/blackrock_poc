MILWAUKEE, Jan. 24, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Briggs & Stratton Corporation (NYSE:BGG):
At its regular quarterly meeting held today, the Board of Directors of Briggs & Stratton Corporation declared a quarterly dividend of fourteen cents ($0.14) per share on the common stock of the Corporation. The dividend is payable April 3, 2018 to shareholders of record at the close of business March 16, 2018.
