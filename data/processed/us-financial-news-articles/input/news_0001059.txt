January 2, 2018 / 9:27 AM / Updated 26 minutes ago Renault Group says France sales up 3.4 percent in 2017, highest in six years Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
PARIS (Reuters) - Renault Group ( RENA.PA ) said on Tuesday its 2017 sales rose 3.4 percent in France to 673,869 units, the highest in six years, driven by a 6.5 percent increase at its no-frills Dacia brand. The logo of Renault is seen during the 87th International Motor Show at Palexpo in Geneva, Switzerland March 8, 2017. REUTERS/Arnd Wiegmann 
Sales for the Renault brand were up 2.7 percent, the company said. Reporting by Michel Rose; editing by Jason Neely
