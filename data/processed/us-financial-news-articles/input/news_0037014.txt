January 23, 2018 / 9:22 PM / Updated 23 minutes ago U.S. investigators to probe crash between Tesla vehicle and fire truck   
WASHINGTON (Reuters) - The U.S. National Transportation Safety Board said on Tuesday it was sending two investigators to examine a crash on Monday involving a Tesla vehicle and a fire truck in California. 
In a post on Twitter, the federal safety agency said the investigation would examine both driver and vehicle factors. Reporting by David Shepardson; Writing by Tim Ahmann; Editing by Eric Walsh
