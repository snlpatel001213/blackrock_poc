NEW YORK, Jan. 30, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Flat Rock Capital Corp. ("Flat Rock Capital") is pleased to announce that its Board of Directors declared a January 2018 monthly dividend of $.108 per share. The dividend will be payable on February 6 th , 2018 to shareholders with a record date of January 26 th , 2018. This is our sixth consecutive monthly dividend of $.108 per share.
About Flat Rock Capital:
Flat Rock Capital, our flagship fund, deploys a defensive, low volatility strategy by investing exclusively in the first lien, floating rate debt of middle market businesses. Flat Rock Capital is a business development company (a "BDC").
We will host a quarterly webinar and distribute a fund fact sheet approximately 45 days after each quarter-end. Our year-end webinar and fact sheet will be distributed approximately 75 days after year-end. Flat Rock Capital also files quarterly 10Qs and annual 10Ks that are available on line via the SEC Edgar system.
About Flat Rock Global, LLC:
Flat Rock Global, LLC, ("Flat Rock Global") the advisor for Flat Rock Capital, is an experienced credit manager focused on unique income investments outside of traditional fixed income. Flat Rock Global funds are available to Institutional Investors, Family Offices, and RIAs.
To learn more about the firm, please visit www.flatrockglobal.com
