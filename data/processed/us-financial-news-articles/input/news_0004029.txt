SAN JOSE, Calif., Jan. 2, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Maxim Integrated Products, Inc. (NASDAQ: MXIM) will announce fiscal second quarter 2018 results after market close on Thursday, January 25, 2018. The full release will be available at investor.maximintegrated.com .
A conference call and live webcast will follow at 2:00 p.m. Pacific Time, which can be accessed at investor.maximintegrated.com. A replay of the webcast will be available at the same location, beginning at 6:00 p.m. Pacific Time on January 25.
About Maxim Integrated
Maxim Integrated develops innovative analog and mixed-signal products and technologies to make systems smaller and smarter, with enhanced security and increased energy efficiency.
