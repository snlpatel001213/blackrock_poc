BURR RIDGE, Ill., Jan. 26, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- BankFinancial Corporation (Nasdaq:BFIN) announced that its Board of Directors declared a cash dividend of $0.08 per common share. The dividend will be payable on February 23, 2018 to stockholders of record on February 7, 2018.
BankFinancial Corporation is the holding company for BankFinancial, NA, a full-service, community-oriented bank providing financial services to individuals, families and businesses through 19 full-service banking offices, located in Cook, DuPage, Lake and Will Counties, Illinois. At September 30, 2017, BankFinancial had total assets of $1.654 billion, total loans of $1.336 billion, total deposits of $1.371 billion and stockholders’ equity of $200 million. BankFinancial Corporation’s common stock trades on the Nasdaq Global Select Market under the symbol BFIN.
