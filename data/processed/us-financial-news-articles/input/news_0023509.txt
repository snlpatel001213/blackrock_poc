Selling candy bars isn’t such a bad business after all.
Swiss food giant Nestlé on Tuesday announced the sale of Butterfinger, Baby Ruth and other American sweet treats to Ferrero Group. The closely held Italian company behind Nutella hazelnut spread and Tic Tac mints is paying 2.8 billion Swiss francs ($2.9 billion) for a business that generated roughly 900 million francs of sales in 2016.
