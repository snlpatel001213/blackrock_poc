DIY diabetics create open source artificial pancreas system 4:24pm GMT - 02:03 
A group of diabetics in Seattle have developed an artificial pancreas system that they are sharing with other diabetics through 'open source' technology. It's an advance that aims to revolutionise the lives of those with the condition. Elly Park reports.
