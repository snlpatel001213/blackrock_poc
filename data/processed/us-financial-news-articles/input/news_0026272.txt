Mission critical services firm adds Fortune 50 company to list of real estate clients
ORANGEBURG, N.Y.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- The SKAE Group, LLC (SKAE), a professional services firm, announced the acquisition of a facility on 2000 Corporate Drive Orangeburg, NY from Verizon Wireless in a sale-leaseback transaction. This is a 255,000 square foot facility comprised of data center and office space. The building sits on a 34-acre campus in an area that has recently become a hot bed of data center activity.

About The SKAE Group, LLC
The SKAE Group, LLC is a mission critical services company that specializes in data centers and critical infrastructure facilities. The company owns, manages and operates a multitude of data centers in the eastern region of the United States. For more information, please visit www.skaepower.com .
