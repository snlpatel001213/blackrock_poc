Animals are freezing in Florida Monday, January 08, 2018 - 01:33 
Fish are dying in Florida, frozen iguanas are falling from trees, manatees are seeking out warmer water and sea turtles are being rescued from frigid water during this January cold snap. Jillian Kitchener reports.
