January 23, 2018 / 12:58 PM / Updated 14 minutes ago Brexit implementation period has not been agreed with EU - PM May's spokesman Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
LONDON (Reuters) - Britain is still working to agree a transitional agreement on exiting the European Union, Prime Minister Theresa May’s spokesman said on Tuesday, after a report by The Independent that a deal had been reached. 
“To suggest that agreement has already been reached would be wrong,” the spokesman said. Reporting By William James. Editing by Andrew MacAskill
