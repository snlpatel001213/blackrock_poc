TORONTO, Jan 31 (Reuters) - Canada’s main stock index opened marginally higher on Wednesday, with most sectors rising, led by technology and healthcare. 
Shortly after the opening bell, the Toronto Stock Exchange’s S&P/TSX composite index was up 15.77 points, or 0.1 percent, at 15,971.28. (Reporting By Nichola Saminather Editing by Chizu Nomiyama)
