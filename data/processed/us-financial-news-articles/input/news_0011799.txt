German workers widen strikes in wage disputes 5:15pm GMT - 01:46 
Powerful German union IG Metall has vowed to step up industrial action ahead of a round of negotiations with employers as it pushes for higher wages and the right to shorter hours for 3.9 million German workers. David Pollard reports.
