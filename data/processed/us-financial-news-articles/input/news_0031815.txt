SAN MATEO, Calif., Jan. 18, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- GoPro, Inc. (NASDAQ: GPRO) today announced that it will release its financial results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2017, after the market closes on February 1, 2018.
GoPro management will host a conference call and live webcast for analysts and investors on February 1, 2018 at 2 p.m. Pacific Time (5 p.m. Eastern Time) to discuss the Company's financial results.
A live webcast of the conference call will be accessible on the "Events & Presentations" section of the Company's website at https://investor.gopro.com . The webcast will be recorded and the recording will be available on GoPro's website approximately two hours after the call and for 90 days thereafter. To listen to the live conference call, please dial toll free (888) 271-8595 or (719) 325-2484, access code 2807712, approximately 10 minutes prior to the start of the call.
