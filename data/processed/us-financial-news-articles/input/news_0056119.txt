January 31, 2018 / 5:04 PM / in 16 minutes Russian court freezes 38.1 pct of ordinary shares in Vozrozhdenie bank -TASS Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
MOSCOW, Jan 31 (Reuters) - A Russian court has frozen 38.1 percent of the ordinary shares of Vozrozhdenie Bank as part of legal claim by Promsvyazbank, TASS news agency reported on Wednesday, citing court documents. (Reporting by Jack Stubbs; Editing by Susan Fenton)
