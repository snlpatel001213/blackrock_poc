RALEIGH, N.C., Jan. 31, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The Board of Directors of First Citizens BancShares Inc. (Nasdaq:FCNCA) declared on Jan. 30, 2018, a quarterly dividend of 35 cents per share on the Class A and Class B common stock. The dividend is payable April 2, 2018, to shareholders of record March 19, 2018.
ABOUT FIRST CITIZENS BANCSHARES
First Citizens BancShares Inc. is the financial holding company for First Citizens Bank. First Citizens Bank provides a broad range of financial services to individuals, businesses, professionals and the medical community through branch offices in 21 states, digital banking, ATMs and telephone banking.
