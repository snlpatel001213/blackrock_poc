NASHVILLE, Tenn., Jan. 12, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- CapStar Financial Holdings, Inc. (“ CapStar ”) (NASDAQ:CSTR) announced today that it will issue its fourth quarter 2017 earnings release after the market closes on January 25, 2018.
