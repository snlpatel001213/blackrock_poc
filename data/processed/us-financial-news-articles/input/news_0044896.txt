Conference call and webcast to be held on Tuesday, January 30 at 5:00 p.m. Eastern
SOUTHFIELD, Mich.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Sterling Bancorp, Inc. (NASDAQ: SBT) announced today that it will issue its fourth quarter and fiscal year ended December 31, 2017 financial results after the market closes on Tuesday, January 30, 2018.
Sterling Bancorp will also host a conference call and webcast at 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time that same day to discuss its financial results.
Conference Call, Replay and Webcast Information:
Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018
Time: 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time
Telephone Access: U.S. callers: (877) 270-2148; International callers: (412) 902-6510
Telephone Replay (available through February 13, 2018): (877) 344-7529; conference ID: 10116614
Webcast Access: A live webcast will be available in the Investor Relations section of the Company’s website. An archived version of the webcast will be available in the same location shortly after the live call has ended.
About Sterling Bancorp, Inc.
Sterling Bancorp, Inc. is a unitary thrift holding company. Its wholly owned subsidiary, Sterling Bank and Trust, F.S.B., has primary branch operations in San Francisco and Los Angeles, California and New York City, and a loan production office in Seattle, Washington. Sterling offers a broad range of loan products to the residential and commercial markets, as well as retail and business banking services. Sterling also has an operations center and a branch in Southfield, Michigan. For additional information, please visit the Company’s website at www.sterlingbank.com .
