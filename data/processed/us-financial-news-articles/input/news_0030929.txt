EL DORADO, Ark.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Deltic Timber Corporation (NYSE:DEL) today announced that it will release fourth quarter and full-year 2017 earnings on Wednesday, February 14, 2017, after the market closes. Deltic will not be hosting a call to discuss results due to the pending merger with Potlatch Corporation. The earnings release will be available through the Investor Relations section of the Deltic website at www.deltic.com .
About Deltic
Deltic Timber Corporation is a natural resources company focused on the efficient and environmentally responsible management of its land holdings. The Company owns approximately 530,000 acres of timberland, operates two sawmills and a medium density fiber-board plant, and is engaged in real estate development.
