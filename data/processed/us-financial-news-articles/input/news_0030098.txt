January 20, 2018 / 11:53 AM / Updated 2 hours ago Two Americans, two Canadians freed after kidnapping in Nigeria - police Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
KADUNA, Nigeria (Reuters) - Two Americans and two Canadians have been freed after being kidnapped in the nothern Nigerian state of Kaduna, a police spokesman said on Saturday. 
Mukhtar Aliyu, a spokesman for Kaduna state police, said they were freed on Friday after being kidnapped on Wednesday. He said no ransom was paid. Reporting by Garba Muhammad; writing by Alexis Akwagyiram; Editing by Janet Lawrence
