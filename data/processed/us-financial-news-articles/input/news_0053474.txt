Billionaire industrialists and major Republican donors Charles and David Koch are launching a new venture capital firm to invest in disruptive technologies. 
The brothers' initiative is seen as a vehicle for challenging government regulations. 
The Kochs' network of political advocacy groups has been meeting in Palm Springs, California. On Saturday, the network pledged to spend up to $400 million on campaign contributions and policy initiatives during the run-up to the midterm congressional elections.
