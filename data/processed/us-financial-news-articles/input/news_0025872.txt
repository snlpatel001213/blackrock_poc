ROSELAND, N.J., Jan. 16, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The board of directors of Automatic Data Processing, Inc. (Nasdaq:ADP), has declared a regular quarterly dividend of 63 cents per share payable April 1, 2018 to shareholders of record on March 9, 2018.
