Myanmar prosecutor seeks charges against Reuters reporters Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:04 
Myanmar prosecutors seek charges against two Reuters reporters under the Official Secrets Act, which carries a maximum prison sentence of 14 years, the reporters’ lawyer says.
