In ‘Questions of Travel’ Elizabeth Bishop asks: “Is it lack of imagination that makes us come / to imagined places, not just stay at home?” Why travel in the first place? What centrifugal impulse makes us hungry for strangeness? All travel, from the cushiest package cruises to the most daring and dangerous explorations, inspires such questions.
Another pair of questions: What do readers expect from travel writing? How does this genre differ from others?
