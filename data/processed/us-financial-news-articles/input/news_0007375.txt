Last year was a great one for home builders. The S&P Homebuilders Select Industry Index was up 27.49% for 2017, compared with 17.74% for the S&P 500. Home builders got a boost from demand for new housing as well as a broad market rally, but many investors stayed out of the sector.
It may not be too late to give home builders a second look.
