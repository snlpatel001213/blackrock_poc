Never in the history of the sport has one golfer owned a course the way that Tiger Woods has owned Torrey Pines. The oceanfront, 36-hole course north of San Diego has been the site of eight of his 79 PGA Tour wins. It was the site of his last major championship, the 2008 U.S. Open. Even before he turned professional, Woods won his sixth Junior World title at Torrey Pines at the age of 15.
