The onetime Miami home of Basketball Hall of Famer Alonzo Mourning is going on the market for just under $12 million.

Mr. Mourning built the Coconut Grove, Fla., house in 1996, the year he signed a seven-year contract with the Miami Heat for more than $100 million. Mr. Mourning, who spent much of his 15-year NBA career with the Heat, later moved to a larger home in Coral Gables, selling the Coconut Grove house for $6 million in 2009.
