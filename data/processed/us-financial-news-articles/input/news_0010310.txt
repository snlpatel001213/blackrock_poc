Thousands race to become Japan's 'luckiest man in the new year'   01:15 
About 5,000 men and women made a chaotic dash through the courtyard of a Nishinomiya shrine in western Japan's Hyogo prefecture aiming to achieve the title of ''luckiest man of the new year.'' No reporter narration.
