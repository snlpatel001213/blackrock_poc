Petrobras to pay $2.95 bln in U.S. class action Wednesday, January 03, 2018 - 01:35 
Brazil's state-controlled oil company, Petroleo Brasileiro SA, agreed to pay $2.95 billion to settle a U.S. class action lawsuit brought by investors who claim they lost money as a result of a corruption scandal. Fred Katayama reports.
