GREENWICH, Conn., Jan. 29, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- FrontFour Capital Group LLC, together with its affiliates, a significant and long-term stockholder of ILG, Inc. ("ILG" or the "Company") (NASDAQ:ILG) today announced it has delivered a formal nomination of four highly qualified director candidates with extensive experience across the lodging, timeshare, gaming and real estate sectors for election to ILG's board of directors at the Company's upcoming 2018 annual meeting of stockholders.
About FrontFour Capital:
FrontFour Capital is an investment adviser based in Greenwich, CT. FrontFour focuses on value-oriented investments in North American companies.
