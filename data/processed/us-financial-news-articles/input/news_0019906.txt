LONDON, Jan 11 (Reuters) - British Prime Minister Theresa May said on Thursday the government was working for the best EU exit deal for the British people.
“What we are doing is working for the best deal for the British people for Brexit,” she told reporters. “I am confident that we will be able to achieve a good deal.” (Reporting by Guy Faulconbridge; editing by Stephen Addison)
