January 3, 2018 / 1:47 PM / Updated 9 minutes ago MOVES-BNY Mellon hires new asset servicing head for Northern Europe Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
Jan 3 (Reuters) - BNY Mellon on Wednesday said it appointed Dennis Presburg head of asset servicing business development for Northern Europe. 
Presburg, who previously worked at State Street, will report to Ileana Sodani in his new role. (Reporting by Laharee Chatterjee in Bengaluru)
