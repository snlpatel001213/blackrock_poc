In 1928 Billy Gawronski, the hero of Laurie Gwen Shapiro’s all-true boy’s adventure story, was a teenager on a mission—to join Cmdr. Richard Byrd’s expedition to Antarctica whatever the obstacles. They were formidable. Billy was only 17 with no skills to offer an expedition attracting huge interest and thousands of applicants.
