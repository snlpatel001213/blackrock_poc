In his op-ed “Republicans Can’t Avoid ObamaCare in 2018” (Jan. 3), Avik Roy advises Republicans to continue to focus on various mechanisms to pay for health-care services such as more competition in the insurance markets but offers no advice to reduce the cost of health care at the provider level. Mr. Roy thus contributes to the confusion over the cause of increasing costs, and it is precisely this confusion that Republicans, supposedly the experts on free markets, illogically perpetuate.
