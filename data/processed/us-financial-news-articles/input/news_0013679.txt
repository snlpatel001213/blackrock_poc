Jan 15 (Reuters) - Ningbo Shuanglin Auto Parts Co Ltd
* Says it sees net profit for FY 2017 to decrease by 30 percent to 45 percent, or to be 179.7 million yuan to 228.7 million yuan
* Says the net profit of FY 2016 was 326.8 million yuan
* Comments that increased price of rolled steel and costs of units as main reasons for the forecast
