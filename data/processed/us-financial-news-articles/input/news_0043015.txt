Woods gives thumbs up on eve of PGA Tour return 2:47am GMT - 01:17 
A relaxed and seemingly content Tiger Woods gave his body a thumbs up on Wednesday on the eve of his official PGA Tour comeback and could hardly contain his enthusiasm at being pain-free after years of searing pain in his back and legs. Rough Cut (no reporter narration).
