Wolff defends book, says 25th Amendment has "come up" inside White House 7:56pm GMT - 02:29 
In an interview with 'CBS This Morning' on Monday, Michael Wolff, the author of ''Fire and Fury: Inside the Trump White House'', said he spoke with President Donald Trump and that there were ''many moments'' when the the 25th Amendment, which allows the removal of the president if he or she is unable to carry out duties of the office, was brought up inside the White House.
