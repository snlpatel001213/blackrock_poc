January 15, 2018 / 11:06 AM / Updated an hour ago Brazil's GPA says sales rose 8.2 percent last year Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
SAO PAULO, Jan 15 (Reuters) - Brazilian retailer GPA SA said its total sales rose 8.2 percent last year, to 44.6 billion reais ($13.9 billion). 
In the fourth quarter, GPA, owned by French retailer Casino Guichard Perrachon SA, had net sales of 12.5 billion reais, 6.8 percent above the same period a year earlier. $1 = 3.2063 reais Reporting by Tatiana Bautzer; Editing by Mark Potter
