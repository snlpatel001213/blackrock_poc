Another week brings another civil war within Britain’s ruling Conservative Party, with Chancellor Philip Hammond again the target. Meanwhile, a new study of last year’s election debacle shows the Tories won’t easily keep Labour leader Jeremy Corbyn from becoming Prime Minister.
The latest infighting stems from internal government reports on the economic consequences of Brexit that will be presented to Prime Minister Theresa May’s cabinet this week. Leaks suggest they’ll be grim reading, although no details have slipped out.
