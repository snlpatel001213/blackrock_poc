Tom Petty died from accidental drug overdose -coroner 3:08am GMT - 00:50 
Rocker Tom Petty died in October due to ''multisystem organ failure'' brought on by an accidental overdose of seven medications, the Los Angeles County Medical Examiner's office said. Jane Ross reports.
