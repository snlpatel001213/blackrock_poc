January 30, 2018 / 12:18 PM / Updated 17 minutes ago Russia's Putin says Moscow wants to develop relations with the United States Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
MOSCOW, Jan 30 (Reuters) - Russian President Vladimir Putin said that Moscow wanted to develop relations with the United States instead of taking any retaliatory steps after the report on the people with ties to Putin was published on Tuesday. 
“We want to establish long-term and stable relations,” Putin said. (Reporting by Darya Korsunskaya; writing by Katya Golubkova)
