J. P. Morgan Chase 's Chairman and CEO Jamie Dimon will stay in that role for another five years, the bank said Monday, though it named two lieutenants to a co-president job. 
Daniel Pinto, 55, and Gordon Smith, 59, will start their new roles as co-president and co-chief operating officers on Tuesday, reporting to Dimon. They are, respectively, the CEOs of J. P. Morgan's corporate and investment bank and its consumer bank.
