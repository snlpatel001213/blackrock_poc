 45 AM / Updated 13 minutes ago Air France denies it made offer to buy Alitalia   
PARIS (Reuters) - Air France ( AIRF.PA ) on Saturday denied that it had made an offer to buy the struggling airline Alitalia, a day after Italy’s industry minister said the French flag carrier had made a joint bid with budget airline EasyJet ( EZJ.L ). “Air France-KLM denies having made an offer to buy Alitalia,” Air France said in a statement. Reporting by Richard Lough and Cyril Altmeyer
