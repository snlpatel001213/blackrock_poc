The private-equity industry is set to get a shot in the arm from the tax overhaul, a new analysis shows.
Though the new tax law contains provisions that are inimical to private-equity firms’ interests, the positives more than outweigh the negatives, according to Hamilton Lane Inc. The private-equity investor and advisory firm plans to present its conclusions to clients beginning Wednesday, in an article seen by The Wall Street Journal.
