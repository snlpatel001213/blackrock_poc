William J. McDonough 1934-2018 When William J. McDonough was 10, his mother died of a stroke while trimming the family’s Christmas tree on Chicago’s West Side. Within a year, his father died of a heart ailment, leaving young William and his three older sisters to fend for themselves, with help from relatives of modest means.
