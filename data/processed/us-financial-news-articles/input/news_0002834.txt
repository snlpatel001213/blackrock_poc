Oil at highest since 2015, as Iran spooks market 8:36am EST - 01:51 
Oil prices on Thursday hit their highest in more than two and a half years, touching levels not seen since before a slump in commodity markets in 2014/15, boosted by tensions in key producer Iran and by ongoing OPEC-led output cuts. Kate King reports.
