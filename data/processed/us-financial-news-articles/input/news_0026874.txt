OSLO, Jan 16 (Reuters) - The spot price for farmed salmon fell by 1.57 Norwegian crowns to an average of 53.99 Norwegian crowns ($6.85) per kilo last week, according to statistics from the Nasdaq Salmon Index.
The price is for producers and applies to freshly sliced salmon for delivery in Oslo. ($1 = 7.8816 Norwegian crowns) (Reporting by xxx, editing by xxx)
