Merkel drops climate target to secure deal 1:39pm EST - 01:11 
Germany's would-be coalition partners have pushed back an ambitious climate-change target in talks to form the next government. It could be an embarrassment to Merkel - an advocate of strict emissions cuts on the world stage - but she needs the deal after months of vacuum at the top.
