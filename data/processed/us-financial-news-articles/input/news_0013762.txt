Gold prices reversed earlier losses to close higher for a fourth straight session Tuesday, helped by declines in the dollar and U.S. stocks.
Front-month gold for January delivery closed up 0.1% to $1,335.40 a troy ounce on the Comex division of the New York Mercantile Exchange. Futures extended those gains slightly in after-hours trading.
