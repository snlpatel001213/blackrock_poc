Sen. Lindsey Graham, (R., S.C.), said Sunday that President Donald Trump should step up pressure on governments in Iran and North Korea, including through threats of military action, to thwart the two American adversaries’ efforts to develop nuclear arms.
Mr. Graham, who has sometimes been a critic of the president, said he believed Mr. Trump should deliver a national televised address threatening to withdraw from the Obama administration’s nuclear deal with Iran unless the terms are changed to be more favorable to the U.S.
