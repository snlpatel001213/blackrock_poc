CBS News has tapped John Dickerson to be a co-host of “CBS This Morning,” succeeding Charlie Rose, who was fired in November after allegations of sexual misconduct during his career as a TV journalist and talk show host.
Currently the anchor of the Washington-based Sunday political show “Face the Nation,” Mr. Dickerson will debut on the New York-based “CBS This Morning” on Wednesday. A successor to Mr. Dickerson on “Face the Nation” will be named in a few weeks, the network said.
