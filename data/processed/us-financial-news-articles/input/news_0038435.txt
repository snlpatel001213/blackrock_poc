Does any man really need a coat with 18 pockets? Nina-Maria Nitsche is about to find out. The new head of design at the Italian luxury menswear label Brioni showed her first collection this past Saturday during Paris Fashion Week. Among reptile-skinned boots and camel-hair overcoats that’ll set you back as much as a Toyota Camry was a reintroduction of Brioni’s travel jacket, first conceived as a bespoke piece in 1968. It’s a pack rat’s dream, with pockets layered inside of pockets like a wearable Matryoshka doll.
