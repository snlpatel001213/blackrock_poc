NEW YORK, Jan. 30, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Rowley Law PLLC is investigating potential claims against Callidus Software Inc. (NASDAQ: CALD) and its board of directors for breach of fiduciary duty concerning the proposed acquisition of the company by SAP SE. Stockholders will receive $36.00 for each share of Callidus Software Inc. that they hold. The transaction is valued at approximately $2.4 billion and is expected to close in the second quarter of 2018.
