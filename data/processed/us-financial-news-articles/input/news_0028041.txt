In the next few weeks, don’t fail to go over your paycheck withholding. You could avoid a bad surprise.
Recently the Treasury Department announced revisions to the 2018 withholding tables to reflect the massive tax changes Congress enacted late last year. These and other changes allow employers to use workers’ W-4 forms already on file to adjust their withholding to reflect tax cuts in the new law.
