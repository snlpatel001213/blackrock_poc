NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- The Board of Directors of Colgate-Palmolive Company (NYSE:CL) today declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.40 per common share, payable on February 15, 2018, to shareholders of record on January 23, 2018. The Company has paid uninterrupted dividends on its common stock since 1895.
