January 29, 2018 / 10:59 AM / Updated 8 hours ago Tech Mahindra third-quarter profit rises over 10 percent Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
(Reuters) - Indian software services exporter Tech Mahindra Ltd posted a 10.2 percent rise in third-quarter net profit, helped by a rise in active clients. 
Net profit rose to 9.43 billion rupees ($148.34 million), from 8.56 billion rupees a year ago. bit.ly/2rNOZJi 
Analysts on average had expected a profit of 7.90 billion rupees, according to Thomson Reuters data. 
Revenue from operations rose nearly 3 percent to 77.76 billion rupees. 
($1 = 63.5700 Indian rupees)
