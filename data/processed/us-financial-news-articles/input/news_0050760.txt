Incumbent Czech president wins a second term 5:45pm GMT - 01:08 
Incumbent Milos Zeman has won the Czech presidential election on Saturday (January 27), securing a second term. Anti-immigration, pro-Russian Zeman beat his pro-EU opponent Jiri Drahos in the close-run race. Saskia O'Donoghue reports
