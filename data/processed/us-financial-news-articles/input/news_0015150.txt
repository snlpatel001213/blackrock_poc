Elvis Express rock and rolls to Australian town of Parkes 9:12am GMT - 01:20 
Hundreds of hip-shaking and wig-wearing Elvis Presley and Priscilla lookalikes, and fans of the late King of Rock and Roll, take a train from Sydney, bound for Australia's annual Elvis festival in the town of Parkes. Samantha Vadas reports.
