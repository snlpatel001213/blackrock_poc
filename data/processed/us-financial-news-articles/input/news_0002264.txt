The president of Fidelity Investments’ equity division is moving to a new role within the fund giant’s personal investing business later this quarter, leaving a post he has held since 2009.
Brian Hogan most recently led equity and high-income investing within the asset-management unit at Boston-based Fidelity. In his new role, he will be head of investment solutions and innovation within the company’s personal-investing unit, a Fidelity spokesman confirmed.
