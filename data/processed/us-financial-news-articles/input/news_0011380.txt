About Trident Capital Cybersecurity
Trident Capital Cybersecurity (TCC) is a $300 million venture fund focused on investing in early stage and select growth equity cybersecurity companies. The firm is well known as the venture capital firm with peerless connections in cybersecurity. Its 47-person Cybersecurity Industry Advisory Council, including industry executives, serial entrepreneurs , customers and former government leaders, is often recognized for its insights, connections and go-to-market support of portfolio companies. TCC's current portfolio companies include 4iQ, Appthority, Attivo, Bayshore Networks, ID Experts, IronNet Cybersecurity, Prevoty and ReversingLabs. The team has been investing in cybersecurity since 1998 and made 34 cybersecurity investments throughout their investment careers.
