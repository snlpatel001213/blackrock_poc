Miami, Fla., Jan. 18, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Ocean Bank and Florida International University executives celebrated the naming of the university’s arena as the Ocean Bank Convocation Center. Pictured at the recent ceremony are Ocean Bank board member Juan del Busto, FIU president Mark Rosenberg, Ocean Bank president and CEO Agostinho Alfonso Macedo, FIU vice chairman Jorge Arrizurrieta and FIU athletic director Pete Garcia.
“It is truly a pleasure and an honor for Ocean Bank to continue our sponsorship of Florida International University. We look forward to the many events that will take place in this Convocation Center and to the positive spotlight they will bring to Ocean Bank,” Macedo said.
