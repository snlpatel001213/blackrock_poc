Pope to moms: Feel free to breastfeed in Sistine Chapel Monday, January 08, 2018 - 01:03 
Pope Francis baptized 33 infants in the Sistine Chapel on Sunday and told the mothers to feel free to breastfeed them if they cried or were hungry. Lisa Bernhard reports.
