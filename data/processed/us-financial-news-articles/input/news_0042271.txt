LAKE SUCCESS, N.Y., Jan. 24, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- The Hain Celestial Group, Inc. (Nasdaq: HAIN), a leading organic and natural products company, will conduct a conference call to discuss its Second Quarter Fiscal Year 2018 financial results on Wednesday, February 7, 2018 at 8:30 AM Eastern Time.
