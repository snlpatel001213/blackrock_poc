 36 PM / Updated 9 minutes ago Magnitude 5.8 earthquake hits Nicaragua - USGS Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
(Reuters) - An earthquake with a magnitude of 5.8 struck Nicaragua about 50 miles (80 km) southwest of Rivas on Tuesday, the United States Geological Survey said. 
An initial alert from the USGS put the magnitude at 6.0 but it revised it some minutes later. It said the tremor had a depth of 24.4 miles (40 km). Reporting by Reuters Americas Desk
