Lithuania liquor ad ban causes headaches 2:50pm GMT - 01:17 
Print distributors across Lithuania are being forced to rip pages out of the magazines they're trying to sell. The offending content: pictures of alcohol - now illegal after a liquor advertising ban was introduced here on the 1st January.
