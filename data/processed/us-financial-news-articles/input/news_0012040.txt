CBRE Group Inc. has hired a former senior executive at Zipcar Inc. to lead a new high-tech team that will customize apps for tenants and building owners to help them navigate the latest in workplace design and amenities.
Andrew Kupiec, who led North American operations for Zipcar, said the new app will enable office workers to do such things as locate colleagues, navigate buildings, reserve workspaces, learn about area events and order food and beverages.
