BURBANK, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- The Walt Disney Company (NYSE: DIS) will discuss fiscal first quarter 2018 financial results via a live audio webcast beginning at 4:30 p.m. EST / 1:30 p.m. PST on Tuesday, February 6, 2018.
Results will be released after the close of regular trading on February 6, 2018.
To listen to the webcast, please visit www.disney.com/investors . The webcast presentation will be archived.
