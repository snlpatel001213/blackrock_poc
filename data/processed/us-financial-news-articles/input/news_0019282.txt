STOCKHOLM, Jan 10 (Reuters) - Clothing sales in physical stores in Sweden, measured in comparable units and in current prices, fell 2.6 percent in 2017 from 2016, the Swedish Trade Federation’s Stilindex showed on Wednesday.
* In December alone, the year-on-year decline was 4.7 percent, the association said in a statement
