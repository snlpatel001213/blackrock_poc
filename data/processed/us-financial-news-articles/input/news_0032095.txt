January 18, 2018 / 9:10 PM / Updated 6 minutes ago CANADA STOCKS-TSX ends lower as resource stocks weigh Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
TORONTO, Jan 18 (Reuters) - Canada’s main stock index finished lower on Thursday, as energy companies and metals miners weighed. 
The Toronto Stock Exchange’s S&P/TSX composite index unofficially closed down 42.23 points, or 0.26 percent, at 16,284.47. (Reporting by Alastair Sharp; Editing by Alistair Bell)
