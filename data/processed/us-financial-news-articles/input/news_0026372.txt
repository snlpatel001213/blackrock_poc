Guangzhou Automobile Group Co. is planning to be the first Chinese auto maker to enter the U.S. market, but first it needs to ditch its “Trumpchi” brand name.
Guangzhou Auto aims to start selling vehicles to American buyers in 2019, but it has decided the Trumpchi name is too politically charged for the U.S. venture. In an interview Monday at the Detroit auto show, GAC executive Yu Jun said the company decided to pull the plug on the name even though it has been active in China for several years.
