Chelsea Manning eyes U.S. Senate seat for Maryland 1:11am GMT - 01:06 
Chelsea Manning, the former U.S. Army soldier who served seven years in military prison for leaking classified data, is seeking the Democratic Party's nomination for the U.S. Senate seat from Maryland, according to Federal election filings seen on Saturday. Jillian Kitchener has more.
