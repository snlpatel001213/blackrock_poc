Lower profit forecast for Carrefour but shares rise 10:10am EST - 01:20 
Carrefour shares rose on Thursday, with traders saying that stronger-than-expected fourth quarter sales from the French supermarket retailer had helped to offset the negative impact of a new profit forecast cut from Carrefour. Francis Maguire reports.
