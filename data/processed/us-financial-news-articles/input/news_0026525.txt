HOUSTON, Jan. 17, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Geo JS Tech Group (OTC: GJST) announced it has acquired Green Fence Recycling and Florence Development LLC. The purchase agreement includes recycling processing system in polyethylene terephthalate (PET) bottles and lithium batteries.
"This agreement allows us to immediately expand operations into recycling," explained Edward Mui, CEO of Geo JS Tech Group. "The processing system, customers and a management team with 25 years of experience in recycling processing will be a great jump start for our 2018 business goals."
This acquisition gives our company the ability to engage in buying, processing and selling ferrous and non-ferrous recycled material and it is expected to provide an anticipated 30 to 40 million dollars in increased revenues in the upcoming fiscal year, a 600% growth year over year.
