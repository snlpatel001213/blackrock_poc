GLENDALE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- The Board of Directors of Avery Dennison Corporation (NYSE:AVY) has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.45 per share. The dividend is payable March 21, 2018, to shareholders of record on March 7, 2018.
