Jan 29(Reuters) - Jolywood Suzhou Sunwatt Co Ltd
* Sees FY 2017 net profit to increase by 33.2 percent to 63.2 percent, or to be 220 million yuan to 269.5 million yuan
* Says FY 2016 net profit was 165.2 million yuan
* Says increased sales of back films and increased income from unit are the main reasons for the forecast
