January 17, 2018 / 2:38 PM / Updated 38 minutes ago MOVES-Debt collector Arrow Global names Dave Sutherland as COO Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
Jan 17 (Reuters) - Debt purchaser and manager Arrow Global Group PLC said on Wednesday it hired Dave Sutherland for the newly created role of chief operating officer. 
Sutherland joins from Nielson Financial Services, where he was the managing director. He has also worked with TD Wealth International, Santander UK, GE Money and Alliance Boots. Reporting by Ahmed Farhatha in Bengaluru
