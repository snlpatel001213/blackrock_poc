TUNIS (Reuters) - Tunisia will increase aid for poor families and needy people by 170 million dinars ($70.3 million), a minister said on Saturday after protests over austerity measures.
“This will concern about 250,000 families,” Mohamed Trabelsi, minister of social affairs, told reporters.
