CALGARY, Alberta, Jan. 15, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Granite Oil Corp. (“ Granite ”) (TSX:GXO) (OTCQX:GXOCF) will pay a dividend of 2.3 cents per common share in cash on February 15, 2018, to shareholders of record on January 31, 2018. This dividend has been designated as an eligible dividend for Canadian income tax purposes.
