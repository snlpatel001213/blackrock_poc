January 9, 2018 / 12:07 PM / Updated 28 minutes ago MOVES-Investcorp hires head for new credit funds unit in Europe Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
Jan 9 (Reuters) - Alternative investments and asset manager Investcorp named Phil Yeates managing director for its newly created European credit funds unit, under its larger credit management business. 
Yeates will be based in London and report to Jeremy Ghose, head of Investcorp Credit Management. (Reporting by Ahmed Farhatha in Bengaluru)
