In “Climate-Change Policies Can Be Punishing for the Poor” (op-ed, Jan. 5), Bjorn Lomborg is dead-on to be concerned about how carbon policies would affect the “energy poor.” So I find it puzzling that he chose to ignore carbon policies designed to address precisely this issue. Alert readers of this newspaper may recall recent op-eds from James Baker, George Shultz, Hank Paulson and others of the Republican “Climate Leadership Council” featuring a revenue-neutral carbon tax.
