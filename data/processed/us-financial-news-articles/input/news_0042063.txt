The American Stroke Association issued new stroke-treatment guidelines Wednesday that will extend to up to 24 hours the time in which stroke patients can be treated with a clot-removal procedure that lessens disability.
Previous guidance was that the thrombectomy could be used up to six hours after a stroke.
