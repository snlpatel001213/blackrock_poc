Jan 8(Reuters) - Zhejiang Kanglongda Special Protection Technology Co Ltd
* Sees FY 2017 net profit to decrease by 6 percent to 16 percent, or to be 79 million yuan to 88 million yuan
* Says FY 2016 net profit was 93.8 million yuan
* Says increased R&D costs, price rise in original auxiliary materials and exchange losses are the main reasons for the forecast
