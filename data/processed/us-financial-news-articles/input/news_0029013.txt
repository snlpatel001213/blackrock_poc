January 18, 2018 / 1:30 PM / in 10 minutes TransCanada says concluded open season for Keystone XL Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
Jan 18 (Reuters) - TransCanada Corp said on Thursday it had closed its open season for taking orders for the use of its Keystone XL pipeline, having secured about 500,000 barrels per day of firm, 20-year commitments. 
“Interest in the project remains strong and TransCanada will look to continue to secure additional long-term contracted volumes,” the company said in a statement. (Reporting by Nivedita Bhattacharjee)
