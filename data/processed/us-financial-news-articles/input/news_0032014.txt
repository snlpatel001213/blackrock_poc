NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- The Board of Directors of Arconic (NYSE: ARNC) today declared a dividend of 6 cents per share on the outstanding common stock of the Company. This dividend will be payable on February 25, 2018 to the holders of record of the common stock at the close of business on February 2, 2018.
