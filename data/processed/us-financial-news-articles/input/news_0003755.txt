January 4, 2018 / 8:21 PM / Updated 9 minutes ago BRIEF-HP Recalls Batteries For Notebook Computers, Mobile Workstations Due To Fire, Burn Hazards Reuters Staff 
Jan 4 (Reuters) - U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission: 
* HP RECALLS BATTERIES FOR NOTEBOOK COMPUTERS AND MOBILE WORKSTATIONS DUE TO FIRE AND BURN HAZARDS 
* SAYS RECALL INVOLVES ABOUT 50,000 LITHIUM-ION BATTERIES FOR HP NOTEBOOK COMPUTERS AND MOBILE WORKSTATIONS 
* SAYS ‍HP WILL PROVIDE FREE BATTERY REPLACEMENT SERVICES BY AN AUTHORIZED TECHNICIAN​ 
* SAYS REGARDING RECALL, HP RECEIVED 8 REPORTS OF BATTERY PACKS OVERHEATING, MELTING, OR CHARRING, INCLUDING 3 REPORTS OF PROPERTY DAMAGE TOTALING $4,500 Source text for Eikon: Further company coverage:
