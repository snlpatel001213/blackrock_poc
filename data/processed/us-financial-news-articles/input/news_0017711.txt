Congratulations to the Journal for providing facts on the consequences of legalization of marijuana in the states of Colorado, Alaska, Oregon and Washington (“Jeff Sessions’s Marijuana Candor,” Review & Outlook, Jan. 6). Attorney General Sessions took an oath of office to uphold the law and to support the Constitution of the U.S. His memo on marijuana does just that. Legalization of marijuana has led to dramatic increases in teenage drug use, increased crime, more (not less) illegal drug-cartel sales of marijuana and double the number of highway fatalities due to drivers using marijuana.
