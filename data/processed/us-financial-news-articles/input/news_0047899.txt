THE NEW YORK outpost of the Ace Hotel chain is known for its vibrant lobby scene—full of creative types working on screenplays or pitching an app in development. This fall, the hotel will gain a sibling called Sister City, located on the Bowery, on Manhattan’s Lower East Side. But whereas the Ace “is very much bringing the city into the lobby,” says Kelly Sawdon, Ace Hotel partner and chief brand officer, the 200-room Sister City is meant to “feel like a respite” from urban life.
