China's trade boom hits speed bump Friday, January 12, 2018 - 01:44 
China's exports and imports growth slowed in December after surging in the previous month, adding to signs of ebbing economic momentum as the government extends a crackdown on financial risks and factory pollution. Silvia Antonioli reports.
