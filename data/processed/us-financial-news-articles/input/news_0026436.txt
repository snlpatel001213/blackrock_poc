SAN ANTONIO--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Abraxas Petroleum Corporation (“Abraxas” or the “Company”) (NASDAQ:AXAS) will host its fourth quarter and year end 2017 earnings conference call on Wednesday, March 14, 2017 at 10 AM Central Time (11 AM Eastern Time). Abraxas plans to announce fourth quarter and year end 2017 operating and financial results prior to the call.
The conference call can be accessed by dialing 844.347.1028 and entering conference code 7399815. A live webcast of the conference call can be accessed under the “Investor Relations” portion of the Company’s website at www.abraxaspetroleum.com . If you are unable to participate in the live conference call, a replay will be available through April 11, 2018 and can be accessed by dialing 855.859.2056 and entering conference code 7399815.
Abraxas Petroleum Corporation is a San Antonio based crude oil and natural gas exploration and production company with operations across the Rocky Mountain, Permian Basin and South Texas regions of the United States.
