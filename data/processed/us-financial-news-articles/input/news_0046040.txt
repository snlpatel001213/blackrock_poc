KIEV, Jan 25 (Reuters) - Ukrainian Finance Minister Oleksandr Danylyuk on Thursday said he expected Ukraine would receive the long-delayed next tranche of loans under a $17.5 billion International Monetary Fund programme in May, news agency Interfax Ukraine reported.
On Wednesday, President Petro Poroshenko and IMF chief Christine Lagarde met for what they called constructive talks about the implementation of reforms mandated by the programme. (Reporting by Alessandra Prentice; Editing by Toby Chopra)
