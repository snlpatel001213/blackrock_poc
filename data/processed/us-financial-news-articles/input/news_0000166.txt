January 3, 2018 / 12:11 PM / Updated 22 minutes ago Ethiopia to release jailed politicians to 'foster national reconciliation' - PM Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
ADDIS ABABA (Reuters) - Ethiopia will release jailed politicians in bid to “foster national reconciliation”, Prime Minister Hailemariam Desalegn said on Wednesday.
