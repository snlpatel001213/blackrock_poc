January 17, 2018 / 7:40 AM / Updated 8 hours ago China says Canada meeting on North Korea showed 'Cold War' mentality Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
BEIJING (Reuters) - China said on Wednesday a just concluded meeting in Canada about North Korea showed a Cold War mentality and would only undermine a settlement of the North Korea issue. 
Foreign ministry spokesman Lu Kang made the comment at a daily news briefing. 
China did not attend the meeting in Vancouver, saying it would not help with efforts to resolve the North Korean nuclear issue. Reporting by Philip Wen; Writing by Ben Blanchard
