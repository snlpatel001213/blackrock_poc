January 14, 2018 / 5:03 PM / Updated 22 minutes ago Magnitude-5.1 quake hits 40 miles from Batangas, Philippines: USGS Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
(Reuters) - A magnitude-5.1 earthquake hit the Philippines on Sunday, about 40 miles west-southwest of Batangas, the U.S. Geological Survey said. 
The USGS said the tremor occurred at 1549 GMT and had a depth of 21.7 miles. Writing by Lisa Von Ahn; Editing by Jeffrey Benkoe
