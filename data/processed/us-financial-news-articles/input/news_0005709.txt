January 3, 2018 / 2:02 PM / Updated 16 minutes ago Quake hits off San Salvador coastline, no reports of damage Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
SAN SALVADOR (Reuters) - A strong magnitude 5.8 earthquake hit off of El Salvador’s coastline on Wednesday, though there was no threat of a tsunami, according to the nation’s Environment and Natural Resources Ministry. 
There were no initial reports of victims or damage to infrastructure. Reporting by Nelson Renteria
