FORT MYERS, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- LTC Global, Inc. today announced that Gene Arsenault has taken the role of Senior Vice President – Business Development and Carrier Partnerships. Mr. Arsenault joined LTC Global last January after serving as National Director of Long Term Care Insurance Sales for John Hancock for 12 years.

LTC Global also announced that Joel Allen has been promoted to Vice President – Business Development and Carrier Partnerships. Mr. Allen joined LTC Global in 2007 and has worked in several marketing and business development roles.
“LTC Global has significantly expanded the services it offers both to larger agencies and to insurance carriers,” said Daniel G. Schmedlen, Jr., chief executive officer of LTC Global. “Gene and Joel will work to expand our current relationships and forge new ones,” Schmedlen said.
About LTC Global
LTC Global is the leading provider of capital to insurance agents and agencies through commission advance programs and renewal commission purchases. LTC Global is also a national distributor of life and health insurance products through its licensed agency subsidiaries. For more information about LTC Global, visit www.ltcglobal.com .
