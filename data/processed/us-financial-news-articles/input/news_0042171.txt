Latin superstar Enrique Iglesias sued his former record company, Universal Music Group, accusing it of underpaying him by millions of dollars when it came to royalties generated by streaming services.
Mr. Iglesias signed with Universal in 1999 and in 2010 amended his contract, which set a royalty rate for sales of albums and song downloads but did not set a rate for streams of his music, according to the suit, filed Wednesday in a Miami federal court.
