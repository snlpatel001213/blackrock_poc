LONDON, Jan 24 (Reuters) - A technical issue is affecting data centres of the National Health Service in Wales, the devolved Welsh administration said on Wednesday, with media reports suggesting that doctors were struggling to access patient records.
“There is currently a technical issue affecting two NHS Wales Data Centres. This is under investigation by the NHS Wales Informatics Service and is being dealt with as a priority,” a spokesman for the Welsh government said. (Reporting by Alistair Smout; editing by Stephen Addison)
