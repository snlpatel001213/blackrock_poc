HARRISBURG, Pa.—A Republican congressman from Pennsylvania who settled a former aide’s sexual-harassment complaint with taxpayer money informed party and campaign officials Thursday that he won’t seek re-election, a decision that came as party officials had begun to search for a replacement candidate.
