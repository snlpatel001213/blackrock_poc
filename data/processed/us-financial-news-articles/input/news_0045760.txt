Jan 25(Reuters) - Airport Facilities Co Ltd
* Says it will repurchase up to 2.1 million shares of its common stock, representing 4.1 percent of outstanding on Jan. 26
* Share repurchase price is 675 yen per share, the closing price of Jan. 25, or up to 1.42 billion yen in total
* Says it will retire up to 2.1 million shares (repurchased on Jan. 26) on Feb. 14
