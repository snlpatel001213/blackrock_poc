PARIS (Reuters) - The European Central Bank must keep a close eye on the impact that swings in the exchange rates have on the inflation outlook, ECB governing council member Francois Villeroy de Galhau said on Friday.
“We have to monitor the effects of the exchange rates on the inflation outlook,” Villeroy said in an interview from Davos with Bloomberg TV.
