ON A CLEAR, warm night in December, roughly 50 paper lanterns by the late artist Isamu Noguchi adorned a private room at The Webster boutique in Miami. An animated crowd, including musicians A$AP Rocky and Ricky Martin, gathered for dinner to celebrate Dior Homme’s latest collection for its Black Carpet eveningwear line. The night capped off a milestone year for Kris Van Assche, the Belgian-born designer who has spent a decade at the helm of the French menswear label.
