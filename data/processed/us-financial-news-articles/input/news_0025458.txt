SINGAPORE, Jan 15 (Reuters) - Sale of private homes by developers in Singapore rose 17.4 percent in December from a year earlier, government data showed on Monday.
Developers sold 431 units last month, compared with 367 units in the same month a year earlier, data compiled by the Urban Redevelopment Authority showed.
Sales fell 45.3 percent from the revised 788 units sold in November.
