STOCKHOLM, Jan 19 (Reuters) - Deliveries of the six biggest categories of white goods (AHAM 6) in the United States rose 2.0 percent year-on-year in December, data from industry body Association of Home Appliance Manufacturers (AHAM) showed late on Thursday.
Shipments in the country, a major market for companies such as Whirlpool and Swedish home appliances maker Electrolux, rose 2.9 percent in 2017. (Reporting by Johannes Hellstrom, editing by Anna Ringstrom)
