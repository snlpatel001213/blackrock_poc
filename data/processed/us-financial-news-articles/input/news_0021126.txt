“Time to get a Chromebook.”
That’s what my wife told me a few weeks ago, after our 9-year-old daughter embarked on a journaling bender, putting page after page of thoughts and stories in Google Docs. Until now, our children had used our MacBooks when the need arose—but it seldom did. Now, the girl was reaching for a laptop every chance she got.
