Macron: 184 jet Airbus-China deal about to take off 12:16pm EST - 01:38 
French President Emmanuel Macron says a contract with China for 184 Airbus A320 narrow-body jets will be finalised soon and that his country also has ambitions to sell A350 and A380 planes to China in coming weeks or months. Sonia Legg reports
