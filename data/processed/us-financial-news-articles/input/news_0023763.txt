January 11, 2018 / 7:43 PM / Updated 22 minutes ago No announcement of any Trump Iran decision expected on Thursday Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
WASHINGTON (Reuters) - There is not expected to be an announcement on Thursday of any decision by U.S.   on whether to reimpose sanctions on Iran that were eased under the 2015 nuclear deal, a U.S. official said.
