MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russian Deputy Prime Minister Vitaly Mutko said on Wednesday that the sale of beer would be allowed at stadiums and fan zones during this year’s soccer World Cup, the Interfax news agency reported.
Russia will host the World Cup from June 14 to July 15 in 12 venues spread across 11 cities including Moscow, St Petersburg and Sochi.
