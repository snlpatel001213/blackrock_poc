OSLO, Jan 23 (Reuters) - The spot price for farmed salmon fell by 1.40 Norwegian crowns to an average of 52.59 Norwegian crowns ($6.69)per kilo last week, according to statistics from the Nasdaq Salmon Index.
The price is for producers and applies to freshly sliced salmon for delivery in Oslo.
