January 25, 2018 / 6:23 PM / in an hour Catalan parliament set to vote for regional leader on Jan. 30 Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
MADRID (Reuters) - The Catalan parliament will vote to choose a new regional leader on Jan. 30, the Catalan parliament said in a statement on Thursday. 
The only candidate for president of the northeastern Spanish region is sacked former leader Carles Puigdemont who is in self-imposed exile in Brussels after spearheading a drive for secession from Spain. Reporting By Sonya Dowsett; Editing by Jesus Aguado
