Euro zone: new data, old dilemma for ECB 9:13pm IST - 01:49 
Inflation in the euro zone slowed in December, vindicating the European Central Bank's decision to keep its policy easy despite growing pressure from Germany and other richer euro zone countries. Even so, as David Pollard reports, the new data comes in a week where the hawks have been sharpening their talons.
