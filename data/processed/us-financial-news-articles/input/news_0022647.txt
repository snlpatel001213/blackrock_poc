January 16, 2018 / 7:17 AM / Updated 24 minutes ago Homewares retailer Dunelm second quarter comparable sales rise 3.4 percent Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
(Reuters) - British homewares retailer Dunelm Group Plc ( DNLM.L ) said on Tuesday its comparable sales rose 3.4 percent in the second quarter, helped by higher online sales. 
The company said revenue rose 13.6 percent to 297.5 million pounds in its second quarter ended Dec. 30. 
Dunelm, which sells cushions, bedding and kitchen equipment, said it expects margin improvement in the second half. Reporting by Radhika Rukmangadhan in Bengaluru; Editing by Bernard Orr
