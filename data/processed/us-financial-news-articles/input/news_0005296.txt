It was Christmastime, but Tom Hudson was in the Halloween spirit. Heading to a holiday party last month, the 25-year-old biomedical researcher in Sioux Falls, S.D., threw on a simple black turtleneck from Banana Republic. With his glasses factored in, he was the spitting image of a certain tech-world colossus. “I was telling people I went as Steve Jobs for Christmas this year,” said Mr. Hudson. “It was kind of a joke, but I think I pulled it off.” His friends thought so too: By night’s end, they were telling him how much they loved his Jobs costume.
