TORONTO—The deaths last month of Barry Sherman, the billionaire founder of generic drugmaker Apotex Inc., and his wife are being investigated as a double homicide, Toronto Police revealed Friday in the latest twist to a mystery that has drawn wide interest throughout Canada and beyond.
The lead investigator said Friday that police “haven’t developed any suspects” yet but have concluded the couple was “targeted” before they were found strangled in their north Toronto home last month.
