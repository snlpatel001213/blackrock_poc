VALLEY FORGE, Pa.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- UGI Corporation (NYSE: UGI ) will announce the results of its first fiscal quarter after the market closes on January 31. The company will hold a live internet audio webcast of its conference call to discuss results and other current activities at 9:00 AM EST on Thursday, February 1.
Interested parties may listen to the audio webcast both live and in replay on the Internet at http://www.ugicorp.com/investor-relations/events-and-presentations/default.aspx or by visiting the company website http://www.ugicorp.com and clicking on Investor Relations.
A telephonic replay will be available from 12:00 PM EST on February 1 through 11:59 PM EST February 8. The replay may be accessed toll free at 855-859-2056 and internationally at 404-537-3406, conference ID 2603134.
About UGI
UGI is a distributor and marketer of energy products and services. Through subsidiaries, UGI operates natural gas and electric utilities in Pennsylvania, distributes propane both domestically and internationally, manages midstream energy and electric generation assets in Pennsylvania, and engages in energy marketing in ten states, the District of Columbia and internationally in France, Belgium and the UK. UGI, through subsidiaries, is the sole General Partner and owns 26% of AmeriGas Partners, L.P. (NYSE:APU), the nation's largest retail propane distributor.
Comprehensive information about UGI Corporation is available on the Internet at http://www.ugicorp.com .
