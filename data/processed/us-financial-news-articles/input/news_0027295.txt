BALTIMORE—Baltimore’s mayor on Friday fired the city’s police commissioner after he failed to reduce the city’s high homicide rate, saying the city’s troubled police department needs new leadership.
Mayor Catherine Pugh said in dismissing Kevin Davis that she is impatient at the continuing violence, which she has described as “out of control.” Under Mr. Davis, who took over the department in 2015, homicides in the city rose, reaching 343 last year, the highest on record on a per capita basis.
