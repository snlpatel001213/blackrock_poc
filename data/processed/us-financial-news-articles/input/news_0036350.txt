MONACO, Jan. 23, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Navios Maritime Midstream Partners L.P. ("Navios Midstream") (NYSE:NAP), announced today that its Board of Directors has declared a cash distribution of $0.4225 per unit for the quarter ended December 31, 2017. This distribution represents an annualized distribution of $1.69 per unit.
The cash distribution will be payable on February 14, 2018 to unit holders of record as of February 9, 2018.
About Navios Maritime Midstream Partners L.P.
Navios Maritime Midstream Partners L.P. is a publicly traded master limited partnership which owns and operates crude oil tankers under long-term employment contracts. For more information, please visit our website at www.navios-midstream.com .
