WASHINGTON—President Donald Trump backed House Republicans’ plans to explore whether to bring back some form of earmarks, a move that could boost deal making in Congress but risks a backlash from conservative forces.
The comments came as the House Rules Committee plans to hold hearings later this month on whether to restore lawmakers’ ability to direct federal funding for specific projects, known commonly as earmarks.
