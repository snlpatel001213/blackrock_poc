Trump declares major disaster in California after wildfires Wednesday, January 03, 2018 - 00:36 
U.S. Secretary of Homeland Security Kirstjen Nielsen said on Tuesday that President Trump has declared a major diaster for two southern California counties that are recovering from the Thomas Fire, which is the largest recorded fire in the state. Rough Cut (no reporter narration).
