The Senate Banking Committee is expected to meet Jan. 23 to consider the nomination of Jelena McWilliams to lead the Federal Deposit Insurance Corp., according to a person familiar with the matter.
If she is confirmed by the full Senate, the Trump administration would move closer to rounding out a team that can enact its promise to revisit financial regulations adopted under President Barack Obama. Ms. McWilliams, the chief legal officer of Cincinnati-based Fifth Third Bancorp, is among the final major pieces of that team.
