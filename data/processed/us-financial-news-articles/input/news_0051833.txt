WestRock Co. has agreed to buy KapStone Paper and Packaging Corp. for about $3.5 billion, looking to gain scale as online shopping helps lift demand for packaging products.
The deal has a so-called enterprise value of about $4.9 billion, which includes $1.36 billion of assumed debt, the companies said Monday.
