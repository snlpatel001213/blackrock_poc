MOSCOW, Jan 24 (Reuters) - Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov discussed preparations for a Syria peace congress in the Russian city of Sochi on Jan. 29-30 with Turkey’s foreign minister in a phone call on Wednesday, the Russian foreign ministry said in statement.
“Additional joint steps” in light of preparations for the event have been discussed, it said.
