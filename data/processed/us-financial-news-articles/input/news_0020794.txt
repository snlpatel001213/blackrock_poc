"Shithole" projected on Trump's D.C. hotel 3:41am IST - 01:10 
Activist and artist Robin Bell projected the word 'shithole' and other slogans on the Trump International Hotel in Washington, D.C., after the president is alleged to have used the word in reference to African nations. Jillian Kitchener reports.
