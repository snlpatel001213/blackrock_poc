HICKORY, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- CommScope Holding Company, Inc. plans to release its fourth quarter 2017 financial results on Thursday, February 15, before the market opens. The release will be followed by an 8:30 a.m. ET conference call in which management will discuss fourth quarter results.
To participate in the conference call, dial 844-397-6169 (US and Canada only) or +1 478-219-0508, approximately 15 minutes before the start of the call to facilitate a timely connection. The conference identification number is 9657389.
The live, listen-only audio of the call will be available through a link on the Investor Relations Events and Presentations page of CommScope’s Investor Relations website .
A webcast replay will be archived on CommScope’s website for a limited period of time following the conference call.
