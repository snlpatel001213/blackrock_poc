MILWAUKEE--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Rockwell Automation, Inc. (NYSE: ROK) is scheduled to report its fiscal 2018 first quarter results on Wednesday, January 24, before the market opens. The release will be posted on the Rockwell Automation website at www.rockwellautomation.com .
A conference call to discuss the quarterly results will be held at 8:30 a.m. Eastern Standard Time on January 24. This call will be audio webcast and accessible on the Rockwell Automation website. Presentation materials will also be available on the website prior to the call.
Both the presentation materials and a replay of the call will be available on the Rockwell Automation website through February 24, 2018.
