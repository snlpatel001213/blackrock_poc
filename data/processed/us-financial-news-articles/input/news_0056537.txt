The U.S. dollar edged lower Tuesday as investors opted to put money into regions with more growth potential.
The Wall Street Journal Dollar Index, which measures the currency against a basket of 16 others, dropped 0.1%, to 83.27. The move included declines of 0.2% against the euro and the yen, and a loss of 0.5% versus the British pound.
