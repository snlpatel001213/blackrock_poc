Jan 11 (Reuters) - United Super Markets Holdings Inc :
* Says it repurchased 371,400 shares of its common stock for 432.6 million yen in total from Dec. 1, 2017 to Dec. 31, 2017
* Says this was part of the share repurchase plan announced on April 11, 2017
* Says it accumulatively repurchased 3.3 million shares for 3.62 billion yen in total as of Dec. 31, 2017
