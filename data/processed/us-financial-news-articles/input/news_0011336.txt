January 16, 2018 / 11:21 AM / Updated 32 minutes ago Russian, Iranian foreign ministers discuss Syria and Iran nuclear deal Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov and his Iranian counterpart Mohammad Javad Zarif discussed the conflict in Syria and the Iran nuclear deal in a phone conversation, the Russian Foreign Ministry said on Tuesday. FILE PHOTO - Iranian Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif and his Russian counterpart Sergei Lavrov enter a hall during a meeting in Moscow, Russia January 10, 2018. REUTERSAlexander Zemlianichenko/Pool 
“The two sides exchanged views on a range of international issues, including the Syrian peace process in the context of the Syrian Congress of National Dialogue in Sochi, as well as the situation surrounding the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action on Iran’s nuclear programme,” Russia’s foreign ministry said.
