Venezuela supermarkets besieged after price cuts 10:54am EST - 01:11 
Scores of Venezuelans gathered outside some Caracas supermarkets at the weekend after President Nicolas Maduro's government forced shops to slash prices, the latest unrest in the country's food shortage crisis. Ciara Lee reports.
