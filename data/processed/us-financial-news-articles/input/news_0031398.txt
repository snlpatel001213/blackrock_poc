In the advertising and marketing-services industry, the word of 2017 was “duopoly”—shorthand for Google and Facebook, and their dominance of the digital media market. The word of 2018 may well be “Amazon.”
There are two reasons for this. The first is the leading role played by Amazon in disrupting retailers and brand owners. The second is Amazon’s ambitions to become a leading search and advertising platform itself.
