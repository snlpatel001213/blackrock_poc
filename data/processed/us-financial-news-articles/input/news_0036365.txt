ZURICH—UBS Group AG has shuffled the top ranks of its wealth-management division, as the world’s largest wealth manager continues to revamp a unit that is expected to be a key driver of profits but faces intense competition.
Christine Novakovic was tapped to head wealth management in Europe, Middle East and Africa, according to a memo send to UBS employees on Monday.
