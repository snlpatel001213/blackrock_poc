January 26, 2018 / 9:48 AM / Updated 12 minutes ago Trump tells Russian news agency he hopes for dialogue with Moscow - TASS Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
MOSCOW (Reuters) - U.S. President Donald Trump said on Friday he hoped for dialogue with Moscow, Russia’s TASS news agency cited him as saying. 
TASS said Trump had made the comment at the World Economic Forum in Davos, Switzerland. 
“We hope for it,” TASS cited Trump as saying in answer to a question. Reporting by Denis Pinchuk; Writing by Maria Tsvetkova; Editing by Andrew Osborn
