January 29, 2018 / 5:53 PM / Updated an hour ago Hedge fund Elliott increases stake in Fox takeover target Sky Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
LONDON (Reuters) - U.S. hedge fund Elliott, which often wages activist campaigns at companies, has lifted its stake in British pay-television broadcaster Sky SKYB.l to 1.29 percent, a stock exchange filing issued on Monday shows. 
It comes as Twenty-First Century Fox attempts to acquire Sky for 11.7 billion-pounds. Elliott first disclosed a 1.09 percent stake in Sky through derivatives on Friday. Reporting by Ben Martin; Editing by Edmund Blair
