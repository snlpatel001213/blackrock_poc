The Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas usually produces its share of wide eyes and gaping mouths. This time around, the excitement is being generated by suitcases that have minds of their own. Joanna Stern of the Journal fearlessly took on these “autonomous carry-on bags”—self-driving suitcases that will follow you around the airport and, presumably, anywhere else.
