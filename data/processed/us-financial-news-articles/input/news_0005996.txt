Reuel Marc Gerecht’s “In the Mideast, Trump Gives Reality a Chance” (op-ed, Dec. 27) should have included more history. The British were concerned about the potential for Arab assistance to rising threats from Nazi Germany. In 1939 Britain called a meeting known as the London Conference. Arab leaders, including Haj Amin al-Husseini, were invited (as well as Zionist leaders) to finalize a British offer concerning Palestine.
