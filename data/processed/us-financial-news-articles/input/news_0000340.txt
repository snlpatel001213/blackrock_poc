Delta accused of bias against Jewish, Israeli flight attendants reuters.com • Delta accused of bias against Jewish, Israeli flight attendants More (Reuters) - Delta Air Lines Inc on Tuesday was hit with a lawsuit by current and former New York-based flight attendants who say managers discriminated against them because they are Jewish or Israeli. The four plaintiffs in the lawsuit filed in federal court in Brooklyn say they were harassed, denied promotions, or unfairly disciplined because of their religion or nationality.
