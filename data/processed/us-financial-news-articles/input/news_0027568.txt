Police bust huge 'pineapple' cocaine ring in Lisbon 3:23pm IST - 01:02 
Police in Portugal and Spain said they seized hundreds of kilograms of cocaine hidden inside fresh pineapples, and arrested nine members of a gang that had transported the drug from South America. Rosanna Philpott reports.
