Jan 23 (Reuters) - Suzhou TFC Optical Communication Co Ltd
* Says it sees net profit for FY 2017 to decrease by 5 percent to 15 percent, or to be 103.3 million yuan to 115.5 million yuan
* Says the net profit of FY 2016 was 121.6 million yuan
* Comments that decreased products price and increased new products investment as main reasons for the forecast
