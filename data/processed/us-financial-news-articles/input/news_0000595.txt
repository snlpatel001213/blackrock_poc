Saudi: fuel hikes and now VAT in bid for revenues 11:32am EST - 01:54 
Saudi Arabia and the UAE introduce VAT on most goods and services as both seek new revenues after years of low oil prices. As David Pollard reports, the move is a first for Gulf states, and comes after a sharp hike in Saudi fuel prices.
