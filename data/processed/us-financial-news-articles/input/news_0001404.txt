New York City plans to close one of the jails on Rikers Island this summer, a move city officials called a major step toward shuttering the troubled complex.
The jail, called the George Motchan Detention Center, houses about 580 men awaiting trial, city officials said Tuesday. One of nine jails on the island, it is the first that officials have said they would permanently close as part of a plan to no longer hold city inmates on the island.
