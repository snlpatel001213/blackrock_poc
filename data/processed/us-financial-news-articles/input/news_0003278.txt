WASHINGTON—Federal Reserve officials in December debated whether new tax cuts would require them to raise short-term interest rates faster this year, after lifting them three times last year.
Officials expressed growing confidence in the strength of the labor market and the economy more broadly, according to minutes of the Fed’s Dec. 12-13 policy meeting, which were released Wednesday.
