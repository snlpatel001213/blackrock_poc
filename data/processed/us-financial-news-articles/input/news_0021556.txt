MAZAR-E-SHARIF, Afghanistan—Atta Mohammad Noor, the ousted governor of Afghanistan’s northern Balkh province, is daring President Ashraf Ghani to come after him.
Last month, Mr. Ghani shocked Mr. Atta—and the rest of Afghanistan—by accepting an undated resignation letter the governor had submitted 18 months earlier, at the start of negotiations over a new coalition government.
