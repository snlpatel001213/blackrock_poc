January 10, 2018 / 4:25 PM / in 32 minutes Republican U.S. Representative Issa of California to retire: statement Reuters Staff 1   U.S. Representative Darrell Issa, who as head of the House of Representatives’ Oversight Committee pursued the investigation of American deaths in Benghazi, Libya, said on Wednesday he would not seek re-election in November. 
“Throughout my service, I worked hard and never lost sight of the people our government is supposed to serve. Yet with the support of my family, I have decided that I will not seek re-election in California’s 49th District,” he said in a statement.Issa is from one of the 23 congressional districts won by Democrat Hillary Clinton in the 2016 presidential election that are currently held by Republicans, who are now considered vulnerable in November congressional elections.
