VANCOUVER, British Columbia—Senior diplomatic officials from 20 countries met to shore up implementation of crippling sanctions targeting North Korea’s access to goods and oil as well as to consider maritime interdiction of goods flowing to the country.
Tuesday’s meeting in Vancouver, convened by the U.S. and Canada, came as talks continued between Seoul and Pyongyang over North Korea’s participation in the coming Winter Olympics, in South Korea.
