Jan 11 (Reuters) - India's mobile phone customer base in November fell 1.33 percent, or a net 15.73 million, to 1.16 billion, data released by the country's telecoms regulator showed on Thursday. Following is a table of mobile phone subscriptions in India, the world's second-biggest wireless market by number of users, as of Nov. 30.
