ATLANTA, Jan. 05, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Supply Chain Commerce Solutions leader Manhattan Associates, Inc. (NASDAQ:MANH) today announced it will release its fourth quarter 2017 financial results on Tuesday, February 6, 2018, after the stock market closes. The press release will be followed by a conference call hosted by Manhattan Associates' senior management at 4:30 p.m. EST, also on February 6, 2018. Investors are invited to listen to a live webcast of the conference call through the investor relations section of the Manhattan Associates website at www.manh.com .
To listen to the live webcast, please go to the website at least 15 minutes before the call to download and install any necessary audio software. For those who cannot listen to the live broadcast, a replay can be accessed shortly after the call by dialing +1-855-859-2056 in the U.S. and Canada, or +1-404-537-3406 outside the U.S., and entering the conference identification number 3099957 or via the web at www.manh.com . The phone replay will be available for two weeks after the call, and the internet broadcast will be available until Manhattan Associates' first quarter 2018 earnings release.
