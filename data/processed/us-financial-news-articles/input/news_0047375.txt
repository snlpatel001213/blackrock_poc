Jan 26 (Reuters) - Pemberton, the asset management group backed by Legal and General, named Phil Ashdown senior portfolio manager. 
Ashdown was a founding investor and executive board director of Tungsten Corp, a global supply chain enabler. (Reporting by Anirban Paul in Bengaluru; Editing by Biju Dwarakanath)
