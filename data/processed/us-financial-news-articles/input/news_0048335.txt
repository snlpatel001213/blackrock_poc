MINNEAPOLIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Piper Jaffray Companies (NYSE: PJC) announced today that it will release its 2017 fourth quarter and full-year financial results prior to the opening of the market on Thursday, February 1, 2018. The company will also hold a conference call to review the financial results at 9 a.m. EST (8 a.m. CST) that same day. Chad Abraham, chief executive officer; Debbra Schoneman, president; and Tim Carter, chief financial officer, will host the call.
The earnings release will be available on Feb. 1, 2018 at the firm’s website at www.piperjaffray.com . The call can be accessed via webcast or by dialing 888-810-0209 (domestic) or 706-902-1361 (international) and referencing reservation #9599039. Callers should dial in at least 15 minutes prior to the call time. A replay of the conference call will be available beginning approximately at noon EST on Feb. 1, 2018 at the same web address or by calling 855-859-2056 and referencing reservation #9599039.
