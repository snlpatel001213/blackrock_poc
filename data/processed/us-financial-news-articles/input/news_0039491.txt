PLEASANTON, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Ellie Mae ® (NYSE:ELLI), the leading cloud-based platform provider for the mortgage finance industry, today announced that it will release financial results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year 2017 ended December 31, 2017 following the close of the market on Thursday, February 8, 2018. The Company will host a corresponding conference call and live webcast at 4:30 p.m. Eastern Time on that day.
Investors may listen to the live conference call (ID 3311197) by dialing 877-723-9502 or 719-325-4835 at 4:30 p.m. Eastern Time on February 8, 2018. An audio replay of the call will be available through February 22, 2018 by dialing 888-203-1112 or 719-457-0820 and entering access code 3311197.
A live webcast of the call will be available on the Investor Relations section of the Company’s website at http://investor.elliemae.com .
Disclosure Information
Ellie Mae uses the investor relations section on its website as means of complying with its disclosure obligations under Regulation FD. Accordingly, investors should monitor Ellie Mae’s investor relations website in addition to following Ellie Mae’s press releases, SEC filings and public conference calls and webcasts.
