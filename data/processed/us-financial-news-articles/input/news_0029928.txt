Jan 18 (Reuters) - GL Pharm Tech Corp :
* Says it will issue 3rd series registered and unsecured private convertible bonds, to raise 2 billion won in proceeds for operations
* Maturity date is Jan. 16, 2025, coupon rate is 2.5 percent and yield to maturity is 2.5 percent for the bonds
* Conversion price is 4,955 won/share and conversion ratio is 100 percent
