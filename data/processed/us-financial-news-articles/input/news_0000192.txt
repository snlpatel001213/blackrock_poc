Trump lawyers demand Bannon 'cease and desist' 6:40pm IST - 01:38 
Lawyers for the president sent a cease and desist letter to Steve Bannon on Wednesday. They said in the letter, seen by Reuters, Bannon had breached an agreement by communicating with author Wolff about Trump, his family members and the campaign and made disparaging remarks about Trump and his family.
