(Reuters) - Wynn Resorts Ltd ( WYNN.O ) Chief Executive Steve Wynn said on Friday that accusations of sexual misconduct made against him that were reported by the Wall Street Journal were “preposterous.”
“The idea that I ever assaulted any woman is preposterous,” Steve Wynn said in a statement emailed to Reuters.
Wynn Resorts shares were down about 8.5 percent to $183.56 in afternoon trade.
Reporting by Pranav Kiran in Bengaluru
