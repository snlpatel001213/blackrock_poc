WASHINGTON—Rep. Gregg Harper, a Mississippi Republican and chairman of the House Administration Committee, will retire at the end of the year and won’t seek a sixth term. He is the 27th House Republican to announce his retirement during this Congress.
Mr. Harper, 61, cited a desire to leave Congress to be with his family, including an adult son who has special needs and his daughter who is expecting her first child.
