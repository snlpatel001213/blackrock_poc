* ORATION REPORTS SOLID FISCAL 2018 SECOND QUARTER RESULTS WITH STRONG REVENUE GROWTH

* SEES FISCAL 2018 REVENUE IN A RANGE OF $6.08 BILLION - 6.14 BILLION, UP 3 - 4% FROM FISCAL 2017
