FORT SMITH, Ark., Jan. 26, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- The Board of Directors of ArcBest ® (Nasdaq: ARCB) has declared a quarterly cash dividend of eight cents ($0.08) per share to holders of record of its Common Stock, $0.01 par value, on February 9, 2018, payable on February 28, 2018.
