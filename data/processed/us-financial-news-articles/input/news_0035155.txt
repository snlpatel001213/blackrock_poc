PLANO, Texas, Jan. 22, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Dr Pepper Snapple Group, Inc., (NYSE: DPS) will issue its fourth quarter and full year 2017 financial results on Wednesday, February 14, before the market opens.
Larry Young, DPS president and CEO, and Marty Ellen, chief financial officer, will discuss the results during an earnings conference call for financial analysts and investors at 9 a.m. CST that day. The conference call and slide presentation will be accessible live through the DPS website at http://www.drpeppersnapple.com and will be archived for replay for a period of 14 days.
About Dr Pepper Snapple Group
