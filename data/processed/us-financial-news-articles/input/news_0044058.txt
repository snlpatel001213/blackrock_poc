YANGON, Myanmar—Bill Richardson, a former U.S. ambassador to the United Nations, has resigned from an international advisory panel on the Rohingya refugee crisis, calling it a “whitewash and a cheerleading operation” for Myanmar leader Aung San Suu Kyi.
