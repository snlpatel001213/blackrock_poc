SAN DIEGO (AP) _ Illumina Inc. (ILMN) on Tuesday reported fourth-quarter earnings of $68 million.

The results surpassed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of 11 analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $1.22 per share.

Illumina expects full-year earnings in the range of $4.50 to $4.60 per share.
