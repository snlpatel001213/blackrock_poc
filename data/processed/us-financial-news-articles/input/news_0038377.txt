January 22, 2018 / 9:27 AM / Updated 2 hours ago Brexit campaigner Farage says doubts return to politics with new party Reuters Staff 1   Brexit campaigner Nigel Farage said on Monday that he was not thinking of creating a new party and doubted that he would return to frontline British politics. 
When asked whether he was thinking of creating a new political party, he said: “No thoughts of a new party whatever some may speculate.” 
When asked if he would return to frontline politics, he said: “Doubt it.” 
Farage declined to comment on the chaos in his former party, UKIP. [nL8N1PH1BX] Reporting by Guy Faulconbridge; editing by Costas Pitas
