Powerball and Mega Millions jackpots top $1 billion Friday, January 05, 2018 - 00:58 
Lottery players on Friday and Saturday night will get two shots at record Powerball and Mega Millions jackpots that together top $1 billion after going months without a winner. Linda So reports.
