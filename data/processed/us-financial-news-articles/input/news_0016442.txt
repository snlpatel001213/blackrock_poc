FREMONT, Calif. (AP) _ Synnex Corp. (SNX) on Tuesday reported fiscal fourth-quarter earnings of $91.1 million.
On a per-share basis, the Fremont, California-based company said it had profit of $2.26. Earnings, adjusted for one-time gains and costs, came to $2.79 per share.
The results topped Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of three analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $2.69 per share.
The high-tech contractor posted revenue of $5.31 billion in the period, also topping Street forecasts. Three analysts surveyed by Zacks expected $4.87 billion.
For the year, the company reported profit of $301.2 million, or $7.51 per share. Revenue was reported as $17.05 billion.
For the current quarter ending in March, Synnex expects its per-share earnings to range from $2.06 to $2.15.
The company said it expects revenue in the range of $4.35 billion to $4.55 billion for the fiscal first quarter.
Synnex shares have risen 3.5 percent since the beginning of the year. In the final minutes of trading on Tuesday, shares hit $140.73, a rise of 17 percent in the last 12 months.
