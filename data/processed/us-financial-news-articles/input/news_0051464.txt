DES MOINES, Iowa, Meredith Corporation (NYSE: MDP; www.meredith.com ), the leading media and marketing company serving more than 110 million unduplicated American women and more than 70 percent of U.S. Millennial women, will report fiscal 2018 second quarter and first half results on Wednesday, January 31, 2018 after the close of the New York Stock Exchange. A conference call will be held later that day at 4:30 PM EST to discuss the results. To listen, visit Meredith Corporation's Investor Relations section at www.meredith.com prior to the start of the call. An archive version of the formal remarks and the audio portion of the webcast will be available later that day on Meredith's website.
Meredith Corporation has been committed to service journalism for more than 115 years. Today, Meredith uses multiple distribution platforms - including broadcast television, print, digital, mobile and video - to provide consumers with content they desire and to deliver the messages of its advertising and marketing partners.
