Brookfield Business Partners LP and institutional partners have reached a deal to buy troubled nuclear contractor Westinghouse Electric Co. for $4.6 billion.
Westinghouse has been reeling from the fallout from customers and investors after it filed for chapter 11 protection in March, calling the fate of nuclear projects around the U.S. into question. The company was driven to bankruptcy from billions of dollars in cost overruns at construction sites, which led to mounting losses for its Japanese parent Toshiba.
