Former New Jersey governor to make TV debut Tuesday Reuters Chris Christie was a key Trump ally during the campaign. 
By Kate King 
ABC News has hired former New Jersey Gov. Chris Christie as a contributor, people familiar with the matter said Monday. 
One of the people said Christie will make his debut Tuesday on “Good Morning America.” He also will participate in the network’s coverage of President Donald Trump’s   Tuesday night. 
Christie will join Meghan McCain, a political commentator, and Alex Castellanos, a Republican consultant and pollster, as conservative contributors recently hired by ABC News. 
A two-term Republican governor, Christie left office Jan. 16 and was succeeded by Phil Murphy, a Democrat. The news of Christie’s new job was first reported by NJ Advance Media.
