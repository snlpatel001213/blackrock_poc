NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Lehman Brothers Holdings Inc. (“LBHI”), as Plan Administrator pursuant to its confirmed chapter 11 plan for the wind down of its controlled affiliates (“Lehman”), announced today that certain of its controlled affiliates intend to explore monetization opportunities related to allowed claims they hold against LBHI and their non-controlled foreign affiliate Lehman Brothers Treasury Co. B.V. There is no assurance that Lehman will proceed with any such opportunities.
