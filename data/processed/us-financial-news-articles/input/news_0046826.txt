SANTA CLARA, Calif., Jan. 25, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- NVIDIA will host a conference call on Thursday, Feb. 8, at 2 p.m. PT (5 p.m. ET) to discuss its financial results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year 2018, ending January 28, 2018.
The call will be webcast live (in listen-only mode) at the following websites: www.nvidia.com and www.streetevents.com . The company’s prepared remarks will be followed by a question and answer session, which will be limited to questions from financial analysts and institutional investors.
Ahead of the call, NVIDIA will provide written commentary on its fourth-quarter results from its CFO. This material will be posted to investor.nvidia.com immediately after the company’s results are publicly announced at approximately 1:20 p.m. PT.
To listen to the conference call, dial (877) 223-3864 or, for those outside the United States, (574) 990-1377, conference ID 6265639.
A replay of the conference call will be available until Feb. 15, 2018, at (855) 859-2056, conference ID 6265639. The webcast will be recorded and available for replay until the company’s conference call to discuss financial results for its first quarter of fiscal year 2019.
