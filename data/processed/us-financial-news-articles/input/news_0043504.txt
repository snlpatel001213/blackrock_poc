Tech firms let Russia examine U.S. software   02:31 
Tech companies including SAP, Symantec, and McAfee have allowed Russia to examine source code in software used across some of the most sensitive corners of the U.S. government, an arrangement that experts said poses a national security risk and is more widespread than previously known.
