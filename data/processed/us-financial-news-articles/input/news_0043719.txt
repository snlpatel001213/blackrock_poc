WASHINGTON—Sen. Chuck Schumer, having led Democrats into and out of a government shutdown, worked Tuesday to mend a public breach in party ranks and to push back against progressive groups who said he capitulated to Republicans.
“We are a lot better off today when it comes to the cause of Dreamers than we were four or five days ago,” Mr. Schumer said. “When there is a Republican president, and a Republican Senate and a Republican House that are quite recalcitrant against Dreamers, you are not going to get it all at once.”
