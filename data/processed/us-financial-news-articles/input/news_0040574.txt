January 25, 2018 / 1:48 PM / in 35 minutes Saudi Arabia says graft crackdown helps stock market Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
DAVOS, Switzerland (Reuters) - Investors have put a record amount of money into the Saudi stock market in recent weeks as they realize that a crackdown on corruption is positive for the economy, Saudi finance minister Mohammed Al-Jadaan said on Thursday.
