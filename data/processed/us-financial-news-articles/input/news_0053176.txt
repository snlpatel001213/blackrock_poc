VW faces inquiry call over diesel fumes test on monkeys Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:49 
Volkswagen's supervisory board has called for an immediate inquiry into who commissioned tests in which monkeys were exposed to toxic diesel fumes. As Matthew Larotonda reports, the German government said such studies were unjustifiable.
