Dennis Kneale’s “The Opioid Crisis Has Plaintiff Lawyers Smelling Cash,” (op-ed, Jan. 4) highlights the influx of plaintiffs’ attorney lawsuits related to the opioid epidemic. These lawsuits that seek to place blame entirely on pharmaceutical distributors fail to recognize the complexity of the crisis and deliberately misrepresent the important role our industry plays in the supply chain.
