NEW YORK, Jan. 25, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Rowley Law PLLC is investigating potential claims against Key Technology, Inc. (NASDAQ: KTEC) and its board of directors for breach of fiduciary duty concerning the proposed acquisition of the company by Duravant LLC. Stockholders will receive $26.75 for each share of Key Technology, Inc. that they hold. The transaction is valued at approximately $175 million and is expected to close in the first quarter of 2018.
