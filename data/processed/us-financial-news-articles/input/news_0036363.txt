January 23, 2018 / 10:36 AM / in 6 minutes MOVES-Hermes Investment names Amit Mehta senior Asia analyst Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
Jan 23 (Reuters) - Hermes Investment Management said on Tuesday it named Amit Mehta director and senior Asia analyst of its emerging markets team. 
Mehta previously worked for companies including Morgan Stanley and KPMG. He will be based in London and report to Gary Greenberg, head of emerging markets. (Reporting by Anirban Paul in Bengaluru)
