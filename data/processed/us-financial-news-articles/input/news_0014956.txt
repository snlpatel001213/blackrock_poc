YULEE, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Rayonier Inc. (NYSE:RYN) announced today the tax treatment of the Company’s 2017 dividend distribution on its Common Stock CUSIP 754907103.
The dividends, totaling $1.00 per share, were paid quarterly as follows: $0.25 on March 31, 2017; $0.25 on June 30, 2017; $0.25 on September 29, 2017; and $0.25 on December 29, 2017. The $1.00 per share dividend is classified for income tax purposes as 100% Capital Gain Distribution and 0% Non-dividend Distribution. About Rayonier
Rayonier is a leading timberland real estate investment trust with assets located in some of the most productive softwood timber growing regions in the United States and New Zealand. As of September 30, 2017, Rayonier owned, leased or managed approximately 2.7 million acres of timberlands located in the U.S. South (1.9 million acres), U.S. Pacific Northwest (378,000 acres) and New Zealand (429,000 acres). More information is available at www.rayonier.com .
