January 18, 2018 / 7:49 PM / Updated 22 minutes ago Tillerson to visit Europe next week for talks on Iran, Syria - State Department Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
WASHINGTON (Reuters) - U.S. Secretary of State Rex Tillerson will travel to Europe next week, stopping in London and Paris for talks on Iran and Syria before heading on to Davos, Switzerland, for the World Economic Forum and then to Warsaw, the State Department said on Thursday.
