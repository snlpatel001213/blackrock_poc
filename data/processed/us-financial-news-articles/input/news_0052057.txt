ST. PAUL, Minn. and KAUNAS, Lithuania, Jan. 30, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- BevSource, Inc, a preeminent provider of beverage development and operations solutions, announced today the acquisition of Lithuanian-based MyDrink Beverages. The acquisition allows BevSource, Inc to expand what it can offer its customers while reaching a broader audience and new clientele.
"For years, MyDrink Beverages has demonstrated their expertise at grabbing the attention of beverage entrepreneurs and brand owners throughout the world," said Todd Geisness, President of BevSource, Inc. "In addition, it was clear to us that MyDrink Beverages aligned with BevSource values and level of service. We are looking forward to growing our business."
"The acquisition of MyDrink Beverages is perfect proof that during the last 10 years we managed to build an industry-leading company for beverage development services," echoed Adomas Pranevicius, Founder and CEO of MyDrink Beverages. "This unique combination of experience will open new opportunities for our clients."
Like BevSource, MyDrink Beverages has spent years providing exceptional beverage consulting services to largely the European market. Together BevSource and MyDrink Beverages will guide beverage visionaries and innovators through the complex world of beverage development and production.
About BevSource, Inc: BevSource, Inc is a leading provider of beverage development and operations solutions to beverage visionaries. As beverage industry navigators, BevSource collaborates with clients to build a custom operational strategy for their beverage, identifies a unique mix of services and vendors to support it, then executes on that plan. For over 15 years, their goal has been to make beverage concepts a reality more efficiently, effectively, and successfully than ever dreamed possible. To learn more about BevSource offering, visit www.bevsource.com .
About MyDrink Beverages: The Lithuanian-based MyDrink Beverages focuses on delivering the highest quality consulting solutions for the beverage industry. Since 2008, MyDrink Beverages has specialized in beverage development and marketing, as well as production management and developing innovative beverage ideas. For more information, please visit www.mydrinkbeverages.com .
