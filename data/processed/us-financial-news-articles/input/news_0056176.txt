OSLO, Jan 30 (Reuters) - The spot price for farmed salmon rose by 2.14 Norwegian crowns to an average of 54.74 Norwegian crowns ($7.12) per kilo last week, according to statistics from the Nasdaq Salmon Index.
The price is for producers and applies to freshly sliced salmon for delivery in Oslo. ($1 = 7.6883 Norwegian crowns) (Reporting by Ole Petter Skonnord)
