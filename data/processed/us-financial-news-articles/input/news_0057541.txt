Xerox Corp. is nearing a deal with Japan’s Fujifilm Holdings Corp. that would mark the end of the independence of the stalwart of 20th-century American industry.
The deal would combine Xerox with a joint venture the company has with Fujifilm, and the U.S. company’s shareholders would own just under half of the resulting entity, according to people familiar with the matter.
