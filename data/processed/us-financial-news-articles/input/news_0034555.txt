Contradicting Kelly, Trump says he's been "very consistent" on wall Thursday, January 18, 2018 - 01:18 
President Trump says his stance on the wall has been ''consistent'' despite earlier suggestions by his chief of staff John Kelly, that Trump's views on the subject have evolved.
