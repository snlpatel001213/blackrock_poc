WHY WOULD any warm-blooded mammal vacation in Montreal in February, when temperatures can average 16 degrees Fahrenheit? Well, to name a few compelling factors, the food isn’t any less stellar, the exchange rate treats Americans kindly, and the air and hotel promotions are generous. But I had a different reason: Winter in Montreal means hockey at its purest, from the Canadiens, the National Hockey League’s most decorated franchise, to pickup games in one of the city’s 275 outdoor rinks open in winter. I spent a weekend there soaking up the hockey culture in every way I could imagine, and left with a higher level of love...
