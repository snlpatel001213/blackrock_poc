CORAOPOLIS, Pa.—President Donald Trump, cabinet officials and other Washington Republicans are scrambling to fend off a Democratic upset in a congressional special election, this one in a southwestern Pennsylvania district that Mr. Trump carried by 20 percentage points in 2016.
The Democrats, in contrast, have yet to invest significant resources in the race, which was never on their target list.
