PHILADELPHIA, Jan. 17, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- PREIT (NYSE: PEI) today announced the appointment of Lisa M. Most as its General Counsel, replacing Bruce Goldman. Ms. Most holds a JD-MBA degree from Washington University in St. Louis and holds an undergraduate degree from the University of Pennsylvania.
Ms. Most has been with PREIT since 1999, holding progressive leadership roles at the Company during her tenure. During her time at PREIT, she has been instrumental in building the legal transactional group and in structuring redevelopment and anchor tenant recapture agreements.
"I have enjoyed working with Lisa over the years as she has continued to expand her responsibilities and am confident she will serve the Company well in her role as General Counsel," said Joseph F. Coradino, CEO of PREIT.
Ms. Most is a member of the Association of Corporate Counsel, the PA Bar Association and the International Council of Shopping Centers.
About PREIT
PREIT (NYSE:PEI) is a publicly traded real estate investment trust that owns and manages quality properties in compelling markets. PREIT's robust portfolio of carefully curated retail and lifestyle offerings mixed with destination dining and entertainment experiences are located primarily in the densely-populated eastern U.S. with concentrations in the mid-Atlantic's top MSAs. Since 2012, the company has driven a transformation guided by an emphasis on portfolio quality and balance sheet strength driven by disciplined capital expenditures.
