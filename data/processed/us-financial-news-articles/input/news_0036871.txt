IRVINE, Calif., Jan. 23, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Mavenlink , the leading provider of cloud-based software for the modern services organization and a G2 Crowd Leader, announced today that accomplished technology sales leader, Doug Tilford, has joined Mavenlink to lead the company through hyper-growth and international expansion. In his role, Doug is responsible for building a world-class global sales team, delivering a differentiated client experience, and expanding strategic partnerships.
"We're excited and proud to have Doug join our talented and ambitious team," said Ray Grainger, CEO and founder of Mavenlink. "With a wealth of knowledge and leadership in global enterprise sales and unwavering commitment to client success, he is the perfect fit to lead our sales organization as we continue to expand globally."
Prior to Mavenlink, Doug spent more than 16 years at Akamai Technologies. While there he helped grow the company from $100 million to more than $2 billion in revenue, holding several leadership positions. He most recently served as the General Manager of Europe, Middle East, Africa and Russia, where he led a team of 500 people and $500 million in revenue. Under his leadership, the EMEA region became one of Akamai's leading divisions. Prior to this appointment, Doug held the role of SVP of Sales for the Americas, where he managed 500 people and $1 billion in revenue. Doug has also held roles responsible for leading high-performing sales and marketing teams at MarketWire.com and the Xerox Corporation.
"This is an incredibly exciting time to join Mavenlink," stated Doug Tilford. "With the company's software, services, expertise, and vision, it is uniquely positioned to lead the $3 trillion global services industry through a state of transformation. I look forward to redefining the future of the services economy along with Mavenlink's forward-thinking clients and incredibly talented team."
Doug joins Mavenlink following a year of significant growth and distinction. In the last 12 months, the company has rapidly added jobs at all of its four US locations, as well as international offices in the UK, Australia and the Philippines.
