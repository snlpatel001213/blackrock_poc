Robert W. Merry’s review of Robert Dallek’s “Franklin D. Roosevelt: A Political Life,” Books, Dec. 23) mentions an FDR Quote: about favoring “the liberty of the community rather than of the liberty of the individual.”
In that little phrase, which might seem innocent to the unwary, is the essence of every totalitarian system in history from Hitler to Mao to Stalin. The Nazis even had “the common good comes before individual good” inscribed on the rims of their silver coins.
