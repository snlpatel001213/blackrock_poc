New leadership after death of Compass Group CEO Tuesday, January 02, 2018 - 01:48 
Compass, the world's largest catering company, has installed a new CEO after Richard Cousins was killed in a seaplane crash on a Sydney river on New Year's Eve. As Kate King reports, police will attempt to raise the plane to get a better idea of what happened.
