High profile UK firms tackle gender pay gap 2:34am IST - 01:41 
The new chief executive of easyJet will take a pay cut to match the salary of his predecessor, Carolyn McCall, in a bid to battle against the gender pay gap. As Kate King reports, British companies are under heightened scrutiny over their pay structures and national broadcaster, the BBC, has faced criticism over why it pays some women less than men in equivalent jobs.
