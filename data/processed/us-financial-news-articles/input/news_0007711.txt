Toshiba gains after $4.6bn deal to sell Westinghouse   01:40 
A subsidiary of Canada's Brookfield Asset Management plans to acquire Westinghouse, Toshiba's bankrupt nuclear services company, for $4.6 billion, in a move that could push the Japanese giant closer to closure on its recent struggles. Kate King reports.
