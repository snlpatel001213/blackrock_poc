Turkey's Erdogan warns of Syrian 'big sweep' 6:20pm GMT - 01:30 
Turkey's President has said that his forces could push all the way east to the frontier with Iraq amid an operation to sweep Kurdish fighters from the Syrian border. But the move risks a possible confrontation with U.S. forces allied to the Kurds.
