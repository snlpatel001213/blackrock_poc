President Donald Trump on Wednesday said his administration would take a “strong look” at the nation’s libel laws, which he called a “disgrace,” following the release of a new book critical of Mr. Trump and the White House.
“If somebody says something that is totally false and knowingly false, the person who has been abused defamed libeled will have meaningful recourse,” Mr. Trump said, speaking at the outset of a cabinet meeting at the White House.
