Japan's Fujifilm snaps up Xerox in $6.1 bln deal 6:00pm GMT - 01:20 
Japan's Fujifilm Holdings is set to take over Xerox Corp, and combine the U.S. company into their joint venture Fuji Xerox in an effort to cut costs. As Silvia Antonioli reports, Fujifilm will own 50.1 percent of Xerox shares.
