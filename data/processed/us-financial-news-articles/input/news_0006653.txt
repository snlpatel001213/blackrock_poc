MANSION McCall, Idaho: A Ski Spot Without the Glitz McCall, Idaho, is no Sun Valley—and many folks hope it stays that way. While the resort town of about 3,000 has the requisite glacial lake and powdery ski slopes, McCall has retained the simple charm and small-town feel that’s less evident in Sun Valley and Ketchum, Idaho, to the southeast. If anything, McCall’s lack of glitz has made it more attractive in recent years to newsmakers and other VIPs looking for a lower profile vacation spot.
