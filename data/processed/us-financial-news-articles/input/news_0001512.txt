Gov. Cuomo claims that capping the federal income tax deduction for state and local taxes leads to unfair double taxation (Review & Outlook, Dec. 29, “Andrew Cuomo’s Tax Lament”).
But, ironically, the governor oversees one of the worst double taxation schemes in America: New York taxes the entire income of a nonresident who works for a New York employer when a nonresident works from home in another state and pays tax in that state.
