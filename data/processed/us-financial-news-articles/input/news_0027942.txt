President Donald Trump’s first year in office has often been marked by his ability to set the news agenda based on his Twitter activity, in no small part because his preferred time for tweeting is early in the day. A Wall Street Journal analysis of nearly 2,300 tweets during the president’s first year in office shows that nearly four in 10 were tweeted between the hours of 5 a.m. and 9 a.m. Many of those were barbs aimed at the media, Democrats and even some Republicans.
