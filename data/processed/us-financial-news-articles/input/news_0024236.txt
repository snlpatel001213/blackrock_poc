Tobin’s Pizza in Bloomington, Ill., makes a number of claims in its effort to lure business: it has been is voted No. 1 in McLean County; it is locally owned; products are made fresh daily.
The clincher, however, may be that Tobin’s boasts the “largest delivery fleet in downstate Illinois.” To support that claim, Moe Davis, the pizzeria’s owner, buys more than 30 Mitsubishi Mirage compact cars at a time, among the lowest-priced cars on the U.S. market.
