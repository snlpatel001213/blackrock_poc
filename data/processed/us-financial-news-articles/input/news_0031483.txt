LAS VEGAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Wynn Resorts, Limited (NASDAQ: WYNN) announced today that it will release the Company’s financial results for the fourth quarter ended December 31, 2017 prior to the market open on Monday, January 22, 2018, followed by a conference call at 9:00 a.m. PT (12:00 p.m. ET).
The call will be broadcast live at www.wynnresorts.com under the “Company Information” section. Interested parties may also dial (855) 415-3151 or, for international callers, (706) 643-0974. The conference call ID is 9767738.
A replay of the call will be available through February 19, 2018 by dialing (855) 859-2056 or, for international callers, (404) 537-3406. The replay access code is 9767738. The call will also be archived at www.wynnresorts.com .
