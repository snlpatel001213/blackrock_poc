The Mayo brothers’ greatest innovation was the group practice of medicine. Dr. Will and Dr. Charlie developed an entirely new approach to medical practice based upon a deeply held belief: The combined wisdom of peers is greater than that of any individual. They called it a “union of forces,” and this modus operandi dictates that multiple medical experts work collaboratively to find solutions for patients with serious, complex illnesses.
