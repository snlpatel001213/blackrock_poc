IRVINE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- First Foundation Inc. (NASDAQ: FFWM) (“First Foundation”), a financial services company with two wholly-owned operating subsidiaries, First Foundation Advisors and First Foundation Bank, today announced that it will report its financial results for the fourth quarter ending December 31, 2017 before the market opens on Wednesday, January 31, 2018.
On January 31, 2018, Scott F. Kavanaugh, Chief Executive Officer and John Michel, Chief Financial Officer of First Foundation Inc. will also host a discussion of the Company’s financial results and performance at 12:00PM ET / 9:00AM PT.
