Pacifica Foundation Inc. might be edging toward bankruptcy as the nonprofit radio broadcaster struggles to pay a $1.8 million judgment over missed lease payments for its Empire State Building antenna.
Bill Crosier, Pacifica’s interim executive director, is urging the foundation’s board to act immediately to protect the foundation’s assets from creditors through a voluntary chapter 11 filing. A “small majority” of Pacifica directors, however, are holding out hope that the foundation can arrange a loan outside of bankruptcy...
