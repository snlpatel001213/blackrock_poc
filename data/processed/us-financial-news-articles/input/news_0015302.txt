Germany toasts strongest growth in 6 years Thursday, January 11, 2018 - 01:39 
German output grew by a less than expected 2.2 percent in 2017, but that's still the strongest GDP rate in six years and unlikely to dispel fears that Europe's growth engine could be running too hot. Silvia Antonioli reports.
