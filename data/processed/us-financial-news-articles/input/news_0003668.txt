NEW YORK, NY, Jan. 04, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Tauriga Sciences, Inc. (OTC PINK: TAUG) ("Tauriga" or the "Company"), engaged in building its business through the development, distribution, and licensing of proprietary products as well as the evaluation of potential acquisition opportunities and equity investments, today announced that the Company has completed the purchase of its initial tranche of the crypto currency called Groestlcoin (code: GRS).
The Company had previously disclosed, via press release on December 27, 2017, that it decided to diversify $25,000 of its Net Cash Position into Groestlcoin and Gain Exposure to the Crypto Currency Sector.
On January 3, 2018, the Company purchased $10,000 of Groestlcoin and now owns 3,805.835 units. The Company expects to complete the balance of this $25,000 aggregate purchase of Groestlcoin over the next few weeks.
The Company utilized the crypto currency exchanges of COINBASE and Livecoin.net to complete this initial tranche and expects to continue along that same path for the additional Groestlcoin purchases.
