Rent-A-Center Inc. said Tuesday that its founder Mark Speese has resigned as chief executive as the company works to quell investor concerns about its performance.

Mr. Speese, who founded the rent-to-own store operator in 1986 and was serving in his second stint as CEO, is the latest person to leave the leadership team in recent months.
