The chairman of USA Gymnastics and two other board members resigned Monday, the organization said, following weeks of internal dissent and public outcry over the board’s handling of a sexual abuse scandal involving the former women’s national team doctor.
The move also came amid mounting pressure from the U.S. Olympic Committee, a person familiar with the matter said.
