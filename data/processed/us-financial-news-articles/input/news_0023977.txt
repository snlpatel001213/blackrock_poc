STOCKHOLM, Jan 16 (Reuters) - Swedish mobile network gear maker Ericsson said on Tuesday it had booked an impairment of 14.2 billion crowns ($1.8 billion) in the fourth quarter, related primarily to goodwill, hitting operating income but not cashflow.
It had flagged in December it may make a goodwill impairment at its struggling digital services and media businesses
