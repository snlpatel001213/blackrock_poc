TD Ameritrade Holding Corp. reported fiscal first-quarter results on Monday. Here’s what you need to know:
Earnings: The discount brokerage reported a profit of $297 million, or 52 cents a share, for the quarter ended Dec. 31, up from $216 million, or 41 cents a share, in the year-earlier period. Excluding acquisition-related expenses and other items, profit was 80 cents a share, compared with analysts’ projections for 50 cents a share, according to Thomson Reuters.
