So-called 529 education-savings accounts, and Americans who can benefit from using them, are big winners in the recent tax overhaul.
New curbs on state and local tax deductions will make these tax-sheltered accounts more attractive. Many people will also benefit from a change that now allows assets in these plans to be used to pay elementary and high school tuition.
