AMMAN, Jordan—Vice President Mike Pence received more blowback Sunday from President Donald Trump’s decision to recognize Jerusalem as the capital of Israel, with Jordan’s king cautioning that such a move should be part of a “comprehensive” Middle East peace deal.
At a luncheon, Jordan’s King Abdullah II told Mr. Pence that Jerusalem’s status “is key to peace in the region” and is as important to “Muslims and Christians as it is to Jews.”
