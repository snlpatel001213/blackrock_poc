Data has become one of the world’s most valuable commodities, and I fully expect the data boom to continue its ascent in 2018.
Last year, the companies collecting, processing and transmitting the world’s data ramped up infrastructure spending, causing computer chip volumes to rocket and the price of memory chips to double. This was a significant contributor to the 21% rise in sector revenue estimated for 2017 by the World Semiconductor Trade Statistics body toward the end of last year.
