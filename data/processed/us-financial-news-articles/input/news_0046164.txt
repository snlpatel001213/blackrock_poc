WINSTON-SALEM, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- HanesBrands (NYSE: HBI) today announced that it will host an Internet audio webcast of its fourth-quarter and full-year 2017 investor conference call at 8:30 a.m. EST Thursday, Feb. 8, 2018.
Prior to the conference call, Hanes will issue a news release disclosing financial results for the fourth quarter and year ended Dec. 30, 2017.
The Internet broadcast, which will consist of prepared comments followed by a question-and-answer session, may be accessed via the investors section of the Hanes corporate website, www.Hanes.com/investors . The call is expected to conclude by 9:30 a.m. EST.
Replays of the conference call will be available via the Internet and telephone. An archived replay of the audio webcast will be available in the investors section of the Hanes corporate website. The telephone playback will be available by noon EST Thursday, Feb. 8, 2018, through midnight EST Thursday, Feb. 15, 2018. The replay will be available by calling toll-free (855) 859-2056, or by toll call at (404) 537-3406. The replay ID is 3879578.
HanesBrands is a socially responsible leading marketer of everyday basic apparel under some of the world’s strongest apparel brands in the Americas, Europe, Australia and Pacific-Asia, including Hanes, Champion, Playtex, DIM, Bali, Maidenform, Bonds, L’eggs, JMS/Just My Size, Wonderbra, Nur Die/Nur Der, Lovable, Alternative, and Gear for Sports. More information about the company and its corporate social responsibility initiatives, including environmental, social compliance and community improvement achievements, may be found on the Hanes corporate website at www.Hanes.com/corporate .
