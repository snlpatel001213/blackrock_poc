Some investors are betting the dollar’s nearly seven-year bull market is due for one last hurrah in 2018.
The view hinges on a sweeping tax overhaul Republicans passed in December. Dollar bulls believe the tax plan could juice an already-growing economy, push the Federal Reserve to raise rates at a faster pace and encourage corporations to repatriate some of the estimated $1 trillion they hold abroad in cash—all outcomes that could fuel a dollar rally.
