LONDON, Jan 16 (Reuters) -
* Chevron will resume oil and gas drilling operations at the Sarta 3 block in Iraqi Kurdistan “in the near future”, a company spokeswoman said in an email.
* The San Ramon, California-based company temporarily suspended its operations in the region in October in the wake of unrest following a controversial independence referendum.
