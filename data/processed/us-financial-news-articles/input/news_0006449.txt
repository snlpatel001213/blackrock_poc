SAN JOSE, Calif.—Nathan Chen will head to the 2018 Winter Olympics as the great American figure skating hope, flanked by veteran skater and first-time Olympian Adam Rippon and another talented teenager, Vincent Zhou.
Chen, 18 years old, will carry the burden of U.S. expectations into South Korea after landing five quadruple jumps in a confident free skate to music from “Mao’s Last Dancer,” punctuated by impressive footwork and intricate spins, to retain his national title.
