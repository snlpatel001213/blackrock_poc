MCKENNEY, Va., Jan. 22, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Touchstone Bank (OTC Pink: TSBA) is pleased to announce the appointment of Mr. Mark Specter to its Board of Directors, effective January 17, 2018. Mr. Specter is the President and Owner and a Commercial Real Estate Broker of Specter Properties, Inc. located in Chesterfield County, Virginia. The company specializes in the sale and leasing of commercial and industrial real estate in Chesterfield County and the surrounding cities and counties. Mr. Specter has over 37 years of experience in the real estate profession and is recognized as a leader in the industry. He is a 1980 graduate of University of Richmond, Virginia and native of Petersburg, Virginia. Mr. Specter has earned numerous awards over the years and is active in the communities he serves.
"We are very fortunate to have Mr. Specter as a member of the Board of Directors. Mark's vast knowledge and experience, particularly in commercial real estate, offers additional expertise and diversity for us. When you combine his expertise and passion for success, it goes hand in hand with Touchstone Bank's values," says James R. Black, President & CEO.
Touchstone Bank is a full-service community bank with approximately $440 million in total assets headquartered in McKenney, Virginia. The Bank has ten branches and one loan center serving Southern and Central Virginia and three branches serving Northern North Carolina. Visit www.touchstone.bank for more information.
