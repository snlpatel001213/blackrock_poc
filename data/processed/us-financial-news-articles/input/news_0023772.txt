France's baby milk scare deepens 4:04pm GMT - 01:37 
France, seeking to limit reputational damage to its agri-business industry, has threatened to impose sanctions after the country’s big supermarkets said recalled baby foods made by Lactalis had still found their way onto shop shelves. Ciara Lee reports.
