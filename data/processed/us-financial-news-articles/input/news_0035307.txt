The chairman of the Securities and Exchange Commission on Monday fired a warning shot at companies that try to use gimmicks to capitalize on investors’ appetite for bitcoin and other cryptocurrency investments.

Jay Clayton told a legal conference in California that the SEC “is looking closely” when companies suddenly profess to be significantly involved with bitcoin or blockchain, the technology that underpins many cryptocurrencies.
