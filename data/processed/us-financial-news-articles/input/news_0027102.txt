WASHINGTON—The top lobbyist for the U.S. oil-and-gas industry is stepping down after 10 years on the job. 
Jack Gerard, the president and CEO of the American Petroleum Institute, sent an email to his staff on Wednesday morning saying that he decided not to seek another five-year contract with the nation’s largest oil-and-gas industry trade association. The move “was not an easy decision,” he wrote, calling the job “among the most fulfilling professional experiences I have had.”
