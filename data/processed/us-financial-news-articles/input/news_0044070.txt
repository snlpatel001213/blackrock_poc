Judge reads Nassar's letter defending himself as 'good doctor' 3:55am IST - 02:39 
Ingham County Circuit Court Judge Rosemarie Aquilina read a statement by Larry Nassar, the long-time USA Gymnastics team doctor, defending himself as a 'good doctor'.
