EVEN IF YOU maintain you’ve had great chilaquiles, this recipe, the second Slow Food Fast contribution from Gonzalo Guzmán, will come as a revelation.

Gently toss half-cooked scrambled eggs with tortilla chips and a slow-burn salsa, and within minutes a velvety sauce will coalesce in the pan, seep into the chips and soften their bite.
