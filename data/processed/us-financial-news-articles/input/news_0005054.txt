Reckless young people—and their hapless parents—lead L.A. private detective Elvis Cole into another dangerous case in Robert Crais’s “The Wanted” (Putnam, 322 pages, $28). In Mr. Crais’s latest story featuring Cole and his partner, Joe Pike, Cole concerns himself with 17-year-old Tyson Connor: an indifferent, introverted student who has taken to wearing expensive clothes, hanging out at “celebutante” clubs and carrying large rolls of cash.
