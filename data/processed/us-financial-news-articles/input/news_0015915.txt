Lithuania liquor ad ban causes headaches 10:19pm IST - 01:17 
Print distributors across Lithuania are being forced to rip pages out of the magazines they're trying to sell. The offending content: pictures of alcohol - now illegal after a liquor advertising ban was introduced here at the start of January. Rosanna Philpott reports.
