PHOENIX—Joe Arpaio, a polarizing national figure for his hard line on immigration, was voted out of office after nearly 24 years as Maricopa County Sheriff. He was convicted of disobeying a court order to stop the immigration raids that helped make him famous.
Now, at 85, Mr. Arpaio is back—and aiming for higher office.
