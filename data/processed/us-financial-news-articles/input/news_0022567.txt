Softbank Group Corp. could be selling its cash cow. Investors excited about the news should pay close attention to where that cash ends up.
The Japanese tech conglomerate is considering selling 30% of its successful domestic telecom business in an initial public offering that could raise around $18 billion, analysts reckon. Rumors about a spinoff have popped up occasionally over the last two years, since Softbank said that it intended to separate its domestic business from its international investments.
