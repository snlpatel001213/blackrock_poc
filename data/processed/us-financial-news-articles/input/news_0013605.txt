BUDAPEST, Jan 8 (Reuters) - Hungary's calendar-adjusted retail sales rose by an annual 6.4 percent in November following 6.3 percent increase in October, the Central Statistics Office (KSH) said on Monday. Food sales grew by 4 percent, non-food sales were up by 10.2 percent, while fuel sales rose by 3.7 percent.
