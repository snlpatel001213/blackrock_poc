Can a 'latte levy' tackle the recyclable cup myth? Friday, January 05, 2018 - 01:54 
British lawmakers have recommended taxing the use of paper coffee cups to cut down on waste as fewer than one percent are recycled in that country. But whether it could recreate the success of similar plastic bag charges adopted in British and American cities is less certain.
