SEOUL—South Korea’s leader acknowledged policy differences with his U.S. ally in their approach to North Korea, but credited President Donald Trump for helping bring Pyongyang back to the negotiating table.
A day after senior officials from the two Koreas met, President Moon Jae-in expressed “guarded optimism,” and called for further inter-Korean dialogue during next month’s Winter Olympics in South Korea.
