Brand new facility a sign of company's global expansion, continued growth in revenue and employee base
SAN JOSE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- 8x8, Inc. (NYSE:EGHT), a leading provider of cloud communications and customer engagement solutions, today announced it signed a lease for an office building under construction in San Jose, California that will become the company’s new corporate headquarters. A key component of 8x8’s global growth strategy, the company plans to move its expanding San Francisco Bay Area employee base, currently located in two separate offices, into the new office building by early 2019.
"Since first coming to North San Jose in 2011, we are proud to see 8×8 moving into a larger Coleman Highline headquarters, which will help facilitate their next phase of growth," said Mayor Sam Liccardo. "I congratulate 8x8 and its CEO Vik Verma on their expansion, and thank them for their confidence and investments in San Jose."
8x8 will be the first tenant at the new Coleman Highline development in San Jose, and will occupy all 162,000 square feet of Class-A office space in building number one. With ample room for future expansion, the development is close to major freeways and San Jose Mineta International Airport, and within walking distance to public transportation, food establishments and other amenities. The new 8x8 headquarters will allow the company to bring all of its employees in the region together in a new, state-of-the-art office facility.
“This is a very exciting time for 8x8. We have been rapidly expanding our global customer base particularly in the midmarket and enterprise sector while hiring the industry’s top talent to join us in our next phase of market leadership,” said Vikram Verma, CEO at 8x8, Inc. “The new 8x8 headquarters demonstrates our commitment to grow our operations in San Jose, and deliver a world-class experience for our employees, partners and customers who help accelerate our global growth strategy.”
