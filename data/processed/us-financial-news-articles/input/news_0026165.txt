 41 PM / Updated 10 minutes ago Magnitude 6.6 quake strikes off Loreto, Mexico - USGS Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
LONDON (Reuters) - A magnitude 6.6 earthquake struck off the coast of Loreto in the Mexican state of Baja California Sur on Friday, the United States Geological Survey reported. 
The epicentre of the quake was 10kms below the surface, the USGS said. Reporting by London desk
