NEW YORK, January 24, 2018 /PRNewswire/ --
IntSights Cyber Intelligence , a leading provider of surface, deep and dark web cyber threat intelligence and risk mitigation solutions, today announced that Itay Kozuch has been hired as Director of Threat Research. A cybersecurity expert with over a decade of experience, Kozuch brings a unique customer perspective to IntSights' research division.
"Itay's previous experience, including such roles as CISO, security consultant, integrator, and senior manager, gives him real-world understanding of the challenges our customers face and how to best help them operationalize intelligence to protect their business," said Guy Nizan, IntSights' CEO and co-founder. "By applying this viewpoint to our research initiatives, he will help our clients better understand the potential business impacts of the threats they face."
Kozuch joins IntSights' Tel Aviv office from KPMG Israel, where he served as manager and head of cyber technologies. He previously led cyber projects for Panama's Cable Onda and the United Bank of Africa. Kozuch has also held security advisory roles with Deloitte LLP, Trustnet Ltd. and SECOZ. He holds a B.A. in political science from the Open University, and his certifications include MCSE, CISO, ISO - Certified Lead Auditor (Information security), CISSP and CCSK.
"I'm very happy and excited to join the IntSights team," said Kozuch. "IntSights is poised for rapid growth and I look forward to driving a research agenda that will allow our customers and partners around the globe to anticipate and stop cyber-attacks."
