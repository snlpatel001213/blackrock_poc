Goldman Sachs Chairman and CEO Lloyd Blankfein did not totally shun bitcoin in Wednesday’s CNBC interview from Davos, “Mad Money” host Jim Cramer said. 
“I think it was important to parse what Lloyd said,” Cramer reflected. “[Blankfein] said customers who want to be involved with bitcoin … we will do business” on some level. 
Blankfein denied reports that the investment bank was considering implementing a new trading desk for digital currencies. But he did say Goldman would be “clearing futures in bitcoin for some of our futures clients.” 
“He did say, ‘If clients want to do something in the futures with crypto, they are there for them,'” Cramer said on “Squawk on the Street.”
