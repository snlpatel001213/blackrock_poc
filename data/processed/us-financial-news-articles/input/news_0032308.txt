ARLINGTON, Va. and LONDON, Jan. 17, 2018 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Willis Towers Watson (NASDAQ:WLTW), a leading global advisory, broking and solutions company, will announce its financial results for the fourth quarter and full year 2017 on Thursday, February 8, 2018 before the market opens.
The company will host a conference call to discuss its financial results at 9:00 a.m. Eastern Time on February 8, 2018. A live broadcast of the conference call will be available online at the Investor Relations section of www.willistowerswatson.com .
An online replay at www.willistowerswatson.com will be available shortly after the call. A telephonic replay of the call will also be available for 24 hours at 404-537-3406, conference ID 5494338.
