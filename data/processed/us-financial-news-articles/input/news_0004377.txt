Kenyan football at risk as sponsorship pulled 10:57am EST - 01:43 
Kenya's premier league football champions face the prospect of exclusion from the Confederation of African Football after an online sports betting firm cancelled 600 million shillings ($5.80 million) in direct annual sponsorship for the country’s main football league. Ciara Lee reports.
