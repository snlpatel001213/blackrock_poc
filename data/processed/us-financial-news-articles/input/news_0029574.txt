January 19, 2018 / 9:27 PM / Updated 6 minutes ago U.S. approves possible sale of 34 F-35s to Belgium for $6.53 bln- Pentagon Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
WASHINGTON, Jan 19 (Reuters) - The U.S. State Department has approved the possible sale of 34 Lockheed Martin Corp F-35 Joint Strike Fighters to Belgium for $6.53 billion, the Pentagon said on Friday. 
The proposed sale would include 34 conventional takeoff F-35s as well as 38 Pratt & Whitney F-135 engines and other equipment for the radar-evading high-tech fighter, the Pentagon said. Reporting by David Alexander; Editing by Eric Walsh
