Stock investors can’t invest in Uber, but they can invest in Nasdaq-listed Yandex, owner of Russia’s top ride-hailing platform. The terrain up for grabs is vast, the potholes legion.
In their current guise, Uber and its mostly venture capital-funded ilk are clever taxi apps. But investors have been willing to finance hugely expensive battles over market share because of what they could be: gateways to an entire transport system centered on driverless cars.
