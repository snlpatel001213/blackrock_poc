Richardson says Suu Kyi remains Myanmar's best hope Friday, January 26, 2018 - 02:00 
Aung San Suu Kyi remains Myanmar's best hope for change, veteran U.S. mediator Bill Richardson said on Friday, days after he got into a fight with the Nobel laureate and quit an international panel advising her government on the Rohingya crisis.
