CINCINNATI--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- The Procter & Gamble Company (NYSE:PG) will webcast a discussion of its second quarter earnings results on Tuesday, January 23, 2018 beginning at 8:30 a.m. ET.
Media and investors may access the live audio webcast at www.pginvestor.com , beginning at 8:30 a.m. ET. The webcast will also be available for replay.
