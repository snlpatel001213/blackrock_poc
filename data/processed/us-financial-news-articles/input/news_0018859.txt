To the thousands of gadget makers flocking to Las Vegas this week, let GoPro serve as a cautionary tale.

The action camera maker deserves credit for creating a market for a new type of device when cheaper and more convenient alternatives abounded. In its best year to date, GoPro sold more than 6.5 million cameras as Apple Inc. was selling its mega-popular iPhone 6, which featured a strong camera in its own right.
