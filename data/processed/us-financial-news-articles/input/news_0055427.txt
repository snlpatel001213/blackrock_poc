Jan 30 (Reuters) - Shenzhen Ysstech Info-Tech Co Ltd
* Says it sees net profit for FY 2017 to increase by 60 percent to 85 percent, or to be 193.7 million yuan to 223.9 million yuan
* Says the net profit of FY 2016 was 121 million yuan
* Comments that increased main business income and investment income as well as decreased costs as main reasons for the forecast
