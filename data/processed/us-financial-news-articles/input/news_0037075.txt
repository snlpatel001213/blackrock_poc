NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- The Law Offices of Vincent Wong notifies investors of an investigation concerning whether Aerohive Networks, Inc. (“Aerohive Networks” or the “Company”) (NYSE: HIVE) violated federal securities laws.
Click here to learn about the case: http://docs.wongesq.com/HIVE-Info-Request-Form-1789 . There is no cost or obligation to you.
During a conference call on November 1, 2017, Aerohive CFO John Ritchie stated that the Company was expecting fourth quarter revenue between $40 and $42 million. Then on January 16, 2018, Aerohive reduced its guidance to approximately $37 million, attributing the reduction to “underlying sales execution issues” discovered at the end of the third quarter. Upon this news, shares of Aerohive fell from a close of $5.70 per share on January 16, 2018, to a close of $4.07 per share on January 17, 2018.
