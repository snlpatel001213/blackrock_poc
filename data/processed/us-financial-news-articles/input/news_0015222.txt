NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Rowley Law PLLC is investigating potential claims against DST Systems, Inc. (NYSE: DST) and its board of directors for breach of fiduciary duty concerning the proposed acquisition of the company by SS&C Technologies Holdings, Inc. Shareholders will receive $84.00 in cash for each share of DST Systems, Inc. they hold. The transaction is valued at approximately $5.4 billion and is expected to close by the third quarter of 2018.
