ATLANTA, Jan. 9, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Secretariat is pleased to announce the promotions of Ted Scott and Mike Saulsbury to Managing Directors. Ted has done an outstanding job of expanding our West Coast practice and has testified on some major international projects. Mike has helped establish the firm's presence in the UAE (Dubai) and has a reputation second to none in the Middle East. Their unwavering commitment to integrity and quality reinforce the firm's core principles.
