REDWOOD CITY, Calif. (AP) _ Electronic Arts Inc. (EA) on Tuesday reported a loss of $186 million in its fiscal third quarter.
On a per-share basis, the Redwood City, California-based company said it had a loss of 60 cents. Earnings, adjusted for non-recurring costs and pretax expenses, were $2.22 per share.
The results surpassed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of 12 analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $2.18 per share.
The video game maker posted revenue of $1.16 billion in the period. Its adjusted revenue was $1.97 billion, falling short of Street forecasts. Eight analysts surveyed by Zacks expected $2.02 billion.
For the current quarter ending in March, Electronic Arts said it expects revenue in the range of $1.53 billion.
The company expects full-year earnings to be $3.25 per share, with revenue expected to be $5.1 billion.
Electronic Arts shares have risen 13 percent since the beginning of the year, while the Standard & Poor's 500 index has increased nearly 6 percent. In the final minutes of trading on Tuesday, shares hit $118.53, a climb of 41 percent in the last 12 months.
