There is no better ally and friend of the U.S., and a better example of a full-functioning democracy, than Norway. As two recent U.S. ambassadors to Oslo, we regret the sour tone and unearned slurs of Orde Kittrie (“What’s the Matter With Norway?,” Jan. 16). Norway is noteworthy in its support of world-wide human rights and humanitarian relief, peacemaking, peacekeeping and a free press.
