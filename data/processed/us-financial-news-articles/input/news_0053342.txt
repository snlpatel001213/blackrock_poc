Regarding Lois Epstein’s Jan. 13 letter bemoaning the Trump administration’s proposal to decrease safety rules relating to the oil-and-gas industry, Ms. Epstein should be mindful that more rules don’t mean better results. Safety in any industry is a difficult beast to tame as it is ponderable only probabilistically. The bureaucratic contention that modifying a highly technical regulation designed to prevent offshore well blowouts will have adverse effects is misplaced at best; any process or situation needs an optimal solution, not a plethora of rules and procedures.
