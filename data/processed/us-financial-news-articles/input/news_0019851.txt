Niall Ferguson has again written a brilliant book, this time in defense of traditional top-down principles of governing the wild market and the wilder international order. “The Square and the Tower” raises the question of just how much the unruly world should be governed—and by whom. Not everyone will agree, but everyone will be charmed and educated.
