ROME—Pope Francis Monday called for stronger efforts for nuclear disarmament, particularly on the Korean Peninsula, the day before representatives of South and North Korea are due to meet in Seoul for their first official bilateral talks in two years.
“It is of paramount importance to support every effort at dialogue” between the countries to “increase mutual trust and ensure a peaceful future for the Korean people and the entire world,” the pope said in his annual address to ambassadors accredited to the Vatican.
