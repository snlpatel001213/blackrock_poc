New York City’s new transit chief Andy Byford said he intends to lead a top-to-bottom modernization of the agency that oversees the city’s subway, which has been plagued by repeated delays and disruptions.
He also said he wants to focus on the basics, which include providing safe, clean, reliable service. “We’ve got to get the basics right, day in, day out,” he said.
