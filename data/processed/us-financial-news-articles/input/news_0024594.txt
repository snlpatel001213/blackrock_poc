VALLEY FORGE, Pa.--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- AmeriGas Partners, L.P. (NYSE: APU ) will announce the results of its first fiscal quarter after the market closes on January 31. The company will hold a live internet audio webcast of its conference call to discuss results and other current activities at 9:00 AM EST on Thursday, February 1.
Interested parties may listen to the audio webcast both live and in replay on the internet at http://investors.amerigas.com/investor-relations/events-presentations or by visiting the company website http://www.amerigas.com and clicking on Investor Relations.
A telephonic replay will be available from 12:00 PM EST on February 1 through 11:59 PM EST February 8. The replay may be accessed toll free at 855-859-2056 and internationally at 404-537-3406, conference ID 2603134.
