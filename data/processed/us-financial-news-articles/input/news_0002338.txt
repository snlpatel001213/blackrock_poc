January 2, 2018 / 11:37 AM / Updated 5 hours ago Man found dead in London's Canary Wharf financial district   
(Reuters) - A man was found dead in a trench beneath a skyscraper in London’s Canary Wharf financial district on Tuesday morning, London Ambulance Service said. The ambulance service said it was called to Bank Street after reports of a person falling from one of the buildings.
