Plane skids off Turkish runway on Black Sea coast, passengers unhurt   00:42 
A Pegasus Airlines plane skidded off the runway of a Turkish coastal airport and ended up halfway down a steep slope into the Black Sea, but no passengers or crew were hurt, Turkish media reported on Sunday. Julian Satterthwaite reports.
