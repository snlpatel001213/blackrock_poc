Jan 11 (Reuters) - Post Holdings said on Thursday it plans to explore options for its private brands businesses, which makes nut butter, pasta and granola products for top retailers, distributors and manufacturers.
The company said it was looking at options, including an initial public offering, a placement of private equity, a sale or a strategic combination.
