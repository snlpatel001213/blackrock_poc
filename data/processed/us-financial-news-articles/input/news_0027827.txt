Jan 18 (Reuters) - Wall Street’s main indexes opened little changed on Thursday as energy stocks took a hit from lower oil prices.
The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 13.58 points, or 0.05 percent, to 26,129.23. The S&P 500 lost 1.76 points, or 0.0628 percent, to 2,800.8. The Nasdaq Composite dropped 4.15 points, or 0.06 percent, to 7,294.13. (Reporting by Sruthi Shankar in Bengaluru; Editing by Anil D‘Silva)
