As a boy on the shores of Lake Victoria in Kenya, Calestous Juma found books were scarce, so he wrote to embassies in Nairobi to request information about the outside world. The Germans sent him a leaflet about what to buy and wear in Düsseldorf. The Chinese dispatched a copy of Mao’s “Little Red Book.”
