January 6, 2018 / 4:02 PM / a few seconds ago Magnitude 5.1 quake hits western Iran: state TV Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
DUBAI (Reuters) - A magnitude 5.1 earthquake hit western Iran on Saturday, state television reported, jolting the same area where a 7.3 quake killed more than 600 people in November. 
The state television did not immediately report any damage or casualties. Reporting by Dubai newsroom; Editing by Angus MacSwan
