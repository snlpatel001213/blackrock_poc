January 17, 2018 / 4:19 PM / Updated 33 minutes ago London Metal Exchange hires Robin Martin to head development Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
LONDON, Jan 17 (Reuters) - The London Metal Exchange has hired Robin Martin as head of market development, replacing Matt Chamberlain who last year was appointed chief executive of the 140-year old exchange, a spokeperson at the LME said. 
Martin will join the LME on March 19. Reporting by Pratima Desai; Editing by Mark Potter
