BoJ: policy unchanged, all eyes now on ECB 8:20am EST - 01:50 
The Bank of Japan has kept monetary settings unchanged as expected and its chief hosed down market speculation of a shift away from ultra-easy policy later this year as inflation remained stubbornly shy of the central bank's target. As David Pollard reports, the decision came just days before the ECB's latest policy decision.
