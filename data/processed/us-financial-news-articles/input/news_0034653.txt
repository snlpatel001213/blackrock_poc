At least three dead in Thai market bombing 10:32am EST - 00:59 
A motorcycle bomb exploded in a market in Thailand's southern Yala province on Monday, killing at least three people and wounding 22, a spokesman for the Internal Security Operations Command (ISOC) said, the first such attack in the region in months. Saskia O'Donoghue reports.
