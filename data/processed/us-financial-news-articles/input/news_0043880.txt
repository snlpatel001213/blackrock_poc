NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- On January 24, 2018, the Board of Trustees of Dreyfus High Yield Strategies Fund (NYSE:DHF) declared from net investment income a monthly cash dividend of $0.0235 per share of beneficial interest, payable on February 22, 2018 to shareholders of record at the close of business on February 7, 2018. The ex-dividend date is February 6, 2018. The previous dividend declared in December 2017 was $0.0235 per share of beneficial interest.
