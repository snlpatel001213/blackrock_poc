U.S. stocks dropped sharply Tuesday as markets turned cautious after an impressive start to the year.
The Dow Jones Industrial Average declined 303 points, or 1.2%, to 26137, on track for its worst day since August. The S&P 500 fell 1% and the Nasdaq Composite lost 1%. The selloff extends declines from Monday, when the Dow and S&P 500 posted their biggest one-day declines of the year.
