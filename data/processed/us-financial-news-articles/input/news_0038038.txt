January 23, 2018 / 5:37 PM / in 28 minutes Norway prosecutors appeal verdict in Hexagon CEO case Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
OSLO, Jan 23 (Reuters) - Norwegian prosecutors will appeal the acquittal of Hexagon Chief Executive Ola Rollen over insider trading charges to a higher court, they said on Tuesday. 
Rollen, who was cleared of all accusations in a unanimous verdict on Jan. 10, now faces the prospect of a new trial at a later date. (Reporting by Ole Petter Skonnord)
