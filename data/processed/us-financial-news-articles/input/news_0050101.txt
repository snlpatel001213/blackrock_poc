SANTA FE, N.M.—Ed Mitnick, a lawyer from Massachusetts, recently traveled to the state capitol here to present an uncomfortable question to a room full of lawmakers.
“What about the serial hugger?” Mr. Mitnick asked, as a murmur rippled through the crowd. “The person who walks around the statehouse, and every time they see someone, they say ‘Gimme a hug. You look like you need a hug.’ And they squeeze you very tight.”
