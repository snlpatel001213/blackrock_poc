To participate in the call, please dial 1-800-239-9838 or 323-794-2551 referencing conference ID #5145633. Callers should dial in five to ten minutes in advance of the conference call. The replay will be available by dialing 1-888-203-1112.
Saia, Inc. (NASDAQ:SAIA) offers customers a wide range of less-than-truckload, non-asset truckload, expedited and logistics services.
