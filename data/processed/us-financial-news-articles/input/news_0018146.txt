Gillian Anderson puts slavery on Davos agenda 12:49pm EST - 02:00 
In 2014 she played a humanitarian photographer in ‘Sold’, a movie about the modern day slave trade in Bangladesh. Now Gillian Anderson has cast herself in the real life role of anti slavery campaigner, and has brought her message to business leaders in Davos.
