SEOUL (Reuters) - South Korea’s presidential office said on Wednesday it welcomes Japan’s decision for Prime Minister Shinzo Abe to visit South Korea in time for the Pyeongchang Olympics next month.
“The two governments of Japan and South Korea have discussed Prime Minister Abe’s visiting South Korea for the Pyeongchang Winter Olympics and welcome Japan’s decision to officially announce the visit,” said the Blue House in a statement.
