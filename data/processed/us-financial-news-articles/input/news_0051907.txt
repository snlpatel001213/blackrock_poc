January 30, 2018 / 12:38 PM / in 31 minutes Wall St. stumbles over high bond yields, health stocks Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
NEW YORK (Reuters) - U.S. stocks fell for the second straight day on Tuesday as healthcare stocks and rising bond yields weighed on all three major U.S. indexes. 
The Dow Jones Industrial Average .DJI fell 361.83 points, or 1.37 percent, to 26,077.65, the S&P 500 .SPX lost 31.06 points, or 1.09 percent, to 2,822.47 and the Nasdaq Composite .IXIC dropped 64.02 points, or 0.86 percent, to 7,402.48. Reporting by Stephen Culp; Editing by Nick Zieminski
