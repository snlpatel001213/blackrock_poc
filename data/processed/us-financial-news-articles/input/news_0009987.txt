Two cousins emerge from a large African-American family. One, Danielle Allen, becomes an academic rising star, a tenured Harvard professor with degrees from Princeton and Cambridge. The other, Michael Allen, completes his GED in prison, where he spent more than a third of his life before being murdered on the tough streets of Los Angeles at the age of 29. How did their paths diverge so wildly? And why did Michael’s have such a tragic end?
