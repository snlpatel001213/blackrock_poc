When Amy Guerrin was battling breast cancer, she found the best way to release her emotions was to channel her inner Ringo Starr and play the air drums. Ms. Guerrin, 52, had discovered Pound, a workout created in 2011 by two recreational drummers and former college athletes that uses weighted drumsticks.
