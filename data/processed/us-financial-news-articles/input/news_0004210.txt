South Korea offers talks with Pyongyang ahead of Olympics 3:15pm IST - 01:30 
South Korea has offered to hold talks next week with North Korean officials to discuss how a delegation could attend the upcoming Winter Olympics, this after Kim Jong Un appeared to extend an olive branch to Seoul in his New Year's speech. Sara Hemrajani reports.
