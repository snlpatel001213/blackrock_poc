KANSAS CITY, Mo., Jan. 24, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Lockton has appointed Debbie Goldstine to executive vice president and U.S. casualty leader. In this expanded role, Goldstine will support delivery of strategic casualty solutions for Lockton clients and advise associates and clients on marketplace and emerging risk trends.
"Debbie has built a reputation with clients and colleagues as an expert in complex risk management issues," said Peter Clune, Lockton's president and COO of U.S. operations. "Our clients, associates and markets will all be energized as they learn about Debbie's new role."
Goldstine is a 20-year industry veteran and has been with Lockton for 12 years. She most recently was senior vice president and U.S. excess casualty practice leader. Goldstine is a past recipient of multiple industry honors including Risk and Insurance's Rising Star, Power Broker and 40 under 40 as well as Insurance Business America "Elite Women of Insurance." Goldstine resides in Chicago with her husband and two young sons.
