U.S. military to accept transgender recruits 1:28pm EST - 01:27 
Transgender people will be allowed for the first time to enlist in the U.S. military starting on Monday, as ordered by federal courts, after President Donald Trump's administration decided not to appeal rulings that blocked his transgender ban.
