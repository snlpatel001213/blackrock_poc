‘Paddington 2” is “The Godfather Part II” of Peruvian bear movies, a sequel that surpasses the superb original. When the little guy with the floppy red hat and a fondness for orange marmalade first appeared on the feature screen three years ago this month, he seemed like a gift from the movie gods in a traditionally lean season. Now he and his creators have outdone themselves. (The creator of the beloved children’s books, Michael Bond, died last June.)
