HOUSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- VOC Energy Trust (NYSE: VOC) announced the Trust distribution of net profits for the fourth quarterly payment period ended December 31, 2017.
Unitholders of record on January 30, 2018 will receive a distribution amounting to $1,870,000 or $0.11 per unit, payable February 14, 2018.
