January 29, 2018 / 12:00 PM / Updated 10 minutes ago Packaging company WestRock to buy KapStone for $4.9 billion Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
(Reuters) - U.S. packaging company WestRock Co ( WRK.N ) said on Monday it would buy KapStone Paper and Packaging Corp ( KS.N ) for an enterprise value of about $4.9 billion (£3.5 billion), to help expand in the western United States. 
The $35-per-share offer represents a premium of 32 percent to KapStone stock’s Friday close. 
WestRock would also assume net debt of about $1.36 billion, the companies said. Reporting by Arunima Banerjee in Bengaluru; Editing by Sriraj Kalluvila
