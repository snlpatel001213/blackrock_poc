When everyone is buying, often the best strategy is to be a seller.
Deal making in the health-care industry is booming with more than $140 billion in announced deals in the past three months, according to Dealogic. This week, drugmakers Sanofi and Celgene announced acquisitions for a combined total value of approximately $20 billion. That follows deals in sectors like hospital systems, health insurance, pharmacy benefit management and physician staffing businesses.
