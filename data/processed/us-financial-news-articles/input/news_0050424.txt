HOUSTON, Jan. 29, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- The Vortex Companies recently announced that it has promoted Matthew Samford from Corporate Controller to Executive Vice President of Finance. "Over the last two years, Matt's played in integral role in the company's rapid expansion," stated Craig Strawbridge, Vortex Companies Chief Financial Officer. "His ability to manage our growth, from developing a comprehensive employee benefits package and handling acquisition integration to assisting our equity partners, proves that this promotion is well-deserved."
Samford graduated from Texas A&M University's, Mays Business School in 2003 and holds both a Bachelors in Accounting and Masters in Finance. He has been a licensed CPA in the state of Texas since 2005. Prior to joining The Vortex Companies in 2016, Matthew was an audit manager at a Big 4 accounting firm, before transitioning into the construction industry. "I've found I really like the unique challenges presented when working for a major construction services company and enjoy contributing to such a successful and talented team," said Mr. Samford. "It is exciting to be a part of the continued growth and success of the Vortex Companies."
The Vortex Companies is one of the fastest growing trenchless solution providers in the water, sewer and industrial marketplace. In addition to bypass and pipe bursting solutions, its list of services includes, large diameter structural relining, pipe, structural and corrosion protection, trenchless robotic systems, pipe and drain cleaners and industrial facility maintenance and renewal.
