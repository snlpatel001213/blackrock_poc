SANTA CLARA, Calif. (AP) _ Intel Corp. (INTC) on Thursday reported a fourth-quarter loss of $687 million, after reporting a profit in the same period a year earlier.
On a per-share basis, the Santa Clara, California-based company said it had a loss of 15 cents. Earnings, adjusted for pretax expenses and amortization costs, were $1.08 per share.
The results exceeded Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of 14 analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 86 cents per share.
The world's largest chipmaker posted revenue of $17.05 billion in the period, also beating Street forecasts. Thirteen analysts surveyed by Zacks expected $16.31 billion.
For the year, the company reported profit of $9.6 billion, or $1.99 per share. Revenue was reported as $62.76 billion.
For the current quarter ending in April, Intel expects its per-share earnings to range from 65 cents to 75 cents.
The company expects full-year earnings in the range of $3.37 to $3.73 per share.
Intel shares have dropped roughly 2 percent since the beginning of the year, while the Standard & Poor's 500 index has increased 6 percent. In the final minutes of trading on Thursday, shares hit $45.30, a rise of 20 percent in the last 12 months.
