January 11, 2018 / 12:53 AM / Updated 28 minutes ago China Premier Li says 2017 GDP growth expected around 6.9 percent Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
BEIJING (Reuters) - China’s Premier Li Keqiang said the economy is expected to have grown around 6.9 percent last year, according to the official Xinhua news agency. Chinese Premier Li Keqiang chairs the second Mekong-Lancang Cooperation leaders meeting, under the theme "Our River of Peace and Sustainable Development", in Phnom Penh, Cambodia January 10, 2018. REUTERS/Samrang Pring 
China’s foreign exchange reserves continued to rise last year, while the country’s corporate leverage ratio declined at a steady pace, Li said at the Mekong-Lancang Cooperation Forum in the Cambodian capital of Phnom Penh on Wednesday, according to Xinhua.
