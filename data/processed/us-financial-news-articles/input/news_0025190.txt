Actor Bruce Willis and his wife Emma Willis are putting their sprawling Central Park West apartment on the market for $17.75 million
The couple purchased the apartment, which is in a prewar co-op building at 271 Central Park West, from Milwaukee Bucks basketball team owner Wesley Edens and his wife Lynn Edens for $16.995 million in 2015, New York City property records show.
