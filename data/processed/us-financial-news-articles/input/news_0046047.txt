LIMERICK, Pa., Victory Bancorp, Inc. (OTC-PINK: VTYB) (the "Company") today announced that its Board of Directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.02 per outstanding share of common stock. The dividend will be paid on or about February 28, 2018 to stockholders of record as of the close of business on February 5, 2018.
Victory Bancorp, Inc. is the parent company of The Victory Bank, a Pennsylvania state chartered commercial bank, headquartered in Limerick, Pennsylvania. The Victory Bank was established in 2008. Victory provides high quality banking services to small and mid-sized businesses and professionals through its two offices located in Montgomery and Berks Counties, Pennsylvania. As of December 31, 2017, The Victory Bank had total assets of $220.7 million, total deposits of $190.0 million, and total equity of $19.8 million. Additional information about Victory Bancorp is available on its website, www.thevictorybank.com .
