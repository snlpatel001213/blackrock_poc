HONOLULU, Jan. 16, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- Central Pacific Financial Corp. (NYSE: CPF), parent company of Central Pacific Bank, will release its fourth quarter 2017 earnings on January 24, 2018, before the open of the New York Stock Exchange. Management will review the results by conference call and live audio webcast beginning at 1:00 p.m. Eastern Time (8:00 a.m. Hawaii Time) on January 24, 2018.
Interested parties may listen to the conference by calling 1-877-505-7644, or by listening to the webcast on the company's investor relations website at http://ir.centralpacificbank.com . A replay of the call will be available through February 24, 2018 by dialing 1-877-344-7529 (passcode: 10116271) and on the company's website.
About Central Pacific Financial Corp.
Central Pacific Financial Corp. is a Hawaii-based bank holding company with $5.6 billion in assets. Central Pacific Bank, its primary subsidiary, operates 35 branches and 81 ATMs in the state of Hawaii as of September 30, 2017. For additional information, please visit the Company's website at http://www.centralpacificbank.com .
