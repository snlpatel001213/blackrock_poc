January 25, 2018 / 4:16 AM / Updated 2 hours ago At least eight killed, 15 injured in Guatemala bus crash Reuters Staff 1 Min Read 
GUATEMALA CITY (Reuters) - At least eight people were killed and 15 were seriously injured in Guatemala when a minibus came off the edge of a road and plunged down a ravine, an emergency services official said on Wednesday. 
The vehicle was travelling in the San Pedro Necta municipality in a mountainous part of western Guatemala when the accident occurred, a spokesman for fire services said. 
It was not immediately clear what caused the minibus to careen off the road, the spokesman said. Reporting by Sofia Menchu; Editing by Paul Tait
